Freshman - Female - Negro

A soldier went to see a fortune teller to get her advice upon 
marriage.  He asked her whom he should marry.
The fortune teller took one look at him and said for 
him to marry at the first chance.
Then he asked if he should marry a pretty girl 
or a smart one.
She replied, "A pretty girl could do better, and a 
smart girl would know better.

A soldier told another one that he had airplane chicken 
for dinner.  There wasn't anything but the wings and tail.

One soldier - Where were you before you joined the army?
Other soldier - I was in N.Y. for six months.
One soldier - What doing
Other soldier- Six months.

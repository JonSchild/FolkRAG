3/25/1942

Dear Alan:

I certainly stirred up a hornet's
nest by referring Mrs. Seeger to the
Putnam letter I had sent you. I
meant for her to see the figures only
or maybe I just carelessly
forgot what was in the letter
I shall not reply to this disagreeable
letter or to any other that
Mrs. Seeger writes to me at any
time. You may say just that to her.

I would like for you
to keep in mind:
1. That I advanced Mrs. Seeger
$330 for transcribing the tunes. This
amount will be charged against
her royalty.

June 21, 1940

Mr. William Ellis
University of Maine
Orono, Maine

Dear Mr. Ellis:

Father sent on to me your nice letter and I
think your offer and expenses will be alright.
I'd like very much to get acquainted with Maine.
Sometime in May should be a good idea. I'll look
forward to hearing from you.

With very best regards, I am,

Sincerely,

Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk Songs
Library of Congress.

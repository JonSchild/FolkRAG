Mrs. Sullivan - evidently very poor - 
comes from Mt. Holyoke, Mass.
“Lost her voice chasing the pig”
- as the Irishman says.
Lies in her bed, like an old
Irish lioness, a fighter, Catholic,
84, came to Springfield in 1866.

"The Green Linnet" -
John Boyle O'Reilly went to
school with her husband -
[John B] John Sullivan -
Reilly could swim 3 miles
under water way. The 3 men
jumped in, Reilly sunk at 
once & swam toward land. 
to fool them.

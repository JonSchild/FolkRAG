JOHN A. LOMAX
THE HOUSE IN THE WOODS
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
12/21/1942.

Dear Mr. Botkin:

I shall be glad to have
the pressings and the addenda
which came along with them.
I thank you also for
sending me Mrs. Boyd's letter
and I shall try to look
after Doc Reed.

Merry Christmas to you
and to yours
Sincerely yours,
John A. Lomax

Port Aransas, Texas,
1/17/1939.

Dear Alan:

letter I enclose from Mr. Putnam
I am going ahead and submit
a 600 page book, in the hope
of fighting the matter to a successful
result direct with
Mr. Brett and Mr. Latham. You
and Mrs. Seeger can therefore
adapt your plans with this
end in view. I may even
cancel the contract and hunt
another publisher.

DEPARTMENT OF LABOR
OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY
WASHINGTON

February 27, 1939

Mr. Alan Lomax
124 West 12th Street
New York, New York

Dear Mr. Lomax:
Would it be possible for you to sing some
of your songs at Miss Perkins' annual party for the
members of the staff of this Department in advance
of our reception and dance on Friday evening, March
3? I will explain the situation when you come for 
your conference in the Secretary's office.
Very sincerely yours,
Mary LaDame
Special Assistant to the 
Secretary

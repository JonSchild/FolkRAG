MEMO
November 6th, 1942.
[*Contact*]

Mr. Lomax telephoned and left the following information for you:

Mr. Elliott of the National War Agency, #7394, has an excellent
singer of folk songs. He is the head of Government at Harvard and knows
two songs.
1. Road to California
2. He Drove three mules for George McVane
However, he might be reluctant to sing them for you but his wife,
who lives at Cambridge, Mass. might sing them.

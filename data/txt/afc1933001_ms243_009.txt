JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS 9/29/1941.
Dear Alan:
I assume from your last
letter that it was your desire
that
I should fix
One Mister Hix.
Well, presumptuous Hix
Is soundly fixt.
Meanwhile, I am loafing in the
Dallas County jury room preparing
to meet out justice in the
case of X versus Y. X, a 
comely woman, it is alleged,
was found in a Dallas gutter,
removed against her will to
a Rescue House, where she
over was man-handled against

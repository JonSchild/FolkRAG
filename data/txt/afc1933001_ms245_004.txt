JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS

11/3/1942

Dear Alan:

1. I've written to Railroad [?]
times. No reply.

2. When I wrote to you last I was in Austin to meet
Johnny and to see Texas defeat
Rice. Johnny was here two
nights ago en route to visit
his rock quarry in Colorado.

3. As you know, I had no
way of telling when the recording
machine could get
here. I grew tired of putting
all my affairs aside for
this uncertainty and made
engagements for my time which
I am now obliged to [?].

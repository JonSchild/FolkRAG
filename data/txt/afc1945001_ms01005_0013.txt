A friend of mine got back from the south
and he said a little town in Alabama had a
normal population of 10,000. They built
a big army camp there, and the next week
the civilian population increased by five
thousand. All prostitutes.

37
male
C

overheard on bus
August 19, 1942
William S. Pates
Pittsburgh, Pa.

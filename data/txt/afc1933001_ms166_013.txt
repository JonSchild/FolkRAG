January 10, 1941

Miss Amelia C. Andrews
Monte Vista Manor
Black Mountain, N. C. 

Dear Miss Andrews:

I should be delighted to have copies of the songs
you mentioned and it's pleasant to know that you are following
the program.  Under separate cover I'm sending you a copy 
of the American School of the Air Manual.

Very sincerely yours, 

Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge
Archiveof American Folk Song
Library of Congress

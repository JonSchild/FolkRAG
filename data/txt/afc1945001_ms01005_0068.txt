September 23, 1942

Mr. Walter T. Forbes
Editor, The Stillwater Gazette
Stillwater, Minnesota

Dear Mr. Forbes:

For Mr. Davis I wish to acknowledge your
letters of September 18th on the subjects of Professor 
Gustave Meyer and the talk at a private
meeting which was reported in the Twin Cities area.

We very much appreciate your interest in
advising us of these incidents.

Sincerely yours,

R. Keith Kane
Chief, Bureau of Intelligence
Office of War Information

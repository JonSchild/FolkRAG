12
Doctor Foster.
Doctor Foster is a good man,
He teaches children all he can,
Reading, Writing, Arithmetic,
And never forgets to use his
stick.
And when he does he make
them dance,
Out of England in France,  [runs (around)]
Out of France, into Spain,
Over the hills, and home again [runs out]

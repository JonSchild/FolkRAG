Four Marines  were playing bridge
in a hut on Wake Island. Suddenly
another leatherneck burst into the room
and shouted, "The Japs are landing a
force of about 200 men down on the
beach."
The four Marines looked at on(e) another
wearily. Finally on(e) said: "I'll
go, I'm dummy this hand."

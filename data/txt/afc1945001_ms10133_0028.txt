F. N. Female.

I. United States can beat the socks off of
Germany, I ain't worried.

II. I hope Hitler beats, maybe these white folks
will know how it feels to be walked on.

III. I ani't care'in if we win or lose, the
Coloured man's condition ani't goin' to be no better.

IV. Mr. Roosevelt is alright, but those white
folk in Congress haven't any love for the Negro.

V. I'm going to war, because I can't get
out of it, not because I wants to.

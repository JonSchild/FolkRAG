JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
5/10/1940

Dear Jess Morris:

I think the copyright
fee is One Dollar. My young
son, Alan Lomax, will look
after details if you will
write him at the Library
of Congress.

When is the R.I.T.? Maybe
I can come out.
Your friend,
John A. Lomax

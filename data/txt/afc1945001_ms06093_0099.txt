August 8, 1942
In many of the plants being built by the Government for the war program, exorbitant rates of  pay are being paid to construction men in order to get these jobs done. While on the other hand manufacturing jobs of equal importance are having literally stolen from them by the attractive high wages paid by the Government through their contractors."
[*Warren, Ohio  18*]

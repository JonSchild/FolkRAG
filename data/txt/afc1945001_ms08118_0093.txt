Francis Williams 

Joke 

A soldier from up North returned 
to an Arkansas camp from his first 
weekend leave.  
"The slow Southern drawl is right up 
my alley," he reported with delight.  
"You ask a girl to kiss you and before 
she can say "no" it's too late"

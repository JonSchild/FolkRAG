January 14, 1938

Mr. Lincoln Thompson
Sound Specialties Company
31 Beckley Avenue
Stamford, Conn.

Dear Mr. Thompson:

If you can come in Sunday afternoon or
Sunday evening, I shall be glad (and anxious)
to see you then.  I will be in the office until
10 p.m. Phone me when you get to town - home
phone, Lincoln 1650 J, if the Library doesn't
answer.

Sincerely yours,

AL:ed

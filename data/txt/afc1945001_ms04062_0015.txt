Concerning the Military 
1. It is being said around town that a local Naval official was 
recently given an expensive wrist watch. (This was supposed 
to be in connection with someone getting into the Navy and 
was told by a businessman who is interested in civic affairs. 
Phoenix - 8-6-42.) [*2*]

Muchison George

8th HR.
English

English Report on War.

Around my neighborhood, there war a rumor
going around that all wool garments were going to be
ration and that each person would be allowed 65 dollars
worth of wool or clothes made of wool a year. Although
we found out different the next day. For in all
of the paper's it was denied.

I read many cartoons in the American
everyday - Private Breger Abroad by Sargent
Breger formely of the Hearld-American Art
Staff.

Musin
Woman died - only instruction
to daughter "Don't comb my hair."
Didn't have any money. Daughter
weeps. Friend advises dig up body
look in hair - they do. 500
pounds of gold.
 
Men & women up to their waists in the
sea gathering moss.

Ballads sung by [all ter] alternation
3 lines man
man & woman together
3 lines woman
man & woman together etc

Women scatter laughing, holding
bonnets over their mouths at the
conclusion of a scandalous ballad

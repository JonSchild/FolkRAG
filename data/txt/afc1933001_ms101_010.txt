Miss Ethel M. Abeel
6227 Crown Avenue
Oakland 11, California
March 9, 1947
Mr. John A Lomax
c/o Atlantic Monthly Co.
Boston, Mass.
Dear Mr Lomax:
I have read your very interesting article in the Atlantic Monthly in March and I am writing about a little song book which came to me where the stall of an old lady was setted up. If it is of interest to you, you may have it "for free."
The title is:
Gentle Annie
Melodist No 1

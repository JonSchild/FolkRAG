JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
3/15/1942

Dear Alan:

One of my virtues - or weaknesses -
has always been that I
can't quit a fight as long as
any hope is left for winning.

One of my best University
friends is Mark McGee of Ft
Worth, Texas. He is State Priorities Director. If you will
send me your file on the tire
matter, including especially Evans
letter to the Dallas man, I'll go
over to Ft Worth and see if I
can move Mark. I want also
copies of all you have written
on the subject. Don't tell anyone
what you are doing. I've located

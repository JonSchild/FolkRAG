Wakes

Don't be cryin that was, [nothin?] , 
you'll keep him from his rest.

Who is it that's dead?
Well, well, may the journey thrive
with him

Would you know him if you saw
him?

I'd know [him] his skin in a 
tanyard or his shadow on
furze bush.

If you take those cattle to
my country, you'd get £ 4 [a drop]
a head -

Faith & if I could take them
Lakes of Kill to Parg I'd get
a £ a drop.

Is your farm empty now?
No it's stocked with a case [feler?]
& two policemen.

Mrs. [Ola] Oda Smith has a large
collection of [f] sentimental song[s]
texts which she has cut from
newspapers as secured from friends
who have written them down. She is [70] seventy
years old and enjoys her radio.
On it she heard Alan Lomax,
[she] and wrote to him sending the
words of Little Colin Ann. She
lives [20] twenty miles from Dallas. Mrs.
Lomax met her at the depot and
drove her out to our home 
where these records were made.

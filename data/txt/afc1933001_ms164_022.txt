December 13, 1940

Mr. Charles A. Thomson, Chief
Division of Cultural Relations
Department of State
Washington, D. C.

Dear Mr. Thomson:

Thank you for the fifty reprints of my bibliography
of American Folk Songs. They are now in my hands and will go
to the persons who have requested them. I greatly appreciate
your promptness and generosity in complying with this request.

Sincerely yours,

Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk Song
Library of Congress

Lambert is not the head of his band at
all, the skinny, toothless, wretch, but one of the
majors.  The sans bas is the mait band or
at least was acting as such when the
band came to sing. Monday [Tuesday] night.  There
were four vaxines, two majors, one coronel.
Mostly young boys and adolescents did
the singing.
Tuesday night a bigger band came, Seven
vaxines, - 3 base, 3 moyen, one tenor,
and one whistle.  [There were] The chef
band and one of the coronels was a man
about sixty, very quiet but very [domineering]
masterful.  Barefooted, carrying a 
whip.  The president was even older.  There
was another coronel, 4 majors
with batons - two young men, two boys
about 10 or 12 -, a sanbas, various
minor officials, about 50 people following
the band.

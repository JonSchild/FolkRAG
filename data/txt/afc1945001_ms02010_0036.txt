     A rumor-control group can perform a significant service
by making people aware that in spreading hate rumors they are playing
into the hands of the enemy's strategy of "Divide and Conquer";
these people who repeat rumors about any racial, religious, or 
social group in this country--whether they mean it or not, whether
they know it or not--are playing into the enemy's hands.
    Hate rumors present a continuing danger to democracy's
war effort. One necessary answer to hate rumors lies in widespread
community campaigns designed to attack the sources of hate, to 
expose the prejudices which lead to hate rumors, and the blanket
hate rumors with energetic information and educational programs.

The Community Rumor-Control Project
     News is ordinarily thought of as a report of day-to-day
happenings. In the course of such a process, interest often runs
ahead of the facts available, and rumors fill in the places where 
information is incomplete. Rumor study will reveal where the need
for more information lies, and the rumor-control group can perform
a valuable function by undertaking to satisfy the special information
needs of the community.
     The rumor-control group can be of greatest service if it
regards itself primarily as an information agency in the community,
helping the other information agencies, and taking responsibility
for detecting and meeting gaps in information by rumor analysis.

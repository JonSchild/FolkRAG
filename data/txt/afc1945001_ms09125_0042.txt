Wadell Hardy
English III
206-5

(31)

My brother Walter Hardy is in the
U.S.N. he said that it is final.
He also ask me was I going joind
I told him know I am joining
when they called for me.  I ask
him [the] did he regret that he
joined the Naval. No he said I like
it find and all the boys here like 
it the same way that I do.
It will make man out of you, or
killed you one, he said.  Right now
he is up to Great Lake Ill. I go 
up to see him every other Sunday.
And I am also going this Sunday.
My brother will be in on April 1
1943 and will I be happy.

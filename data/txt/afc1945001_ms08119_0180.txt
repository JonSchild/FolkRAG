M.C. LINNER-BROWN
                                     Joke
            A buck private went by a hotel and saluted 
a man decorated with bars and chains.
The man gave him a dirty look. The soldier
told him "why do you give me a dirty look"
He said "I ain't no Cornell or General
Im a door man."

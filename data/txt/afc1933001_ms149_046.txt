CENTRAL STATE TEACHERS COLLEGE
MOUNT PLEASANT, MICHIGAN


September 29, 1939

Mr. Alan Lomax
Library of Congress
Washington, D. C.

My dear Mr. Lomax:

Thanks for the lumberjack records.  I shall make
good use of them.

Did you make a record of Perry Allen's songs?  If 
you did, I would appreciate a record; for Perry Allen
is well know in this community.


Yours truly,
EC Beck
E. C. Beck.

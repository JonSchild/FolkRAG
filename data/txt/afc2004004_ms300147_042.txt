Dec. 29 - 59
Como miss. R.1 Box 129
Dear Mr Lomax I recived your
card and was indeed glad to hear
from you all this leaves me and
family doing very well at the
present hope you all are doing
fine I am sorry of beening so
long writing but I and my Wife
bouth have been sick hope you
all are doing fine so all from

Lonnie Young

My Best Wishes to you

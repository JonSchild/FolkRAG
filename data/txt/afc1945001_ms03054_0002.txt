RUMOR: Norfolk, Virginia
August 22
Negroes are organizing. Serious race riots are expected.
Auxiliary blackout policemen were forbidden to carry guns prior to
last week. They have now been instructed to obtain pistols and be
prepared for race riots which will occur during the next blackout.
[* 5
WA Gill
SSB*]

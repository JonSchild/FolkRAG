JOHN A. LOMAX . ROUTE 4 BOX 341 FOREST HILLS . DALLAS TEXAS

Dear Strunk: Phone bridge or change the enclosed as you like. I am sorry to be so hurried. Later I shall write you further about details of the work.

Sincerely,

John A. Lomax

University Station,
Austin, Texas.
9/8/1934

Everyone knows of the great work Henry Kaiser is doing to help win the war. He is building and launching ships in record breaking time. Mr. Kaiser had an appointment with some newspapermen When Kaiser arrived late for his appointment, he was asked what had happened to him. "I had to wait thirty minutes for a taxi" explained Kaiser.
"Thirty minutes?" A newspaperman asked. "Then why didn't you build one?"

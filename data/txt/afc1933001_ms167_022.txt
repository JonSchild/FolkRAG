February 6, 1941

Miss Joan I. Rayner
134 East 40th Street
New York City

Dear Miss Rayner:

I shall put a ten year restriction on your
record, especially stating that it should not be
used without your permission. Be sure to call by
the next time you're in town. It was a pleasure
to see you both.

Sincerely yours,

Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk Song
Library of Congress

The camp was made in a hollow
where a spring trickled water
from a overhanging crag. I
knocked on the side of the
house and a large, placid
faced woman came to the door.
No other person was around.

I told her that I was
looking for the songs and
poetry of the west that people
carried in their minds - 
people that had no place
to keep books or newspaper.
I found her much interested
and helpful: although
she belonged to the "drifters" I
found that she was a woman
of education. She loved the
life of a cowboy.

I know two gained parodies

December 21, 1938

Mrs. J. K. Beachy, President
The San Antonio Speech Arts Assn.
1858 West Summit
San Antonio, Texas

Dear Mrs. Beachy:

You will be glad to know that
a correspondent of mine in Kansas
City has sent me a copy of the lost
article. I enclose it herewith and
again I regret that the incident
occurred.

Very sincerely yours,


Assistant in Charge of
The Archive of American
Folk-song

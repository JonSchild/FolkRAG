"Rumors"
OAKLAND PUBLIC LIBRARY
Anybody can get a job in the shipyards nowadays.
They won't overwork you, either. The shipyards
want to hire as many men as they can get, because
they get 6% of whatever they pay out. [*7a*]

Oakland. August 12, 1942. (Told on streetcar to a member
of my family.)

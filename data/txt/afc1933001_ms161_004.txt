September 11, 1940

Miss Isabel Scott
Columbia Lecture Bureau
484 Madison Ave.
New York City

Dear Miss Scott:

The enclosed note explains itself. I hope it may mean a date. I would suggest that you not go too steep on the price and dovetail the date with my Minnesota jaunt.

I like the new leaflet much better.

Sincerely,

Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk Song
Library of Congress

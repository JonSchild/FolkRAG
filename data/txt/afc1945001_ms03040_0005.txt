From Margery C. Quigley, Montclair, New Jersey
Heard in Montclair, New Jersey, August 10, 1942
Subject: BANK FAILURES

Conversation between two will-to-do women, approximately
aged 50:
Elsie, have you any considerable amount of cash around
the house?
No, we always keep it in the bank.
Well, you ought to draw it out.

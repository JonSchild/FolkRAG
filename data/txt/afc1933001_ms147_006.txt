July 8, 1939

[*A*]

Mr. R. P. Wetherald
Recording and Record Sales
RCA Manufacturing Co. Inc.
Camden, New Jersey

Dear Mr. Wetherald:

Thank you for your kind letter of 
July 7.  We enjoyed having you with us
and hope you will be able to come soon
again.

I am leaving today for a vacation
for two or three weeks but if there is
anything you with to have done in the
meantime, please do not hesitate to call
on us.  It will be handled either by my
assistant, Mr. Waters, or by Mr. Lomax
himself.

Sincerely yours,

Harold Spivacke
Chief, Division of Music

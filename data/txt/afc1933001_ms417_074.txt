If you can't sing these songs, read through 'em. But you know
doggone well that you can sing. Take this book down to the union
hall and run [throggh] through the pages . with your union brothers and
sisters, and see which songs you like best.

Concerning these second parts we have written down for some
of the songs: 'Course you don't have to sing them, and it sounds
O K if you don't, but if there's some fellow with a slightly
higher voice, sic him on those tenor parts and let him howl 'em
out like a lone wolf. Sounds awful nice.

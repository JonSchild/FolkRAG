

October 10, 1942


Mr . Robert C. Tryon, Chief 
Psychology Division
Office of Strategic Services
Washington, D.C.

Doer Mr. Tryon:

In reply to your letter of October 2nd,
addressed to Dr. Keith Kane, I understand that you
have already received the reports requested.

Attached for your attention is a critique
of one of the reports by the Surveys Division, prepared 
by Dr. Cordon Allport. 

Sincerely yours,


Eugene Katz
Chief, Special Services Division

Davenport, Iowa, August 4.--I overheard Mr. Z telling about the
large number of young men who are getting occupational deferment from
the Draft. He was telling about the large number of young men in
clerical and administrative jobs in the various defense industries in
that particular area who were being deferred, and he was wondering how
they got by their draft boards.   [*48*]

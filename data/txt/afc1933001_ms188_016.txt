November 12, 1942

Mr. W. H. Pipes
144 Hill Street
Ann Arbor, Michigan

Dear Mr. Pipes:

I am glad to learn that you are interested in the Negro
Holiness church study. That seems to me that most profitable thing
that you could do at this time and I shall submit that project as part
of the larger plan.
With appreciation of your kind letter, I am

Sincerely yours,

Alan Lomax
Bureau of Special Operations

Room 2033, Bldg. V
14th and Pa. Ave.
ALOMAX:MM

H.S. Sophomore
Negro-Female

1. We are over here starving while the
soldiers are wasting food.
2. Some people say the war will be 
over in four years.
3. The Germanys consister negroes
worst than dogs.
4. They are going to put all the
girls 16 and over on the farm
this summer.
5. The salesman tells you to buy
clothes while they have them.

San Antonio, August 2
Manpower commission or some group now requires laborers to carry job passports. "What does this mean," I asked.  "It means labor cannot move from one industry to another without a document on the order of a passport." My question: "Is this true of all labor?" "No, only skilled labor and labor employed in war industries. Further power will soon be exercised probably covering everyone, even you, in such jobs as yours." Question: "When do you think this is likely to be exercised?" "Right after the fall election are concluded." 38

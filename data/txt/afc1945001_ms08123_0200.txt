To a certain extent they were right but when a country is as greatly aroused as ours it can readily change its motto. In all branches of American Service the men observe the strictist of military rules. For instance in the Navy the Admiral is almost worshipped. While in the army soldiers get extra duty for not having and using the correct military courtesy. The men are given ridgid tests to determine what service would best suit them

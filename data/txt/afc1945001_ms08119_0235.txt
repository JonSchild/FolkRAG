Eng. 6 Per 3
March 15, 1943
[*A*]

A Japanese fifth columnists reports to his superior
that their is great famine in America. On every ones
lips is the phrase "Whats Cookin'."

Question- What is the difference between a girdle
and a Jap? Answer- They both creep up behind
you and stab you in the back and it takes a
good Yank to bring - them down.

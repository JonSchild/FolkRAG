To whom this may be presented:
Mr. Alan Lomax, a valued member of the staff of the
Division of Music of the Library of Congress, is traveling
in Michigan, Wisconsin, Minnesota and vicinity on official
business of the Library for the purpose of recording folk-music 
for the Archive of American Folk-Song in the Library
of Congress.
Such facilities and courtesies as may be accorded him
will be appreciated by the Administration of the Library.

Acting Librarian

August the second,
Nineteen hundred and thirty eight

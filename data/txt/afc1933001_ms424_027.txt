26.


THE JASMINE FLOWER
(China)

(Page 164 - One Hundred Folk Songs of All Nations)


Sweet and fair a Jasmine Flower!
Sweet and fair a Jasmine Flower!
Bloom'd within my summer bower!
Bloom'd within my summer bower!
Never will I part from thee.
Do thou fortune bring me,
Do thou fortune bring me.

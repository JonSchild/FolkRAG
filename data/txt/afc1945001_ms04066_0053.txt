TUESDAY, AUGUST 4, 1942 -- JACKSONVILLE

Overheard in Cigar Store:    Man "A" offered to bet that Nazi saboteurs
probably would get three or four years imprisonment. Man "B" agreed,
and said that, if so, the eight Generals were the ones who ought to be taken
out and shot.   Jacksonville, Fla.   8-4-42    4

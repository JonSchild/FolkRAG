GOt in the box-car 'mongst lotta other 
niggers & rode all de way. Nowsuh,
didn' sleep, didn' need no sleep, thinkin'
'bout Eva & Shreveport. It was 
3:00 in the afternoon when I got back 
home an' I went & called Eva.

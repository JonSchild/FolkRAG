OLD PAINT

Stanzas contributed by Davidson, the cook, at Swenson's Throckmorton
Ranch.

Old Paint had a colt way down on the Rio Grande,
We roped him and branded him and called him Cheyenne.

Old Paint's a good saddler, he paces when he can
He paces all the time if he's not in the sand.

Transcribed and reviewed by contributors participating in the By The People project at crowd.loc.gov.

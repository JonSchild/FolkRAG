6                Down In The Valley
  [* one girl*]
                    Down in the valley where the
                     green grass grows
                   There little Mary grows like 
                  a rose. 
                    She grows she grows she grows
                    (like a rose) so sweet that
                    dear little Brenda comes to tea
[*2nd girl enters*] 
                    Brenda Brenda will you marry
                    me. 
                    Yes sir Yes sir at half- past-
                     three we'll have sugar cakes ice

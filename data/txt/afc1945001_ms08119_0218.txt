                                          Rumor
               War has made America cautious
She wants to prevent if possible the
hardships and disasters of the last war.
Thus, we have rationing. Now, if we
are to believe in rumor, clothes as
 well as gas, food, tires, shoes, etc,
will be ration. Three days will tell
the tale for March 15th was to be
the dead line, according to rumor.

Senior- Negro - Male

1. Henry Ford: When do you want me to deliver those tanks, Hitler? 
Hitler: Don't bother Henry, I'll pick them up on my way through Detroit. 
2. One German: What's the largest city in the world 
Another German: New York, London, Berlin, Paris, etc. 
First German: You're all wrong. It is Stalingrad. Our troops have been marching in it for weeks and haven't reached the center yet.
3. Narrator: Rommel ought he called the desert cat instead of the desert fox. After all when a fox has been killed or captured so many times, he dies. 
4. One Negro to another Negro soldier: Who is headed to Casablanca: Going home, eh?

Port Aransas, Texas,
2/23/1939.

My dear Mr. Spivocke:

Perhaps your predecessor had a right to complain at me for my failure to write more explicitly about my schedule on the collecting trips. Be that as it may, I should like you to have concrete evidence that I am preparing my engagements ahead of time. See the enclosed letters already received.

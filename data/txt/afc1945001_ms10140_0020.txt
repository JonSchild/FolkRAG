During the last war the germans had a complaint
to make against those d---ned black soldiers.
When the germans would fire at the white soldiers
they (white soldiers) would fall over backwards and remain there,
but when those blacks were sent against them
and were fired upon they would fall on their faces.
When the Germans fired again those black devils
would get up and keep on coming. 
A math student entering the army
had this to say. " I ought to be good on
 the rifle range because I've had
"triggernometry."
There is consternation at a Jap camp - a 
U.S. Marine is walking around five miles
from there.

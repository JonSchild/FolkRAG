Riggs, Susie
March 12, 1942

It has been rumored that before this war is over the United States will be engaged in a war with Russia. The one reason it is said that the Russians have not been told of the help the United States are extending to them. 

The draft is taking men so fast that it is said that they feel your body and if your body is warm you find yourself in a part of the armed forces.

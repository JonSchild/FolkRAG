St James
Mich
Aug 18 1958
Dear Mr Lomax I seen
in newsweek magazine where
you were coming Back after
7 yrs song ballading in Europe
Hope you went to Ireland
I wrote you several times
letters returned if this will
reach you I wld be glad to
hear from you
John W Green

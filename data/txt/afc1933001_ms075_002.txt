LIBRARY OF CONGRESS
WASHINGTON

DIVISION OF MUSIC Oct. 3, 1940

Mr. John A. Lomax
General Delivery
Memphis, Tenn.

Dear Mr. Lomax:

Alan tells me that you have had
a great deal of trouble with the
recorder so we are preparing another
for shipment to you. Please wire
me where to ship it and whether or not
you need any records or needles. If
you have any dull needles, return
them to me and I will sharpen them.

With best regards,

Sincerely yours,
Jerome B. Wiesner
Engineer, Division of Music

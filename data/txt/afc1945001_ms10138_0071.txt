I've heard people say that Hitler
is tricking the Italians.
Some say that Hitler will need
an asbestos suite where he'll
end up at.
A man once said that if Hitler 
was assassinated the War would
practically be over.
A friend of mine's said that
after we win the war Mussolini
will need a Chaplin mustache
to disguise himself from the Italians
for the mess he got them into.
My last statement is that
Hell is too good for Hitler, Mussolini,
and Hirohito.

February 27, 1941


Miss Lois Pollard
Lincoln School, Sec. 6, 4-A
Denver, Colorado

Dear Lois:
Please thank your grandmother for me for
giving you the words to "Nobody's Darling
But Mine" to send to me. I very much
appreciate the trouble you both went  to
on this account. 

Very truly yours,

Alan Lomax 
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk Song

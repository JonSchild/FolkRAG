[*[1942?]*]

Dear Mr. Lomax:
I[']m forwarding your card
to Alan at Nashville, where it should
reach him all right unless his plans
have changed since he last wrote. His expectation
is to return here on or about
the 29th, but I think he may be delayed
somewhat.
Sincerely yours,

Assistant

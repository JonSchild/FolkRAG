Pat heft 
Went into prison in 1918 for 35 years sentence of
which he served 7 yrs. & 8 mos.  "The Gov'nor 
tol' me;  'I'se jus' goin to keep you here to
play for me'.  He waz a woman's man - he
alway had a big crowd of ladies with him.
They tol me:  'the gov'nor goin' to forget you,
he won' set his own mana free'.  But I knowed
he would set me free.

Report on Rumors - 7
ARIZONA
August 15, 1942

Defeatist Attitude.
1.  There is something funny about the Japanese and Russians. 
They have a secret understanding.  It is the policy of these countries 
to avoid major encounters.  (Chinese doctor, intelligent, about 55 
years of age.  Phoenix, 8-11-42.)

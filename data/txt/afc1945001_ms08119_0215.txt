Mar 12                                             (a)
                    A private was doing guard duty for the
first time, and heard approaching footsteps. He
shouted "Halt!" and a sergeant identified
himself. After walking a short way, the
private again shouted, "Halt!" The sergeant
came back  to ask why they private had shouted
twice, and the private replied "my orders are
to shout halt three times and then shoot.

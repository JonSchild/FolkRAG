July 12, 1961

Paul Marshall, Esq.
56 . 45th Street
New York, N.Y.

Dear Paul, On May 12 we mailed to you a group of Alan Lomax contracts,
which you were going to ammend.

We have no word from you and would appreciate learning
the status of these papers as quickly as possible.

Cordially,

Paul A. Rosen

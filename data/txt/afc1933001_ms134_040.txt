MEMORANDUM

FROM THE Division of Music
June 17, 1938

TO THE 
LIBRARIAN OF CONGRESS

REFERRING TO Recommendation for official
leave for Mr. Alan Lomax

I recommend that three days official leave and per diem allowance
be granted Mr. Alan Lomax for his trip to Beaver, Pennsylvania on June
18, 19, 20. The purpose of the trip is described in the attached
memorandum from Mr. Lomax.

Harold Spivacke
Chief, Division of Music

APPROVED
LIBRARIAN

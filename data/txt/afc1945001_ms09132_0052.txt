Junior N.F.

1. There has been a ration on sitting 
in chairs why, asked the little boy
Because they are rationing every
thing even your toe nails. 

2. Why, Uncle Sam is taking all the
boys to the army, I suppose the 
girls are taking the Hermites
way.

3. It was said by a young
woman one day - Well we 
are eating horse and dog meat -
What next - we will be eating
each other.

The flames from the fireplace and one coil oil lamp furnished all the light,
a light that flickered and trembled from the wind creeping through the cracks between the 
logs, perhaps from the music that throbbed through the cabin and seemed to sway the branches
outside. It was a happy evening, and up in Washington Elmo Newcomer's music and calls
live for other generations. [I list the names of some of his tunes.] Other ? of Elmo Newcomer ?

Admiral Byrd found "Home on the Range" cheering during his long, cheerless vigil
at the South Pole. I first printed the phrase "Git along, little dogies," immmortalized and
unacknowledged in teh deservedly popular "Last Round-Up."

But best of all,- a librarian once told me that he had bought forty copies of

2/23
General:-Private Jones go and take this
horse and have him shod.
Private: Yes Sir: I'll go at once.
An hour later the General returns
General: Private Jones, why is the horse
dead?
Private: You told me to go and have 
him shot.
General: Shot? I told you to take him 
to the blacksmith.

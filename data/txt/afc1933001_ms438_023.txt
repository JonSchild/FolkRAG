"Aunty,  did you bring me any silvo?
Aunty, [xx] did you bring me any gold?
What did you rbing me,   dear aunty,
To keep me from de gallis pole?"
"I didn' bring you no silvo,
I didn' bring you no gold,
I jus' come here to see you
Hung upon de gallis pole. "
"Father,  did you bring me any silvo?
Father,  did you bring me any gold? 
What did you bring me,  dear father,
To keep me from the gallis pole?"
"Son,  I brought you some silvo,
Son,  I brought you some gold,
Son, I brought you li'l' ev'ything,
To keep you from de gallis pole."

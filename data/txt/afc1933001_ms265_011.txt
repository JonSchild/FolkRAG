Allan Lomax

Under the direction and supervision of the Chief of the Music Division 
to administer and develop the Archive of American Folk Song, consisting 
of over 3,000 folk tunes (some folk tales, prayers and sermons) recorded 
on disks, involving the classifying and cataloging of these records, and the 
arranging for and handling of gifts, deposits and exchange of material, 
requiring familiarity with folk song music and literature.

Transcribed and reviewed by contributors participating in the By The People project at crowd.loc.gov.

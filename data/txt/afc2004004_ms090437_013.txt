17.5
Sid Hemphill, playing a droning figure 
on his fiddle, so that the sound is like a 
locomotive rhythm, so that the fiddle in effect
becomes a drum, shout out his memories of
a hog hunt, his voice harsh, commanding,
savage, his eyes popping with excitement &
blood-lust--He gives long wailing pig-
squeals

Rumor 

Someone was telling me
that a soldiers mother received 
a letter from him while he
was a prisoner in Japan and
under the stamp he wrote 
telling her that the Japs had
cut out his tongue and burned 
his eye out and that he had
undergone many tortures. 

Betty Orvidas 
Civics  5th Period
Rm. 517

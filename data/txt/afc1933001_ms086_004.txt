Mr. John A. Lomacks. Otey, Texas
9/4/41

Dear Sir

I am Sending to you this type of song to night I hope that they may be suitable for recording and I also appreciate the Visit the you all paid me some time ago & I also do appreciate the donation that were given to me & sir give my regards of respect to your faimly and also mr Allen too & please tell mr Allen to write to me some time & give my regard to Mrs Elnora Livingston too. Give them my address I would like to hear from you Soon sir

Respectifuly yours,

James Baker, No, 57469

Box #1

Otey Texas

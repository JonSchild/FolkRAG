4. Find out when I may get

5. I am glad to hear of your
Columbia plans, and I hope
you can arrange to hold
on to your Washington connections.
I would consider
coming and working in your

6. Always I am concerned about
you and your welfare, but I
no longer get "mad" at any
of my children. Lovingly, Father.

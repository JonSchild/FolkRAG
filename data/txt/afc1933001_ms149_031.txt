Wellner Motors
January 11th,
1 9 4 1
Mr, Alan Lomax
Music Division
Library of Congress
Washington, D.C.
Dear Mr. Lomax:-
Enclosed you will find original copy of Conditional
Sales Contract on New 1939 Ford Std. Coupe which you
purchased from us in June 1939 and which has been
paid in full.
We wish to extend to you at this time our appreciation
for your valued business and trust that whenever you
are again in the market to purchase an automobile, we
may be favored with your business.
Very truly yours,
WELLNER MOTORS, INC.
K. Wellner
KW/p
encl.

via Toronto to Niagara
Falls, Canada. We should 
be there by Sept 2" or 3".
Our further movements 
[depend?] -- though I 
hope we can go on back 
to Texas.  Mr. Wetherold's
letter would have deserved 
no reply except 
for your intercession and 
that of Harold, who wrote 
me at some length.
I've not been feeling 
so well, but am better 
today.  Weather delightful.
Glad you enjoy your 
home. Acquire a regular 
one of you own as 
soon as you can.
Lovingly, Father.

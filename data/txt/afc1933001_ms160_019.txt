August 12, 1940

Mr. James Mitchell
c/o The NewYorker
New York City

Dear Mr. Mitchell:

     I greatly enjoyed your article on Madame Olga
and would like to get the text of the Circus Version
of Root Hog or Die. If you don't have it, could you
furnish me with lead where it may be secured?

Sincerely yours,

Alan Lomax
Assistant in charge
Archive of American Folk Songs
Library of Congress

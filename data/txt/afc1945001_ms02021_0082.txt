COLORADO  AUGUST 29, 1942
-3-

The story most frequently heard these days in Denver is
that the government is drafting men for army service who have wives
and dependent children. The conclusion is - you never can tell
when you will be called regardless of dependency or any other status.
Responsible federal officials tell us there is no basis of
fact for such stories but they admit that they are widely circulated.

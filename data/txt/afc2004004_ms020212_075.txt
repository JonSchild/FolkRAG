121 West Third Street
New York 12, New York
July 19, 1963

Dear Bessie:
Enclosed is a letter and contract from Sid Davis.
He sent it to me because, apparently, it was
sent back to him by the Post Office.  Would you
please sign your name on the line over your signature
and return the contract to Mr. Davis in
Atlanta as soon as possible.
Love,
Alan Lomax

A: Did they have a band?

R: Yes.

A: How many instruments?

R: Four. That other fellow, he used to be in my hand. Second violin,
bass, and guitar. Sometimes he kept a horn-blow. Sometimes I had
ten bands.

A: What were the names of some of the places that you played?

R: Oh, Hillhouse, Moore, some Sherry.

A: What were some of the joints in Hillhouse?

R: Well there were no joints. You see, a fellow would give a big dance
in our house tonight and go to a picnic -- (End of section).

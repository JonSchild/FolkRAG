12
So the keeper lost his breeches
And he got into disgrace,
The policeman got well laughed
at, with all about the place,
And the watchers they go skulking
by and hanging down their heads
They cursed that night they got
Such a fight with the loving
landry maid.

Tusdale Poems & Ballads by
Thomas & John C Raine
1830-1930

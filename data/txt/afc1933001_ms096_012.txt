JOHN A. LOMAX
THE HOUSE IN THE WOODS
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
12/2/1942

Dear Professor Botkin: Please reply to
the enclosed letter.

I have two accounts against the
Folk Song Archive - one four months
old. Will you please ask Miss
Rogers to see if she can divine
what is delaying payment and to
let me know?

With all good wishes
I am 
Sincerely and truly,
John A. Lomax

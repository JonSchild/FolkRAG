You owe it to yourself and to the world 
(there'll be plenty left) to tell your 
story.  From what you have written
to me you will have a good 
income next year.  Why 
not set aside enough to give 
yourself a year of absolute
freedom?  I'll even forego the pleasure 
I take in writing to you so frequently!
I very much wish you to 
contribute a chapter to my book 
devoted to our joint experiences
as ballad hunters.  To be most 
effective you should write with 
absolute frankness and unsparing

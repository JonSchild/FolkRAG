January 17, 1941

Mr. Gordon King
7456 San Benito Way
Dallas, Texas

Dear Mr. Kings:

Would it be possible that you and Mrs. King might
wish to continue to rent the house up to March 1st. If so,
you may consider you have it rented. Kindly let me know on
this point. I enclose, herewith, your light bill.

With kindest regards, I am,
Sincerely,

John A. Lomax
Music Division
Library of Congress

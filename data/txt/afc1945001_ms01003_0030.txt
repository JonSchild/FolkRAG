OEM-33

EXECUTIVE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT                                                  
OFFICE FOR EMERGENCY MANAGEMENT

OFFICE MEMORANDUM

To:               Cornelius Du Bois, Executive Assistant 
Date:  March 30, 1942
From:          Eugene Katz, Special Service Division [*EK*]

Subject:      Rumor Analysis

Here is a statement of the rumor problem, which has
been put before Drs. Stanton, Franzen and Cottrell. I hope
that we will be able to get together this week and reach some
kind of a formula which will satisfy all of us.

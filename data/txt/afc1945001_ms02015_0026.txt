OFFICE OF FACTS AND FIGURES
WASHINGTON
February 11, 1942
MEMORANDUM
To: Cornelius DuBois
From: Charles Tyroler, II
Re: Government Agencies with Field Staffs
SUGGESTED
A survey of these agencies with a view to getting a
picture of
1. The general data they already have on the state
and nature of public opinion.
2. The potential or actual facilities they possess for
acquiring further material.

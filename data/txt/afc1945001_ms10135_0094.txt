College Junior
Negro, Female.

I. The say Uncle Sam is not a
woman but he sure can take
a woman's man.

II. What is the difference between
a woman and a soldier, a 
soldier gets knocked down and
a woman get knocked up.

III. This Army have caused many
heart aches between men, women
and children from every walk of life
such as mothers, fathers, sisters,
wives, children and sweethearts.

IV. Why do the negroes have to
go to the army to fight when they
have nothing to fight for. They have no
priviledges on buses, street cars, trains
cafires or any public places.

Defeatist Attitude

1.  I heard the other day that the German failure in Russian last year was
not because of the effect of cold on the German soldiers, but because the
war machine operated with synthetic fuels did not work efficiently
in the extreme cold.  (Government employ in a War agency, salary
about three thousand, long-time state resident.  Phoenix  8-3-42)

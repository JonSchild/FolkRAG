September 1. 1937

Dear John, 
You are welcome to use the two pieces from 
The American Songbag which you mentioned in your 
note of July 7. You must overlook my delay about 
this.  It has been a summer with heavy demands on 
my time.  My affectionate regards to you and Alan.
Yours, 
Carl

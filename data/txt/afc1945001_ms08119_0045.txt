              Company coming
A patriotic lady sent an invitation to a ^officer at a nearby
Army camp. The invitation  read, Mr. & Mrs.
Brown request the pleasure of Captain Green's
Company to dinner. She received the reply
from the officer stating his company would
all be there for dinner with the exception
of three men on leave and 5 men sick.

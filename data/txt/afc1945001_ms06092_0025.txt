The government sure is putting the
synthetic rubber plants up in a hurry.
You don't need to worry about rationing,
Mr. Pates.....three plants in Pittsburgh
are all set to go.  In another six months
we'll have all the gas and rubber same
as we always had.

35
male
D

attendant at gasolene station
Pittsburgh, Pa.
William S. Pates

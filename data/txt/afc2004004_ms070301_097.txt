3.
Then away he ran with all his speed
to raise the watchers all 
Well knowing that an army was 
ready at his call
He said, I'll nap the miner lad
whoever that he be
I'll send him off to Durham Gaol
to sup skilly galle.

4.
The Policeman lives close at hand -
I've quite forgot his name
He is kept there on purpose to 
protect the squire's game
He says we must be careful &
surround him if we can.

5.
Oh Hark! said the policeman
Yonder's something I do see
It is two men a standing beside
yon old oak tree

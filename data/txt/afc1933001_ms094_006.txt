THE UNIVERSITY OF TEXAS 
AUSTIN 
DEPARTMENT OF CLASSICAL LANGUAGES
MAY 9, 1942
Dear John Avery: 
I am very sorry to 
hear of your being sick, 
but I trust you 
are over the worst. Take
care of yourself. Or 
rather give full heed to
the counsels of the 
Lady Ruby. She has 
a lot of sense
I had a delightful 
dinner the other day

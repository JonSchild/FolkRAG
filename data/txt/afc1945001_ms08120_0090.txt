Plato Bibbs
Schmidt - 331

English

One day Hitler and some of
his Officials were riding along a
country road when they ran over a
dog. Hitler told one of the Officials to go
back and tell the owner of the accident.
When the farmer came to the
door the Officer said "Hiel Hitler! The
Dog is dead."

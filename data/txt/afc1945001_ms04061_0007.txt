An editor of a Colorado Springs daily paper writes about 
the disturbing rumors of an epidemic of jaundice in the army camps 
caused by use of defective serum for yellow fever inoculation.  
The rumor seems to imply a doubt as to the Surgeon General's 
explanation that the vaccine has been modified to eliminate 
further trouble.  [*8-7  Colorado  24*]

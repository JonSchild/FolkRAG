February 10, 194

Mr. John A. Lomax
7456 San Benito Way
Dallas, Texas

Dear Mr. Lomax:
          This will acknowledge receipt of your letter
of February 6th. Dr. Botkin has been called out of 
town on business for a week or ten days. Upon his
return I am sure he shall be glad to send you the
information requested.

                                               Sincerely yours,

                                               Secretary to
                                               B. A Botkin
                                               Assistant in Charge
                                               Archive of American Folk Song

Social Security Board
Old Age and Survivors Insurance
Austin, Texas
8-5-1942

There is much talk locally along the general line of dissatisfaction
with the apparently slow procedure in the trial of the
Nazi saboteurs. Expressions very from statements that they should
have been taken out and hanged immediately to criticisms of the
scarcity of news information covering the testimony at the trial
itself.  [*40*]

Ruby Stavropoulos
March 12, 1943                          [*a*]
I read about a dog which was
left on a train in which a soldier
was traviling. It was a very small
dog. At the end of his ride, the
soldier seeing that no one claimed
it, took it to his camp, & it
became their mascot. It also received
a uniform.

What the Brooklyn Dodgers did to
the Chicago Red soxs. 
No III. 
A Negro lady was seated comfortably
on a gray hound bus on the
front seat, the bus driver walked
up to her and stated "I'm sorry
Lady but you'll have to move." 
She replied calmly, "Mister, you
set down in that seat, keep your 
eyes on the road and Remember 
Pearl Harbor. She didn't move.

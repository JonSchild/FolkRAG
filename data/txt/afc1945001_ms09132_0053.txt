Senior - Negro - Female

I. It was said shortly after the rationing
that a woman neatly wrapped a box
and sent it to a special friend in
the army. Someone else seeing her asked
why send an empty box? Her reply
was, so I can get more coffee. 

II. In regards to the rationing of shoes 
this statement was made;
I can't leave the house! Why
a friend ask?  I don't have a way to 
go. Well what happened to your car?
I put it away because of the rubber
and gas shortage. Then do as everybody 
else, start walking you have two
good feet don't you? Yes, but no shoes.

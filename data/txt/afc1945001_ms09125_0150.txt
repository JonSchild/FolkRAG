When the Japs attacked
Guadacanal the captain
of a company of men told
his men that they were
out numbered four to one
One of the men shot four
Japs and then he put his
gun down.  When the captain
asked him why he put
his gun down the reply was
that they were [ouct] out
numbered four to one and 
he had shot his four.

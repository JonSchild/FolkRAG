December 12, 1940

Dear Sirs:

In connection with a special examination of company records,
there is enclosed a statement of your account as of November
30, 1940.

If the balance does not agree with your records, it is
important that you advise as to any difference by communicating 
directly with the undersigned.

If the balance is correct, please sign as indicated and
return the statement in the enclosed envelope.

Yours very truly,
L. Merrick
Auditor

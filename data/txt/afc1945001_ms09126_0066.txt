5. I heard that graduation was going to be in May because of the many fellows of draft age in the graduating classes. 

6. When I went down to my job I was first fingerprinted, that was O.K. I had my picture taken, that was O.K. They gave me a number, I began not to like this. They had more guards there than they had in a penitentiary. They also took my life history just like any other prisoner but, "This is war and we all have to be careful," they told me.

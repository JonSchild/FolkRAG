Mr. Bond
Superintendent of Buildings
November 19, 1937

Music

I recommend that the following work be done in the Southwest 
Attic:

Rearranging bookcases and stacks.

Removal to the attic of the recording machine 
and the motor generator that are at 
present in the basement of the Music 
Division.

Removal to the attic of the records making up 
the Archive of American Folk-Song that 
are at present in Deck 50 and in the basement 
of the Music Division.

Acting-Chief, 
Division of Music

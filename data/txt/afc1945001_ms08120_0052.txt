This anecdote refers to mothers not knowing
where their children are.

A man walked up to his friend's house who
was working in the back yard. The man
went into the back yard where he saw his
friend, and they started chatting, and soon
the man got off the subject and said, where
is your dog? And the mans friend said
under the house, he said, "where are the chickens?"
The man said, "in their coop." He said, "where
is your cow," the man said, "in the barn"
and so the friend said where is your son
John, and your daughter, Mary? And the
man said, "I don't know.

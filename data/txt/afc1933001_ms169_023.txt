April 11,  1941
Dear Elise:
Thanks for the list of fiddle tunes, the
clippings and the good wishes. I want to
know about your plunge into the deep you
say you are going to take soon, so
that I can strow some rice in after you.
Very best regards from Elizabeth and myself.
Sincerely,

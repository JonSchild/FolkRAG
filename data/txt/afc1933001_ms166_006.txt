The Library of Congress     GENERAL ORDER NO. 1009
Office of the Librarian         January 8 1941

TO THE MEMBERS OF THE LIBRARY STAFF:

All persons who may be spared will be excused on
Inauguration Day, January 20, 1941.

The exhibition halls will be open to visitors. from 9 a.m. to 10 p.m.

The usual Divisions comprising the Sunday and Holiday
force will be open from 2 to 10 p.m.

Archibald MacLeish
THE LIBRARY OF CONGRESS

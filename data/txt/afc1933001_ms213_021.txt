Port Aransas, Texas,
3/22/1939.

Dear Alan:
I mailed this morning to Howard
Cady, Macmillans, my contribution 
for the Dummy for the Ballad book
which must be in print by May.
I wrote today also to Mrs. Seeger,
telling her what I had done and gently
urging her to forward to Mr. Cady
several sample songs, words, music 
and compilers' notes (which you 
may have to supply). I find Mrs. 
Seeger, with all her fine qualities,
a spasmodic correspondent
comparable - and more undependable - 
than some others I could mention.

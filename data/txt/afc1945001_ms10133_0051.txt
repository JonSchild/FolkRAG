F. N. F

I have heard the remark made that the negro will be better off if the Japanese would win this war as if the United Nations would. The reason is that the Japanese would be kinder to them than the U.N. would because we are of the darker races. And the Japanese feel that the darker races should rule the earth. 

The English are not worth having as allies after the way the turned on the French. This refers particularly to the bombing of the French navy.

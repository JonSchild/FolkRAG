My teacher would whack me 'croos the head an' that would make me worse 'cuaes I'd be tunin' up for a cry. I'd jus' tell him straight up an' down, say "Professor, ain' no use you doggin' me,  I just can not learn them funny words. '.... My two friend girls, in that class died an' lef' me all alone. An after that I jus' couldn't learn a thing about latin. I s'pose I jus' had my min' on them, 'cause I sho couldn' get it on that Latin.

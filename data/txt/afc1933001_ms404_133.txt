Blind Willie McTell
He learned to read Braille
in Michigan, soon he "followed
shows".
He was a friend & partner
for several years of Blind Willie
Johnson; saw him last in
Union, Missouri.  According to
Blind Willie McTell, Blind Willie 
Johnson sang [sa??i?] music
best. He had a deep voice, was
a "notable singer and excellent
guitar picker". From a letter
which he received from Johnson's wife [he] McTell thinks Johnson is dead.

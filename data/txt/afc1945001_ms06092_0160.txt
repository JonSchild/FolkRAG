The Spang-Chalfont mill has been closed
down for a couple of weeks....my husband is
taking his vacation because of it.  He
doesn't know why they're shut down, but I
think the government doesn't see any sense
in making their product (pipes) when they
can't get it across the ocean anyhow.

32                                     heard on bus         
female                             Pittsburgh, Pa. 
C                                       Helen J. Pates
                                         August 6, 1942

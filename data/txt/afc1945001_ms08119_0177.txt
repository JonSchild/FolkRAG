  A  M.C. LINNER-BROWN        March 12, 1943

                  Joke
    A bouy named Gus said : "One of my
ancestors fought in the Revolutionary War.
He was in the battle of Brooklyn."
The Bus said: "Yeah? Was he a hero?"
Gus: Was he? Say , the bullets were
flying this way and that , but they never
hit him. He was the first Brooklyn
Dodger!"

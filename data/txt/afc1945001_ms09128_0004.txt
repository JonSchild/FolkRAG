Income Tax Jokes 

1.  One man had so many dependents 
they thought he was the new "Father
of our country. 

2.  Every time you bring in the bacon
you cook another Jap's goose. 

3.  When you pay your income tax 
is called "Declaration of Dependents, 
and if you don't say your tax
they will put you in "De Pen. 

4.  A man went to the hospital
and looked about the room, so a
nurse called from the other side
of the room and said "Come on
over your wife has just presented 
you with another deduction."

Virginia Sanders

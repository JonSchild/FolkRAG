As the new recruit in camp, was reporting to his superior officer he was saying to himself, he was sure he was not going to be taken when they had heard he had defective hearing.
Aloud he said, "Sir, I'm sure there has been some mistake. I'm hard of hearing in one ear and I'm afraid it has been overlooked."
"Ah! that's allright," said the officer, "do you see that soldier over there filling the pail with water? Well go over there and tell him it's full. He is blind.

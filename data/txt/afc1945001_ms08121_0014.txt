As a military homing 
pigeon was flying leisurely 
to its destination with a 
report from the front, it 
was jostled by a second 
pigeon which bawled: 
"Get a move on. I've 
got a denial of the report. 

Barber: "Haven't I shaved 
you before, sir?"
Soldier: "Nope, I got that 
scar at Pearl Harbor.

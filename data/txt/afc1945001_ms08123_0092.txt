Maxine Johnson   8th   March, 5, 1943

U.S. History

A lady went into a store to purchase
ten pounds of butter. She said
to the storekeeper that she wanted 
to stock up before all the hoarders
got to it.

Henry Kaiser was to have an
appointment with two men at
ten o'clock. The men did not 
arrive until ten-thirty. Mr. Kaiser
asked why the men were late and
they explained that they had to wait
thirty minutes for a taxi. "Well,"
said Mr. Kaiser, "If I had had to
wait that long, I'd of built a 
taxi.

Bluefield State Teachers College
Freshman - Negro - female

1.  I heard that the white sailors and marines
in New Guinea and Australia were painting
themselves black to keep the Japanese from shooting
them.

2. That Gen. McArthur was killed at Bataan
and the story of his escape was faked.

3.  That there were plenty of gasoline and oil
and rationing was started just to make the
people on the home front "feel the war"

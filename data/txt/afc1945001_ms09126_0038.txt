Merile Mason
7th Per March 12, 1943



English 

One day an Air Raid Warden
came around to talk to a house
wife and said to her "For
incending bombs you should
have this buckets of sand and
a long handheld phone/" The
housewife said, "But what if 
it happens to be a high-explosive
bomb?" The Warden answered, 
"Then you won't need the sand."

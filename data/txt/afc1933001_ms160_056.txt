August 29, 1940
Mr. William H. Fineshriber, Jr.
Columbia Broadcasting System
485 Madison Ave.
New York City
Dear Bill:
I'm sure you'll be interested in the enclosure from
J.A.L.'s comments on C. B. S. publicity methods in Texas.
I would like to have your opinion about whether the program
is going to sell. Am rather vaguely, on vacation at
the moment.
With sincerest regards, I am,
Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk Songs
Library of Congress

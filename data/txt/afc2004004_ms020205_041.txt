Reverend Roe. Reel 1. P. 7
Well, I had married then, and my wife, she was teaching a little school 
up there, and I worked on the farm. I was a good farmer. I made some 
money there on the farm that year. My wife taken sick and I had to 
send her home, and it was that year that I began preaching.

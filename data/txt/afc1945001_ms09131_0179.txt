College Freshman-Female-White

1. Women slaves in Germany to increase population. 
2. Harmful talk of the inefficiency of our government to cope with the governments of our allies. Reports as to our government's lack of concern rather there lack of means to change conditions, such as strikes of days off because of illness.
3. The possible danger for girls even in a respected community near our army camps. Warnings such as: "Come prepared with artillery men are all alike." 
4. There are always rumors about the time that our certain reserve will be called. Why don't they let the fellows know?

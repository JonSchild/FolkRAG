Bernice Center

Last Saturday, the rumor got started that
clothes rationing would start the folling Monday.
A customer who had been in earlier in the day
come rushing back about 7:30 P.M, breathlessly
exclaiming that she and her husband wanted
to lay in some clothes so as not to be caught
as I was by shoe rationing.

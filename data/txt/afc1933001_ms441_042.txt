I'm All Out an' Down

[Hpoin']
Hopin' pay-day would move off a li'l' further, he looks aroun' 
an' he cry...

It was a man expert with his [wh] line. Ev'y time he hit the mule 
he cut the hair off an' write his 'nitials.

I swear to God I know that man wish that woman wouldn' come 
come on pay-day.

JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
10/10/1941

Dear Alan:

When I denounced to you
as a lie the story Professor
Benson dragged into the [ch] celebration
of the Twenty-fifth year of
Negro freedom, I took small
risks, though it has taken some
time to furnish the evidence
herewith submitted.

I realize sadly that 
my considerable labor wont
do any good whatever - Professor

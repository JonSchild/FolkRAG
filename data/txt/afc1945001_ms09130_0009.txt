12B      Boy      Negro

1.  There will be a clothes ration Sat.
   night, Feb. 27.
2.  They are going to send the negroes
   to Africa after the war.
3.  Hitler is dead.
4.  Clothes will not be ration at all
   It is to much trouble.
5.  The negro should not be in this
   war.  He should be fighting for his
   rights here in the United States.

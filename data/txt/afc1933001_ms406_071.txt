But finally in an attempted bank robbery at Round Rock,Texas, twenty miles north of Austin, the Capitol City, through the treachery of one of his companions, on July 20, 1887, he "met his fate."  In a fight with a company of Texas Rangers Sam was mortally wounded, dying the next day, and refusing to the last to give the names of his assistants.

I'll go anywhere as you
may direct, but I fear
you shouldn't lean too
heavily on W.P.A. They
are snail slow and
as uncertain as the
wind.

As for me, with all
my faults and blunders,
I am for you in any
way you can use
me.

Always sincerely,

John A. Lomax

Music
August 30, 1937

Chief Dlerk
(Attention Mr. Bond)

[*AAFS*]

Please furnish for the use of Mr. Alan Lomax, who is about to
set out on a recording expedition for the Archive of American Folk-
Songs, the following:

A grounding attachment for the converter, consisting of
a spike with 50 feet of wire and a clip for attachment
to the converter.

Friction tape

4 twenty-five feet extension cords with plugs and sockets.

Soldering iron with solder

4 battery terminals

2 battery carrier straps.


Acting-Chief,
Division of Music

Be the time when me old
father [wd go to the field walk]
with 4 others wd do 4 or 5
gals of cider before um wd 
go to work in the morning.
Sleep under hay all night.
Drink a gallon a piece in
the morning & then mow
the grass while the dew
was still un um & it
mowed easy -

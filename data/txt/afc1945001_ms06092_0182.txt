Salt Lake City, Utah

August 3: Why don't we get busy and do something? Why is England so
slow? What is England doing with all of her troops sitting back of the English
Channel? It is all right to build up our production and to send our boys to
all parts of the world to fight, but what about England? Everytime you read
a newspaper article, it is either the Australians, the Irish, or the Canadians
who are doing the fighting. You never hear of an Englishman being on the front
line.  63

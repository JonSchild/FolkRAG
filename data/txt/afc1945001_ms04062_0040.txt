

Quincy, Massachusetts. August 4, 1942

IV.     He then berated a certain army major with 
whom he had allegedly recently discussed employment. 
He expressed the opinion that this unnamed army major 
and others in the same category were incompetents, 
who through influence had been placed in personnel 
positions for which they had no aptitude or ability.

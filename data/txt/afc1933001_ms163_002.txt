November 1, 1940

Mr. Merritt H. Coleman
Columbia Artists, Inc.
485 Madison Ave.
New York City

Dear Mr. Coleman:

    I will be in New York, November 8 to 12th.
and will see you at that time.

    Sincerely yours,
    Alan Lomax
    Assistant in Charge
    Archive of American Folk Song
    Library of Congress

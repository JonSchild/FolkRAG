September 8. 1938
Mr. Alan Lomax
General Delivery
Mackinac City, Michigan
Dear Alan:
The Library has approved the payment 
of $15 to Mr. James W. Green for the 
ballads which you got from him. Please
send me his exact address so that payment 
can be made without any trouble.
Sincerely,
Harold Spivacke
Chief, Division of Music

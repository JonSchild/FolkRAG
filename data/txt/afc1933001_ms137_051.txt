Federal Bureau of Investigation
United States Department of Justice

Post Office Box 2118
Detroit - Michigan
September 29, 1938

Mr. Alan Lomax
Assistant, Division of Music
Library of Congress
c/o General Delivery
Duluth, Minnesota

Dear Sir

Receipt is acknowledged of your
letter dated September 20, 1938, at Escanaba,
Michigan.

You are advised that this office is
presently conducting an investigation with
reference to the recording machine belonging to
the Library of Congress that was stolen out of
your car in Detroit, Michigan, on September 9
or 10, 1938.

Very truly yours

[*John S Bugas*]
John S. Bugas
Special Agent in Charge

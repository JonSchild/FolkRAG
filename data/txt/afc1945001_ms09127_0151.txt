Jokes about the War

"You must pay for the boy," said
the conductor, while the six-year-old
tried to shrink in his seat.
"He's only three," said the mother tartly.
"Goodness, he looks older," exclaimed
the conductor.
The mother threw out her hands
helplessly. "Can I help it if he
worries about the war?"

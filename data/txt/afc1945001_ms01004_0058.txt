July 3, 1942

MEMORANDUM

To: Mr. Eugene Katz
From: Cornelius DuBois

From the material you have gradually acquired do you think
you could produce a report pretty soon on the nature and
prevalence of rumors?

Do you think such a report would have enough meat to be
good survey material?

Howard Smith                 [*Title*]            March 12, 1943

                          Joke
One cold December morning on the Russian front
two German infantry men while repeatadly dodging
Russian shells struck up a conversation about peace.
Says the first German, "Well soon this nightmare vill
be over, der Fuhrer hass asked for peace!" "Ya!"
yells the 2nd German, "Unbelivable!", "thack dehiber." 
"Ya dots right, he vants a piece a England, a
piece of Russia und a piece of America.

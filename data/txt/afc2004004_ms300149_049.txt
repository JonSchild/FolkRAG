Senatobia Miss
October 20, 1961
Post Office

Dear Mr. Alan
How are you all fine I hope we all are fine so
far but grand father is in the hospital and 
as low as he can be I don't know what to tell you
right now but will write if he get better
or worth grand father been sick all the year
now this is all I have to say, that why
we havent wrote.
from your truly
[*Write- Miss] Sidney Lee Hemphill
Grand father is dead he died friday
morning at 5 o clock we will
berrie him sunday at 2 o clock at
New Salm Church
[*$10 personal chk*]
[*?????*]

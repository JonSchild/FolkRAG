money, they taken her money.  And when they came back, you know--they
all came back to where I was stay ng, in fact, I was staying at one

I listened at em and I just walked in as though I'd knew just everything,
you know, and I says, "Well, the woman's up?"  They say, "Oh
yeah, she got up."  I say, "Where's that you all got from um?"  They
kinda look at one another.  I say, "Put it here on the table."

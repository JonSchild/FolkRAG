Crystal Falls, Mich,
R. 1 Bx. 214, Jan. 18, 1939.

Mr. Alan Lomax
Library of Congress
Washington, D. C.

Dear Sir:
   Will you please forward me
the record Mr. Vernon Rautanen
sang, as per agreement while
you were on your tour last
Fall.  The name of the song is 
"Two Kings Children."
   Thanking you, very kindly,
   I am,
           Yours very truly,
              Mrs. Vernon Rautanen

[*CBS Misc.*]

February 26, 1941

Miss Elinor F. Inman
Columbia Broadcasting System
New York City

Dear Miss Inman:

I enclose my check for $3.50 in payment of the 
fine work you did for me on January 29 and 30, 
1941.  It was much appreciated.

Very truly yours,

Alan Lomax

MOIN MALADE? MOIN ALLER AU TI DOCTEUR.
(I am ill, I went to the doctor)     men chorus
Sung by Alpha Ferguson, with Anse-la-Raye, St. Lucia, July 18, 1962

Moin malade, moin aller au ti docteur
Docteur dits moin l'estomac moin manquer
Moin/moute et moin dit doudou moin ca /Deriver
"Doudou si ou content moin faire bon manger ban moins"

Moin descendre en ville, moins acheter macaroni
Moin acheter macaroni moin acheter l'onion
Moin acheter beurre et moins devine mounte
Et moin dit doudou moins-ca, et moin dit 
"Dou dou si ou aimer moins, faire bons manger bans moins"

"I am ill, I want to see the doctor
The doctor told me you have a weak stomach
I returned home and told my sweetheart this
"Doudou (sweetheart) if you love me cook some good food for me.

I went to Castries, I bought macaroni, 
I bought macaroni I bought onion, 
I bought butter and returned home, 
And I told my sweetheart: 
"Doudou if you love me cook some good food for me."

(This is a wake song . . . composed by Alpha Ferguson sung at the funeral wake, followed the nine-night wake."

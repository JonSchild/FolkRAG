                              Rumors
      Rumors are being spreaded conserning
rashing of food and clothing and other essential
war needs. Our first rashion book was for sugar
this was accepted by the public as a whole. But
some people started predicting what was to
be rationed next, and how it would effect the
cevilian population rashing is for one thing helping
us to save and make what we get and have last
longer.

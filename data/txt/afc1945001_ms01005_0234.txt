Mr. R. Keith Kane, Chief, Bureau of Intelligence         October 19, 1942

Eugene Katz, Special Services Division

Newspaper correspondent's inquiries


Nobody in this Division, so far as I have been able to
find out, has talked to Mr. Frank I. Weller, World Wide, regarding 
rumors. This is in response to your memorandum of October
12th, regarding a recent story in the newspapers.

June 30, 1942

To Whom It Concern:

Mr. John A. Lomax is Honorary Curator of the Archive of 
American Folk Song in the Library of Congress.  He is about to
make a trip for the Library of Congress to Texas, Oklahoma and
New Mexico to record cowboy songs and other important types of
indigenous music.  The records he makes will be deposited in the
Archive of American Folk Song in the Library of Congress for the 
use and benefit of the American people.
Such courtesies and facilities as may be afforded him will
be appreciated by the authorities of the Library.

Acting-Librarian of Congress.

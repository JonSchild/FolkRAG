Ethel Oakley
M.C. LINNER-BROWN
Joke
I  There was a illustration in the "American Legion
Magazine" of a young lady in a grocery store. After
she finished buying her groceries she looked up at 
the clerk and said, "haven't you got a pound of coffee
for little old me"?

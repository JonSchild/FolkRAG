Before leaving Dallas I wrote
to the Sacred Harp Singers
Headquarters in Atlanta, Georgia,
asking for the address of the 
leading singer of East Texas.
Back came a letter referring 
me to R.J. Bohannon at Lufkin, Tex.
Through him I learned of the
District Singing at Corrigan
on September 29. On that Sunday
we drove to Corrigan from Jasper
via a dirt road and ferry.

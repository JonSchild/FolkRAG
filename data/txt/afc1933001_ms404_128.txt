We hitched our machine on to a 
light which flickered on the
Commissary gallery while
our microphone stood in
the semi-darkness. We found
steady foundation for the 
machine on the gallery itself. The
records we made that night-
mainly sprituals-were the
first ever recorded in the 
Mississippi Delta (down in the 
heart of it) in these identical
circumstances. I believe this
to be the richest unexplored 
field for folk song and folk
lore in these United States.

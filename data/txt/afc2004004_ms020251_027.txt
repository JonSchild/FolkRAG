Reel of 10/4/61, Side 1, Song 10

ONE FOOT, TWO FOOT

This is a game.  You say the same verse over and over. . .You have
to lay down to play it, and pull  them big dresses away up.

One foot, two foot, three foot, Sal,
Bob, Demilius, Angus, Mal,
Mary, Terry,
Money foot.

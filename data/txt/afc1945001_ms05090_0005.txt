My father told me years before he died
that this country would be beat by the Japs.
and Mrs. Pates. . .I think he was right.  He
knowed a lot of things and told me a lot has
come true.  Another lady I work for is
scared to death the Japs are gonna come
right here to Pittsburgh.
35
female, negress
D
laundress in my home
Pittsburgh, Pa.
Helen J. Pates
August 6, 1942

Macon. Tenn.
                                               Nov 4. 1959.

Dear Sir
     If you have made
anything on what you
have I would like to
have as much as three
or four copies I would
like to buy if I can.
If you can get down
here anytime soon
I will have three or
four more for you -
             Yours truly,
               Augusto Crawford
               RI Williston, Tenn.

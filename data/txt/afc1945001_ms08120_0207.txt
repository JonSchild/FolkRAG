A soldier said to a Chinese soldier.
"Say Bud, what's your name?"
Chinese "My name's Scat."
Soldier "Is that your native or American 
name."
Chinese "That's my American name."
Solider "Hell what's your native name?"
Chinese At-choo-'s my native name.

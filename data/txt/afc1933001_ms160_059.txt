August 30, 1940

Mr. Frederick A. Stokes
443-449 Fourth Ave.
New York City

Dear Mr. Stokes:

     I greatly appreciate the copy of the delightful
"American Mother Goose" that you sent me. It's a 
read addition to my library and I know I shall enjoy
reading it.

With sincerest regards, I am,

Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk Songs
Library of Congress

7456 SAN BENITO WAY

Dear Alan:  1. Your thought
about Mr. MacLeish was a
happy one. Yesterday I sent
him a spread of blandishments
that ought to prove a
successful lure, provided
you are right in your
prognostications. I imagine
he will notify you of his
decision.

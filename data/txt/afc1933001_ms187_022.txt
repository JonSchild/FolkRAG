October 9, 1942

Mr. S. T. Burns
Professor of School Music
Department of Music
Indiana University
Bloomington, Indiana

Dear Mr. Burns:

I am being transferred to the Office
of War Information to tackle a hell of an
assignment next week, so I guess all bets
are off.

Thanks for the offer and for the
reminder.

Best regards,

Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge of the
Archive of American Folk-Song

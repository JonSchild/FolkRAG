August 3, Helena
1. There certainly is need for stabilization or freezing of wages,
especially at Fort Harrison. Young boys highs school age ad recent high school graduates are getting from $9.00 up to $15.00 and $17.00 a day. Twenty men quit the East Helena smelter in one day to work at the Fort, thus causing a great inconvenience at the smelter. (Laborer)  Helena Montana  8-3  12  E3226

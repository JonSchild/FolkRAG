Memorandum
March 17, 1943 
From:  C. C. Haag 
Regional Analyst
Raleigh, N.C. 

To:  Dr Eugene Horowitz 
Special Services, O.W 2
Wash. D.C. 

Subject:  Negro student reports 

The material was collected at the only colored H.S. in
town in one of its largest classes for 3 days this
week.  The type of material requested was clearly
explained and anonymity requested.

Transcribed and reviewed by contributors participating in the By The People project at crowd.loc.gov.

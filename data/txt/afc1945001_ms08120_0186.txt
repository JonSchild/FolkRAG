Bernice Windham
March 12, 1943
Rumors
One Sunday in church a man told us that they had rationed stockings that saturday night, and a lot of the ladies went to a dry goods store that was open all day Sunday and bought 293 pairs. On monday they found out that stockings were not rationed.

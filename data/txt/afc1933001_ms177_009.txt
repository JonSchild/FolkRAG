December 6, 1941

Miss Mary Jo Wallace
Contract Division
Columbia Lecture Bureau
745 Fifth Avenue
New York City

Dear Miss Wallace:

I enclose check for $25.00 covering
the fee for my last engagement. I will
forward the $20.00 for the publicity account
early in January.

Sincerely yours,

Alan Lomax

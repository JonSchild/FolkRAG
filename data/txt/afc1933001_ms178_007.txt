January 8th, 1942

Mr. Alan Lomax
Archive of American Folk Song
The Library of Congress
Washington, D.C.

Dear Mr. Lomax:

If you have finished using the 16mm print of
TALL TALES, will you please return it to us as soon as possible,
or if Mr. Brand still has it, please phone him to return it.

Have you completed writing the introduction and
other data we have been discussing?

Sincerely yours,

BRANDON FILMS, Inc.

T. J. Brandon

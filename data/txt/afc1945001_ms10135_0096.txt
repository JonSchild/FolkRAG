College Freshman
Negro-Female

The drastic results of the war will probably be: that the Negro in U.S. will be horses and the white man will be the plow. This is simply for the purpose of mere existence.

When a lady went to a restaurant to eat a steak or chops, she carefully weighed each thing that she ate, in order that she could subtract this amount from her weekly quota.

After the great crisis it is rumored that if Hitler is not dead or dying, he is to be tried by the nation, for all crimes that man can possibly commit. It is thought by many that he will ask for mercy as he goes to the bar. All he will get is: mercy, mercy, Gestapo what a lover of life and happiness you were.

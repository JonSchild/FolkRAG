MARRIED MAN GOING TO KEEP YOUR SECRET.*

Married man going to keep your secret,
Hi-li-li-lee-o.
Married man going to keep your secret,
Hi-li-li-lee-o.
Chorus:
Hi-li-li-lee-o,
Hi-li-li-lee-o,
Hi-li-li-lee-o,
Hi-li-li-lee-o.

Single boy going to talk about you.
Hi-li-li-lee-o.
Single boy going to talk about you.
Hi-li-li-lee-o.

*Jumping dance from the old Cat Island,
Bahamas. Sung by Elizabeth Austin, and a
group of local Negro Bahaman women.
Summer, 1935. 420-A-2.

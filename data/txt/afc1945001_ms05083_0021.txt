San Francisco
August 6, 1942

[underlined] Subject: [/underlined] Camp Expansion in Arizona

[circled] 74 [/circled]

There are now 58,000 Negro soldiers at Fort Huachuca, Arizona, and there is going to be an expansion this year to 300,000 Negro troops.

Anecdotes.

Barber:  "Haven't I shaved you before,
Soldier?"
Soldier:  "Nope, I got that scar at Pearl
Harbor."

When Henry Kaiser arrived in Washington,
he was late for an appointment with the
newspaperman.
"What happened?" Asked a newspaper
reporter.
"I had to wait thirty minutes for a
taxi," answered Kaiser.
"Thirty minutes?" a newspaperman
asked, "Then why didn't you build
one?"

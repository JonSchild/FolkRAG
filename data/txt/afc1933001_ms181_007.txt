EDITORIAL OFFICES

April 2, 1942

Dear Alan:

The recorded programs of American living history
which you wrote about might well work into a general
story on Radio that Joe Kastner is doing at this time.
I gave him the material.

The folk music story is still in the cards. I hope
to complete it soon. 

Sincerely,
[signed] Roger Butterfield

ROGER BUTTERFIELD

Laurium, Mich.
Nov 12 1938

Dear Allan,

Thought I would
drop a line or two [let] to let
you know we are fine & hope
you the same. It is 20 min
after six & I am waiting for
my boy friend to come
I am going to the dance with
him I just came from a
friends of ours their baby
just died this morning 12 o'clock
& they brought it
home 6 o'clock to nite

I wish you could
come for New Years.
Im waiting for next
month because my birth
day is on the 11th,

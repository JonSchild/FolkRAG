A
Joke

Gus: One of my ancestors fought
in the Revolutionary War. He
was in the battle of Brooklyn.
Bill: Yeah? Was he a hero?
Gus: Was he? Say, the bullets
were flying this way and that
but they never hit him. He
was the first Brooklyn Dodger.

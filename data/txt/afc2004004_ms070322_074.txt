and then drew back into the
Atlantic, carrying men women children,
pigs, chickens, cows &
horses out to sea to feed
some giant old appetite.
Anyhow we agreed to take
one of the cottages & to return the
next day. Drove on and slept 
in the Porthminster Hotel in St.
Ives. The night porter, who was 
very tottery, confided that he hated
oldfashioned things - St Ives was
oldfashioned & he liked new modern
things. The oldfashioned bed was
very fine.

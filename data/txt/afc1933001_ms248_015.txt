JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
2/8/1942

Dear Alan: Answering your letter
of March 2:

1. How did you come out
with your singing at the
March 2 meeting?

2. I await with interest
the results of your conference
with Leon Henderson.

3. If you can promote this
trip by auto, I'll promise definitely
to fill up the Archive
in the matter of Country Songs,
dance calls, hollers, etc. I've
got a lot of them staked out.

4. And I"ll get you some

February 26, 1942

Mr. Frank Luther
Decca Records, Inc.
50 West 57 Street
New York, N. Y.

Dear Frank,
     In reference to your request to include
songs from American Ballads and Folk Songs, in your
forthcoming book, I wish to let you know that my 
father, John A. Lomax, 7456 San Benito Way, Dallas,
Texas has all permission rights in regard to our
books.

With all good wishes,
   Cordially yours,
   Alan 

mdr 

cc: John A Lomax

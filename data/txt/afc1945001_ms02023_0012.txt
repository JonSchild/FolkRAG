FLORIDA

SATURDAY. AUG. 15. 1942 -- JACKSONVILLE

Executive in Operations Department of Bell Telephone Company:
Say in his travels he runs into an occasional person who feels that this nation never will be able to redeem Victory Bonds, and after the war they may be discounted 50% or some similar step taken to reduce the war debt at the expense of bond buyers.

SUMMARY

Last week several observant and trusted friends who travel this State widely and constantly were quoted to the effect that rumors are exceptionally infrequent. A checkup this week from the same sources, together with additional investigation, brings renewed statement in similar vein.
Note has been made that you wish to be covered constantly on rumors and their origin, and this instruction will be observed carefully and material of this nature submitted voluntarily, as new tales are heard or old ones reappear.

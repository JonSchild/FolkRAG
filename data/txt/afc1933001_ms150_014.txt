October 13, 1939

Peter Stammer
61 4th Avenue
New York City

Dear Sir:

Please ship me at once the two books which I asked you about
early in September: Big Money by Dos Bassos, Harcourt Brass, 1936,
($1.25); English and Scottish Popular Ballards, Cambridge Edition,
($2.50). Money Order enclosed covering charges. I would appreciate
dispatch on this order.

Sincerely yours,

Alan Lomax 

Enc. 1.

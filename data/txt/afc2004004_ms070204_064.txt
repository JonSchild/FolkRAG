Capt. Asa Trueblood
Charles, Mich
Sept. 10
Father killed in C. War.
Uncle lost in Sherman's 
march to the sea & nothing
no trace of him were found.
Gd. Father went to the war 
when he was 70 & cdn
march - hon. discharge -
changed name went back
drove 4 mules amb. wagon
- until he hear his sons 
were dead, then came 
home again - cd

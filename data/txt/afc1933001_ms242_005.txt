Dallas
Sunday, 8/31/1941.

Dear Alan: An  electric fan tore
to pieces the thumb nail of my right 
hand. You will find my writing
more illegible than usual.

1. Chris has been drafted and
will be stationed at Corpus 
Christi. Shirley has sold her
home and will rent a house
in Lubbock. She will be
happier I think, as will Pat
and Susan, to stay where most
of their friends live, rather
than to come here where she
would be most welcome.

2. Now about Bess: She will
be in Washington in a week or so.

I notice, too, that in your damned letter you asked us to send in to the Library of Congress all the vulgar songs these men knew.  What in the hell do you want with dirty songs.  And did you think think we would violate postal laws to send you indecent stuff through the mails?"  I don't believe Professor Kittridge who has advised me to save all types of folk stuff could have appeased that angry and outraged warden.  He was still [p??ting] to the door.

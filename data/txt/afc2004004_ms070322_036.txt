One of the nicest things
you English fellas ever sent 
across the ocean to us is this
Cornish ballad about - well,
just listen -

[Some time you ought to go out]
Now let's [ha] have a 
little square dance music -
Here's one from out Oklahoma
way - where the long legged cowboys
swing their gals & sing #

[After this was over I felt
fouled, tired, hollow, jaundiced,
weary of life, hating myself.
Robin, wishing that I live a
hardworking, quiet, dignified life
of belief. Presbyterian...]

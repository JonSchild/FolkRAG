Dec. 9, 1940

Hawdy Alan,

I listen every Tuesday
to your program of Folk
Music. I really enjoy
your singing.

I'd appreciate it very
much if you'd send me
a personally autographed
picture of yourself,
much obliged.

Veronica Mileskay
435 Susg. Street
Forest City, Penns.

Blind Lemon

Notes taken during rendition of the song:

He lef' his piano an' two li'l' chillun at home, a li'l' boy an' a li'l' girl..

She done forgot what she had done tol' the preacher an' what she done tol' de Lawd....

Conclusion:
Ol' lady commence a-cryin'
"Lawd, have mercy on me." (repeated three times for stanza)

She tol' de li'l' girl to play de las' piece on de piano..
de li'l' girl jump down an' played de las' piece..

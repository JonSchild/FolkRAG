To you there in your armchair
Back in the U.S.A.

I'm not enjoyin' this here war;
I want to cut it short;
And I can end it doggone quick
If I get your support.

So keep on buying bonds and stamps
And stay nights on the job
Then I can promise 'twon't take long
To lick the Axis mob.

Frances April
English III
March 12, 1943
Period 1

(32)

1. A man had been drafted
and was down at his local
board taking an examination.
The purpose of the test was to 
see how fast he could think. 
The man ask him if he was
out on the battle field and the
Japs attacked what would he do?
He said "he would run."

2.  A buck private wrote home
to his wife and children and
told them that he had gained
thirty-six pounds.  Six pounds 
of flesh and thirty pounds of
equipment.

The head of a social agency reports being told by a friend of a pilot just returned from Coral Sea that when they were about to engage the Japs, he called to his bombardier: "Are you ready to handle it?" The answer: "I'll do the best I can but I never have shot a gun before." Inference: The U.S. is putting people on guns without any training. [* Michigan- (Aug 3-8) 9*]

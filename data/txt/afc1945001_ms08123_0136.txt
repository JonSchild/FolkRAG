"Joke"
Once a lady went to a store in the
country, to buy some meat.  [When] The butcher
asked her what did she want.  she gave 
him her order, she ordered a roast, chops,
and hamburger.  When he had filled the 
order she ask him to deliver it for her
he asked her with what was he going to
deliver with.  She told him there was
a horse and buggie outside he told 
her there [was] is a buggie outside you
have bought the horse.

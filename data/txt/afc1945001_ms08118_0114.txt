Perry Hammonds

                                                    Joke
G
Gus: One of my ancestors fought in the
Revolutionary War.  He was in the 
Battle of Brooklyn.
Bus: Yeah?  Was he a hero?
Gus: Was he?  Say, the bullets 
were flying this way and that,
but they never hit him.  He was
the first Brooklyn Dodger.

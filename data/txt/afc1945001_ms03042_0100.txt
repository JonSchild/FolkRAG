IV. Heard in New Rochelle, New York on August 13, 1942

In the Jacobs Ship Yards at City Island in the Bronx,
there are many Nazis working, even though the company has
defense contracts. A few weeks ago they had a big fire there
and they think it was deliberate sabotage. [*266 C*]

1520 THIRTY-THIRD STREET
WASHINGTON, D.C.

Dear Alan:

Thank you so much for the book.
Some day, when the world gets good again,
I will use it with great pleasure. In the 
meantime, I am glad to have it.

Most sincerely,
Ada

March 6, 1942

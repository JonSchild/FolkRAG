If Hitler isn't dead, something
else has happened. He never kept still
this long voluntarily.

When Hitler said he'd cause no mothers
to weep, he wasn't talking about Italians,
Hungarians, and Rumanians.

The blessing of poverty is to enter
a grocery store and see all the nice
things the rich people can't buy either.

A stamp a day will pay the way 
to "victory".

The Germans knew what they were
doing when they asked for a mere
armistice, that means a rest between
fights.

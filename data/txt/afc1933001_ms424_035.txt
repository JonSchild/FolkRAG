34.

CHERRY BLOOM
(Japan)

(Page 446 - Folk Songs of Many Peoples, Vol.II.)

Cherry bloom; Cherry bloom;
Is it cloud within the sky
Frail white mist-wreaths floating by?

May for fragrance faints in air
Wand'ring, waiting everywhere.
Come with me! Come with me!
Far has fled winter's gloom.

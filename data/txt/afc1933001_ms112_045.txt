Special Personnel Memorandum No. 35
March 24, 1943

The Library of Congress
Office of the Director of Personnel

TO ALL RATING AND REVIEWING OFFICERS:

there will be a meeting of all rating and reviewing officers 
in the Coolindge Auditorium, Friday March 26, at 9:00 A. M.  It is 
important that every person who has responsibility for preparing efficiency 
ratings be there,  Bring your efficiency rating manuals and 
other materials that have been distributed for your information.

Byron F. Lindsey
Chairman Efficiency Rating Committee

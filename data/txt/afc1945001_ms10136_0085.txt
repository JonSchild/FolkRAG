1.  Ole colored lady was listening to the radio and grumbling
to herself.  Her "madam" heard her and asked her what
was her trouble.  She said-
"I'll be glad when they catch this Pearl Harbor!
Goin' 'round here murdering and killin'!"
"Pearl Harbor!" asked her madam, "Do you know
who Pearl Harbor is?"
"Shore.  Some murdering, thieving woman running
around loose."
"No, Sadie, Pearl Harbor's an island.

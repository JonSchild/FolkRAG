                                                        August 8, 1942                      [*54*]

                     RUMOR REPORT
In the past many rumors of reported submarine sinkings outside Portland Harbor 
have been heard all about the city but no rumors of any description heard 
during August 3 - 8th inclusive.

                                                                           [*Keith C Coombs*]
                                                                           Keith C. Coombs, Manager

Ricee, Catherine

81

1. Man to lady "Is she progressive or conservative?
Lady I don't know. She wears last year's
hat, drives this years car, and lives
on next years income.

2. Eight enlisted men were assigned
to move the post library at Camp
Davis, North Carolina.
The men carried out the assignment,
but left one book.
Is title was: You can't take it
with you.

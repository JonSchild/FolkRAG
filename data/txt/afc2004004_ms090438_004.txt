205 E, Alexander St
Greenville, Miss.

Mr. Alan Lomax,

Dear Sir;
in reply to your letter of recent date, i wish to state that i am sending
you twelve (12) copies of different songs. from which you can select the
songs that meets your approval, please excuse the delay in writing due to
the fact i was out of the city and upon my arrival found your letter awaiting
me. thanks for the enterest you are manifesting in my work, if you dont find
the songs that meets your approval among these twelve let me know by return
mail and i will send you another pact of songs.
signed
 (Signature)
 Charles Haffer Jr.

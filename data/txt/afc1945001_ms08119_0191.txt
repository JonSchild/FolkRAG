Tessie Greswald
March 12, 1942                                             (a)
                      Rationing Rumor
   At the nearing of each weekend my friend
(who works in a clothing store) tells of how
all the people came to stack up on clothes
for fear they will rationed suprisedly on
a Sunday (like the shoes) and that they
won't be able have enough new clothes
on hand.

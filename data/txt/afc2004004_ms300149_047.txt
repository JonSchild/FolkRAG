Galax, Virginia
October 17, 1961
Dear Mr. Lomax,
I received your letter
and the check & I would
like to take this opportunity
to thank you for both.
If you are ever down 
here I have lots more tunes
I will be glad to play for
you.
Yours truly, 
Charlie Higgins

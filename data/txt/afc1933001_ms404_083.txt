Uncle Billy Macree
#3974
#33
 I could see at once that uncle Billy 
was impressed by a visitor introduced
by the President of the First National
Bank. My friend went away and 
Uncle Billy and I talked long
after the sun had fallen behind the
big trees that shaded his place from
the evening sun.

"I was a  singer and dancer
and [?] when I was a boy.
That was long ago, in slavery
times. I was ten years old when 
freedom came (that would make him 
85 years old) But says, No I
can't remember more"

Later he spent two
evenings in my hotel room
and sang in his thin, piping

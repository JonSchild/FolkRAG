San Bernadino
Jan 30- 1940

K.M.X.
Mr Lomax
Kind Sir
I listen to you every day
and sure injoy the
school of the Air

I am sendy you the
name of a friedn that
drove the 20 mule Borax
team he is 85 yea old
and know several Old
songs and sings Beautifuly
his name is Ed Styles
E- 9th St San Bernardino

Mrs. E.M, Loughan 988- H St
I heard mothe sing the songs
you sing California

He told us that [he] a man had
started to write the story of his life
but had never completed it. He then
confided he'd invented a machine
that generated 250 volts of current
without gasoline, but had destroyed 
the only model [for fear] because
"they" were trying to steal it. Expressed 
his intention of making a new model
now that he had "learned how to write
the government"

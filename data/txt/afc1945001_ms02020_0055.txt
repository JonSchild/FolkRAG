Rm. 873, 1355 Market Street,
San Francisco, California.
October 13, 1942.

Mr. William H. Webber,
Office of War Information,
1400 Pennsylvania Avenue, N.W.,
Washington, D. C.

Dear Mr. Webber:

Enclosed is Rumor Report requested
in your letter of October 1, 1942.

California takes Columbus Day
very seriously and this necessitated waiting until
today to gather in the last rumors.

Very truly yours,

J. Crawford Brooks,
Administrative Assistant.

GEORGE PALMER PUTNAM

August 2, 1938

Dear Mr. Spivacke:
Thank you for your letter of
July 29th. I look forward to  the receipt
of the discs. After I have tried them out,
you will hear from me further.
Please understand how genuinely
I appreciate your courteous cooperation.
Sincerely,
G. P. Putnam

Harold Spivacke, Esq.,
Library of Congress,
Washington, D.C.

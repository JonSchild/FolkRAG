OFFICE OF WAR INFORMATION

WASHINGTON
October 13, 1942
1110 Raleigh Building
Raleigh, N. C.

Mr. William H. Webber
Bureau of Public Inquiries
Office of War Information
1400 Pennsylvania Avenue, N. W.
Washington, D. C.

Dear M. Webber:

In the survey of rumors there were only two reported to me and
they are enclosed and on the cards that the individual used in reporting
the rumor to me.

Each individual that was requested to assist in this survey were
willing to do so. Each one stated that it was not too burdensome and that
they would be willing to cooperate anytime in the future that they were
requested to do so.

Beauty Operator: Mrs. Lawrence, manager of the Vogue Beauty Salon
Security National Bank Building
Raleigh, N. C.

Drug Store: Mr. Coxe, owner of Coxe Drug Store
Corner of Hillsoboro and Salisbury Streets
Raleigh, N. C.

Civic Club member: Mr. Jack Biggs, President Junior Chamber of Commerce
Raleigh, Building
Raleigh, N. C.

Patrolman Captain Kelly, Police Department
Raleigh, N. C.

Very truly yours,

Gilbert L. Shermer
Field Representative

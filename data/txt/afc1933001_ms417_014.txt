THAT OLD FEELING

"About the time I made up this song there was a popular 
song about called "That Old Feeling." It went, 'When I saw you 
last night I got that old feeling.' And I got to thinking 
about the kind of old feeling us workers got - that old hungry feeling." 

- Sara Ogan

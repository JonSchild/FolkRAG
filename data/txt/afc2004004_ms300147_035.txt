Dec 24 1959
Como Miss R 1 Box 185

Mr Lowmax and Miss Shelley Callin
Just to say we recieve your letters
We was indeed glad to here from
you all. Ho you-all made it home all
rite and how you-all aprearate
what we did. So I am Sign and Return
Back to You-all hope it satfide you
both.

This is all From
Miles Pratcher and
Robert Pratcher

O Yes Ida Mae tows Say

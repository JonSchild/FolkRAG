My plans are yet uncertain.
I might come back and join
with you to shake down the
Carnegies or the Rockefellers.
At least just now I don't feel
like getting ready for a year
in Europe. I don't like the
prospect of being buried at sea. I'm
tired down to the inner recesses of the
marrow of my bones.
Alan may have to translate 
this letter which is for him as

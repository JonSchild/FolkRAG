[*Personal*]

June 30 1942
Accokeek, Md. 

Dear Allan,

Bobby Straus and I are
to be married at six P.M.
Saturday, July eleventh,
at Mike Straus' Linnean
Hill, Porter Street and
Klingle Road, N.W.

We both hope you
can be with us for the
festivities.

Sincerely,

Lenore Thomas

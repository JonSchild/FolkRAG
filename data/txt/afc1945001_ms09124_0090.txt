Harper, Marcia
Per.  9   3/12/43

   In regard to rumors, I
 had heard that the "Merchant 
Marines had refused to unload 
supplies at Guadalcanal and
 also called the "fighting
boys" nothing
 but "big saps."  I must say that
 since then radio commentators have
 denied such happenings.

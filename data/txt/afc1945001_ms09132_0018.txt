Soph. N. M.

1. They ought to send all these
black bastards back to Africa, since
we're in war already. (said by a white man)

2. They rationed sugar to stop
bootleggers of whiskey.

3. Germans will backtrack a
thousand miles to accomplish
their goal or even gain two miles,
so don't be surprised when
they are [?] a little.

[*Quote*] 4. If the nigger ain't got no place
in America let the white man
fight and get his own dam self
killed." (Said by a negro)

5. I'm going out to work on my
Uncle's ranch soon.

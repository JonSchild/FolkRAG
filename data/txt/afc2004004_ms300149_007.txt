Feb 20-1961
915 Wolcott ave
Norfolk VA. 

Dear Mr. Lomax
I was glad to here from you and it 
have been a long time you have 
us where we can nod do any thing if 
you Can please try and get them out, 
or sent us the tape, and we will pay 
you for them, because some of the 
men is goin with another grope, if 
you can't records by the 27th please 
sent them back to us. Because our 
grope will not be together after then. 
They are goin with another grope. 
                                      Clarence McLeon

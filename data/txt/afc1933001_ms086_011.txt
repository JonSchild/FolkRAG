LEGAL DEPARTMENT

September 25, 1941

Mr. John A. Lomax
7456 Benito Way
Dallas, Texas

Dear Mr. Lomax:

    We acknowledge receipt of your
letter dated September 19th in connection
with four of the musical compositions in the
Burl Ives record album.

    We shall, of course, give this
matter our immediate attention.

    In order that we may make a
complete check, kindly be good enough to
send us piano copies for the compositions
on which you claim copyright, together with
complete copyright information on each of
the compositions.

    Very truly yours,
    COLUMBIA RECORDING CORPORATION

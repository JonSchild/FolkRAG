cramped quarters on board ship to much more comfortable quarters in a 
hotel while the American soldiers were left on board to swelter in the 
heat. The American boys were deeply resentful of this treatment and 
when they were finally allowed to land there were numerous fights 
between them and the English soldiers. (Woman prominent in club work

If you get mixed up now,
it's 3 quid a week -

Highest ever I knowed was
5 shillings a week. A farmer's 
son. He got a factory maid.

He moved away to Wales & came back
& married her & got on damn first
afterwards.  Tell you what it 
was, her parents interfered you
see.

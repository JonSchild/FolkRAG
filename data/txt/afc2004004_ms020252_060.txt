2nd R. of 10/12/61, Song 3, p. 3

Look a Yonder
Ready to trael, Lawd,
With her shoes, Lawd,
Can't she travel, Lawd,
With her shoes, Lawd.

Look-a yonder,  (2)
See my brother come
Walkin' down,
Lawd, he got on his travelin'
Shoes. . . .

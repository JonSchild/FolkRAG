Raleigh, North Carolina    August 6, 1942

A statement was made by a young lady that she had heard that the people of the
East Coast around Wilmington, North Carolina were expecting something serious to
happen since there were several troops coming through with supplies, tanks, guns,
etc. This statement was made by a young lady who is an office workers and who is
of a very high character.  14

BEREA COLLEGE
Berea, Kentucky

Office of the President
WILLIAM J. HUTCHINS

August 28, 1937

Mr. Alan Lomax,
Assistant in Charge,
Archive of American Folk-Song,
Library of Congress,
Washington, D. C.

My dear Mr. Lomax:
In response to your letter of August 25th,
may I tell you that I shall be happy to meet you when
you come to Berea, and shall be glad to be of any possible
service to you.

Sincerely yours,
[*Wm. J. Hutchins*]

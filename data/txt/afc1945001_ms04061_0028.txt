Jacksonville Barber: Received a letter from a relative living in 
Alabama, who had heard that Camp Blanding is about to be abandoned because of 
the prevalence of typhoid. The rumor was corrected by the barber who advised 
his relative that on the contrary Camp Blanding is being enlarged.

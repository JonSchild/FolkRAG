[*A. 
American
Council of
Learned Societies*]

February 20, 1939

Mr. Mortimer Graves
907 Fifteenth Street, N. W.
Washington, D. C.

Dear Mr. Graves,

   Thank you for sending us the application
blank.  As you know, we intend to apply on behalf
of Mr. Alan Lomax.  We have sent the form
to him in New York and as soon as we receive it
properly filled out, we shall send it on to you.

Very sincerely yours,

Harold Spivacke
Chief, Division of Music

Reel of 10/12/61, Song 10

ONE SAW, TWO SAW

One saw, two saw,
Zigazog, zah,
Bobtail, Doninicka,
Deedle daw, dah,
Halem, scalem, 
Virgin Mary,
Ikedem, I liked 'im,
Buck.

One red, two red,
Dickery, said,
Hallibow, crackabow,
Tin and lead,
Pee, poe,
Must be done,
Twinkle, twinkle,
Twenty-one.

Engine driver
Number nine,
I'll stick your head
In turpentine,
Turpentine will make you shine,
Engine driver
Number nine.

Zepol
In honor of all the loi.
56A2

Ref: La famille 'sembler
La famille 'sembler non, agoe,
Guine m' 'a inder ou-la.

Solo: M' 'a inder dificile houngan-moin,
M' 'a inder la-a

Refrain etc..

The cakes and fruit are being broken up and the kola and
liquer are being opened for the loi. They are thought to be
actually present enjoying the feast that has been prepared for
them.

Dogfish cutting net to [?]
Makerel dyeing in the net & it can't be

The young people is no use - I am 
not good a man as my father was
& my son is growing up worse than
I am. 
Drinking whiskey at 6 A M before
bfast - girl brings to father's bed along 
with pipe.
Conury bads from fair, drank, kicking
& fighting in the canoes -
woman Odean me -
Fog settles just enough to trap lonely
weather on the cliffs -
Girls & boys go out to rocky headland
to dance & play games - men in grass
playing cards -

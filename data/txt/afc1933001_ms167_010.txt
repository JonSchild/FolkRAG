The words and tunes to the British ballads may be found in a
great number of American collections, among them:
"English Folk Songs of the Souther Appalachians", by
Cecil Starke, Oxford University Press
"Traditional Ballads of Virginia" by Davis, Howard
University Press
"British Ballads from Maine" by Barry, Eckstrom and
Smythe, Yale University Press
"The Ozarks" by Vance Randolph, Vanguard Press

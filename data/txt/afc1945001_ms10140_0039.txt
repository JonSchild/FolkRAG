College

1. Two Colored men were standing on the corner and one said to other:
A. "These here womenfolks makes me sick. Theys always startin'
somethin'!
B. Ain't it de truth! But what's de doin' now?"
A. Ain't you done heard 'bout dat Pearl Harbor? She got all
dese menfolks 'fightin' war bout her! Ain't it a shame?

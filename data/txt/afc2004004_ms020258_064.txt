645 Johnson Lane
St. Simon Is.
June 19, 1966

Dear Jonie & Allen
Just a few lines to tell you some News.
I am Going to be on the Phelida Festival
how about that I also have some good
news about London I received a letter
from Hedy today I am having some
pictures made of me. And I am sorry
that I wasent able to take the trip but
I would see you In [August] July.
John, Bessie, Emma are all well.
Give Love to all.
Love, Mable.

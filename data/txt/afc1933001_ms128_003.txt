Division of Music
December 30, 1937

Fiftieth Anniversary Meeting
of the American Folksong Society
at Yale University, Dec. 27 and
28, 1937.

I recommend that Mr. John Lomax and Mr. Alan Lomax be
sent, as representatives of the Archives of American Folk-Song,
to the Fiftieth Anniversary Meeting of The American Folklore
Society at Yale University, December 27 and 28, 1937, travelling
expenses and per diem allowance to be charged to the
appropriation for the Increase of the Library.

Acting-Chief,
Division of Music

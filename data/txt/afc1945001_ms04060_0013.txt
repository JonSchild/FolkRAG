7.  A number of people [have] said [to me lately] that England has demonstrated 
a lack of capability in administration of an empire.  This is 
proven by the fact that she does not enjoy the same loyalty from her 
colonial troops as the United States has received from native 
Philippine troops.  (Phoenix 8-4-42)  [*2*]

Dallas
7456 San Benito Way
11/27/1939.

Dear Alan:

Perhaps this is my last
letter to you. No one knows
except that awful Judge, way
up somewhere above, or that
sinister looking female who
sits and cuts the strands
of life.

In a recent letter to
me you spoke of "getting out
of the red". This confidence
on your part seems to
invite my comment. At
any rate I happen to be

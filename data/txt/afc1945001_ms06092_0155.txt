Seattle, Washington
August 7, 1942

   An employee of Boeings says the new Renton plant will be used to make the
new Army Stratosphere bombers instead of the Navy Patrol boats.  Even though
the plant in Kansas has been tooled for this Army ship they will be produced
in both the Renton and Kansas plants.

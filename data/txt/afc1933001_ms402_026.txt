"I once knowed a gal in the year o' forty-nine
A handsome young thin, by the name o' Caroline; 
I never could persuade her fer ter leave me be,
And she went and she took and she married me.

"I once knowed a gal in the year o' fifty-two
A handsome young thing by the name of Emmy Lou;
I never could persuade her fer ter leave me be,
And she went and she took and she married me.

"I once knowed a gal in the year o' fifty-three
A handsome young thing by the name o' Emmy ee;
I never could persuade her fer ter leave me be
And she went out and she took and she married me."

CAFE TEXAN
HUNTSVILLE'S
NEWEST
HUNTSVILLE, TEXAS
May 16, 1939

Mr. John A. Lomax
Merryville, La.

Dear Sir:

I had the express man to pick
up the package you left here
with me and I am sending
you the receipt that he gave
me for the package. The
express man said you had
C.O.D. on the package and
he said that you meant
for the to collect $500. before
they deliver the package.

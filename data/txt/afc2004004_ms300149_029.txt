Dear Dale:

Enclosed is a check in the amount of
$13.39, which is payment to you for
accompanying Charlie Higgins with your
guitar on two numbers. These two 
songs are coming out on a record which
will be part of a new series published
by Prestige recording company. The
records will be out by Christmas.

All my best regards to you.

Your friend,

Alan Lomax

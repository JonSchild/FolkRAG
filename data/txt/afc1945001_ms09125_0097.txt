Gladys Taylor

It was rumored that Hitler was dead;
and also that we are to get out of
school, May 20th.
"It is permissible to classify all sisters
and brothers, aunts and uncles who live
more than 3 gallons away as distant
relatives."  said the Italian optimist: "I
think we are going to lose this war."
Replied the Italian pessimist, "Yes, but when."
"I want to get a little on hand
before those hoarders get busy." said
the woman who ordered ten pounds
of butter.
Rhymn
A Slip of a lip may sink a ship.

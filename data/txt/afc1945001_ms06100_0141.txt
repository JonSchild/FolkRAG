SHARPSVILLE - Monday, August 3, 1942.

"Fixing rents as of April 1941 isn't fair to the 
landlord. He had years and years when he lost money and it's unfair 
to ask him to reduce a rent that has been agreed upon as fair 
with the tenant for the last six months or year."       [*70*]

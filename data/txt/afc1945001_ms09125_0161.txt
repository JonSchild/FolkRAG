Jokes on the War

1. Close your big mouth [or?] you slap a jap
2.  lest do our part in winning this war bring
your Cooper Brass so we can sink a jap ship.
3. Our boy's are doing their part let do our 
part back home.
4 Save all of your tin can & grease to help
win the war.
5 Buy more war bonds & stamp to help buy a 
jeep.
6 We can win this war by doing our part
& we will win.

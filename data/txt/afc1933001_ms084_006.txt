Mobile, Alabama
July the eighth

My dear Mr Lomax

During the last year
which Mrs Tartt was working with
me she spoke of you many, many
times and told me of the work
you had done together. Consequently
I am taking the liberty of writing
to tell you of the deplorable
plight Mrs Tartt is in. We have
had to close her project, along with
many others, on account of quota
reduction of W.P.A, which leaves
her with no work and not a 
cent to call her own. She is

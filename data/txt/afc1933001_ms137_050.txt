September 29, 1938

Alan Lomax
General Delivery
Calumet, Michigan

Sent total advance money last Saturday to Ironwood as per instructions/
why did you not get it  am sending salary check to Calumet will
instruct Ironwood Postmaster to forward advance check to Calumet
be sure you stay there to receive it  am waiting for instructions
about records

Edward N. Waters

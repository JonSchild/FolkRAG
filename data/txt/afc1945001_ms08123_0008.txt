A nave flyer was forced down on one of 
the small islands in the South Seas while
returning to his base. While he was on the 
island he put his pearashoot on the beach 
so the patrol flyers mite see it and drop
supplys. He sheard his supplys with the
chief of the island. When the flyer was 
picked by a patrol boat, he left his parishoot
with the chief. For a time afterwards the 
chief put it on the beach and got supplys
from the patrol plane, untill they got
wise to the chief.

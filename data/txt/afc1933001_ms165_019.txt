1319 Murdock Road
Pittsburgh, Pa.
Glen Comstock

Dear Jo,
I have just borrowed
a pen from the bartender,
and am now going to write 
you a letter, but before
that, I want to say to
you how thankful I was
for you illegally to play
me those records, and will
ask you to do the same
thing next time I got to
Washington.
As you might suspect,
I'm at that Irish saloon in
New Haven, which I gave
you that address of, you

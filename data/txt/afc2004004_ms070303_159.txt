Burgers was very anxious that
I not put on the air the time
he had recorded for me earlier.
They were John McLellan times
& Ross, the great white skip
of piping, wouldn't like that.
John learned from all three 
of the grt sources of piping-
John McDonnel of Inverness,
_ of Aberdeen & Willi Ross.
These three men hate each other
like poison and would never play
each others tunes. In fact the
Aberdeen man has been waiting for
years for W Ross to die so he
can spit on his grave...

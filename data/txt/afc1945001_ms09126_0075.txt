Blind man: "What good am I in the
army? I can't see."
Officer: "That's all right. The army can
use you."
Man with no legs: "What good am I in 
the army?"
Officer: "Well, you see we have a blind
man over there who can't see, you
can't walk, but you can direct him
where to sit those pails."

It is rummored that the clothes ration
books are already stored in a warehouse
in Chicago.

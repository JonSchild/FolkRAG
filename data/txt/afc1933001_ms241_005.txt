July 12, 1941

Dear Alan : I have waited 
two days before replying to your 
letter of information about the Archive.
The outline you give is quite sufficient
so the delayed batch
including the "geological check 
list" will not be needed.
My modest little essay is 
all written out and Miss
Terrill is now typing it for me #
I'll be leaving before a 
machine could get to me.
I don't know if Peter Hurd 
is at home when I [come] get

Newark, N. J., August 7, 1942

"Although there is an abundant supply of fuel oil and gasoline in this
country, the Standard Oil Co. refuses to use available tank cars for
transporting it, in an effort to force the Government to build a pipe
line which will be turned over to them after the emergency no longer
exists." [*70*]

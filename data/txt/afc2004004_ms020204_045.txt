I just went off in trance or something. A:A terrible
thing, etc...V: Lawd, have mercy. I hates to think about it
now. She sho did tell me. And I left there. They carried
me from there-- they carried me home-- they wanted to carry me 
to the hospital--but I didn't want to go to the hospital--
I wanted to go home, I wanted to go home-- I was there for just
a half a day and they put me back on forty-fo that evenin....

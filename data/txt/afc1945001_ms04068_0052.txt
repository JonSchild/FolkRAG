Seattle Washington
August 1, 1942

I heard the President of a large war industries plant tell of the lack 
of interest by some of his workers, particularly single men who go fishing, 
get drunk and just stay off the job. He said 22 men were absent.

August 7,1942
I have since heard this same man quoted several times and the figure runs 
from 22 men to 25 percent.  [*(22)*]

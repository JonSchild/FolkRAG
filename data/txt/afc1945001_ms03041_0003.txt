"Oh, I though you were with the Army too. There are so many new customers, I don't know any more. If the Army men don't bring their wives, then they marry while they are here. I have one customer who has been engaged three times this summer already to soldiers and her real boy friend is a civilian.
"I don't know why the girls want to marry these men when they know they are goin' to be shipped out right away -- but I know some of 'em that's doin' it so the Army will pay em so much a month "cause they are a soldier's wife -- and then they get the insurance, too."
(Roswell, August 14)

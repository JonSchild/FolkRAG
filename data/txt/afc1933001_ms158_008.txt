June 4, 1940

Mr. O'Connell
R. C. A. Victor
Camden, N. J.

Dear Mr O'Connell:

Carlton Smith has asked me to order for him a group of
selected records of American Folk Songs issued by Victor.
He is leaving Friday on a plane and will appreciate your
sending him collect the records on the inclosed list.
some of them may be out of print.

Very sincerely yours,

March 10, 1942

Mr. William L. Langer
Coordinator of Information
Washington, D. C.

Dear Mr. Langer:

Many thanks for sending me the material from
Mr. Gregory V. Drumm on the prevention of the
spread of rumors. I appreciate having this a
lot.

Sincerely yours,

Cornelius Dubois
Executive Assistant
Bureau of Intelligence

Merryville, La. 5/8/39
Dear Mr. Spivacke: I am assuming
that you are sending the records to
Merryville, La. My next address is
Pine Bluff, Ark.; next Sunday and Monday,
Parchman, Miss; next Livingston, 
Ala. c/o Mrs. Tartt. Fifty-four
records sent to you from Huntsville.
The day of shipment being Sunday
the Express office was not open. A
friend misunderstood my directions
and on Monday shipped them
C.O.D. $500. The order should read
charges collect. All goes well.
Miss T. and Jno. Lomax.

At the prison gates the guards bluntly refused entrance to my companion and chauffeur in the car one Leadbelly.  I left him gloomy and uneasy because so many "Laws" were close by, angry probably at another instance of race discrimination, more miserable from an aching tooth he had [refused?] to have treated.  I remember when I came back the car was spattered with blood x Leadbelly had been trying to pull his molar with the plyers.

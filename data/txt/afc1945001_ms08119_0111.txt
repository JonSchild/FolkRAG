Rhyme

They've taken our tires
right off of our car
They've taken our shoes
So we can't walk far

But when we imagine
The boys over there
Then think of our homes
free from sorrow or care

We say, take our tires,
our shoes, & our gas
So the sorrows of war
before long, shall pass.

By Betty Joe Hart

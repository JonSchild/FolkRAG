October 9, 1942

The Seven Book Hunters
Station O, Box 22
New York, N. Y.

Gentlemen:

I am interested in purchasing the
following books:

Scott, Sir Walter: Minstrelsy of 
the Scottish Border. London, T. 
Tegg, 1839.

Sharp, Evelyn:  Here we go round.
2d ed.  N. Y., Morrow & co., 1928.

I shall appreciate an early reply.

Sincerely yours,

Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge of the
Archive of American Folk-Song

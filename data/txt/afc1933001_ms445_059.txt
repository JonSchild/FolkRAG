RELIGIOUS SONGS
a)Negro

1. Prayer - This Evening, Our Father. A.A.F.S.
#233A1. Negro, Raleigh, N.C. 1936.

2. Adam in the Garden Pinnin' Leaves. A.A.F.S. #105B1.
Alberta Bradford, 72, and Becky Elsy, 86.
Lafayette, Louisiana, June, 1934. Text partly from
"Befo' De War Spirituals," E.A. McIlhenny, p. 37.

3. Samson. Sylvester Johnson, DeRidder, Louisiana,
1939. Part of text from Jesse Allison and Dock Reed,
Livingston, Alabama, Summer 1939.

4. Job. A.A.F.S. #1321. Veral Hall and Dock Reed,
Livingston, Alabama. 1937.

5. The Man of Cavalry. See: (Sermon in Alpha index)
Sin Killer Griffin.

6. John Done Saw That Number. A.A.F.S. 1335A1. Vera
Hall, Livingston, Alabama. 1937. See: Jazz Hot and
Hybrid.

7. John Was a-Writin'. A.A.F.S. 257A3. Sung by a group
of Negro men in State Penitentiary, Milledgeville, Ga.
December, 1934.

8. Dem Bones. A.A.F.S. #177. Sung by group of Negroes
at Darrington Farm, Texas.

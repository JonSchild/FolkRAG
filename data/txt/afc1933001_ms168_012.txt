March 12, 1941

Mr. Alan Lomax
Library of Congress
Washington, D.C.

My dear Mr. Lomax:

In accordance with our conversation, we
will deduct $20. from your fee March 5th with the
Smith College, Northampton, Massachusetts to be
applied toward your Publicity Account. We shall
also deduct $20. from each subsequent engagement
arranged for you to be applied to your Publicity
Account until it is cleared up.

For your information we list an itemized
account of your publicity bill.

Advertising in "Program" magazine Oct.   $10.93
Advertising in "Program" magazine Nov.    10.93
Circulars                                                            83.38
Advertising in "Program" magazine Jan.      10.93
                                                                       $116.17

We have representatives working on engagements
for you on your return from Texas between
May 7th and May 15th, and when you are able to let
us know a period of time you will be available for
summer school engagements, we shall get to work on
these.

Best regards.

Sincerely yours,
Mary Jo Wallace

Got a Gal In Town Did Her Mouf Chuck Full o' Gold

"A gal did her mouf chuck full o' gol'," is not only desirable as "portable property", but because she must have something to make a man crazy, if he'll get down and sweat to cover her teeth with plates of gold: Veblen's, "Conspicious consumption."

Got a gal in the country. ain't got no good way to go, (3) 
An' I sin' goin' back there to be her man no mo'.

Got a gal in town, got a mouf chuck full of gold, (3) 
Got a gal in town did a fat-moof *playing her board.

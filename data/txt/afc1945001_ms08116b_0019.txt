College - Junior - Negro - male

1. We are going to invade Berlin and
walk down the streets and kill
every German soldier we meet.

2. We are going to Burma and unblock
the road so those Chinese, can receive
Uncle Sam's load.

3. Don' worry Americans because those
japs don't eat anything but rice and one
of our bullets will send him to paradise.

4. Tojo believes in harey Karey I hear but
he trembles in his boots when Uncle Sam
comes near.

5. Those little brown Japs are about
5'-2" give foot two but they are going
to be six feet deep when the American
Army gets through.

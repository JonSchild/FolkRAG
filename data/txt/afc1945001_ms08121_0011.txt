War Stories
A fellow was in court for speeding around 
corners and the judge was balling him out
after the judge was done, he asked the man if 
he had anything to say, the fellow replied, why judge, I thought I was saveing rubber 
going around corners on two wheels.

One soldier said to another soldier do you 
know how to make your paints last, 
the second soldier said no how can you make 
your paints last, the first soldier replied
make the coat first.

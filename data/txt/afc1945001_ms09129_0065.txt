Two privates were discussing the army and the different ranks.
Bob - Which would you rather be, Bill -- a colonel or a private?
Bill - Well, a colonel, of course.
Bob - I sure wouldn't -- I'd rather be a private with a chicken on my knee than a colonel with an eagle on my shoulder.

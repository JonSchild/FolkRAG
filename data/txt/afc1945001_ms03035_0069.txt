Michigan
Detroit
Reported by five policemen patrolling
Detroit beats
Patrolman E

Rumor: 1.  
A woman stated that if people were smart that they would draw their
money out of the bank and hide it because the Government was going to
tax all bank accounts to the amount of 5% on each account.  This
information was heard by the beauty operator of the establishment at
29 Seward.  The woman who made this statement is an office worker.
This neighborhood is located in the apartment house section, which
houses mostly working women.

Rumor: 2.
This man stated that the colored men were getting all the good jobs
and that the Government was hiring mostly colored people for jobs in
the Postoffice, and mail delivery, and all branches of the Government.
He also stated that the colored people were in the same class as the
Japs, and that they belonged to the same race.  This section is nearly
 all American people who are buying homes or building homes.  This man
seemed to be an American.  He was dressed in a painters outfit.

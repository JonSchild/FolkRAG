Dorothy Aldrick
March 15, 1943
Rhymes

We can help win this war by doing our part. The parents can help by buying war Bonds. And stamps. The Childrens can help by saving tin cans, brass, rubbers, papers, and other junk. 

There are many other ways how we can win this war. Uncle Sam is only asking us to do a little, which we are able to do. We must, we will, we can, and we will win this war

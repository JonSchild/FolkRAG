The Golden Gate Quartet and Joshua White with his guitar
will play and sing for you now four Negro songs

CAINCHA LINE 'EM - a Negro railroad work song
JOHN HENRY - the ballad of the great Negro still driven
men, hard traveling, a blues about hard times. And
NOAH - a Negro spiritual ballad.
First CAINCHA LINE 'EM, a song used for straightening track 
on the railroad.

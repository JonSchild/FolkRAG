Port Aransas, Texas
3/2/1939
Dear Alan:
Miss A. Froelich, at some number 
on Second Street Brooklyn, sent through
me [your] the proofs to [the] your review of 
Dr. Hudson's book. No changes whatever
except that I put in one word to 
tighten the English. I thought she 
might be in a hurry, since a 
long delay had already elapsed.
Also I did not know certainly
how to reach you. Some of 
your Columbia associates
can furnish her exact address,
so you can order 
your reprints.  Your letter to

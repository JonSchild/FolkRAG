Boston, August 6, 1942
"Boys in Army did not get as many furloughs as Navy boys and that reason was 'they wouldn't come back'; 'that many were going over the hill'; 'that this month they only received $10 pay as deductions made for wives and that some hadn't lived with their wives for years!"

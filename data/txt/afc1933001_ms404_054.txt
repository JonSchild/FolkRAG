Washington, Nov. 28, 1940

Before starting September 14 on our 6500 mile
trip from Austin to Washington, at
Alan Lomaxes suggestion suggestion
Bess Brown Lomax and I made some records
in Dallas and vicinity.
——— " ——— 
On September 6 Bess Lomax
recorded [4] four hymns sung in our home
in Dallas by Aubrey Burns. His father
is a retired Methodist Circuit rider
and the young man plans
to follow his father's calling.

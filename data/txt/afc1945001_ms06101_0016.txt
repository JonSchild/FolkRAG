Willis Hall, Detroit Board of Commerce executive, received
August 4 a letter from Iowa asking if it was true that new automobiles
had been stacked on top of one another in open fields around Detroit. The
writer said such a report was current in his area and that it was hurting
Iowa war bond sales. [*Michigan 9 (Aug. 3-8)*]

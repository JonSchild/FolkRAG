Rudolph Stringfellow 
[*M. C. LINNER-BROWN*]

Joke 

There were two corpls.  One said to the 
the orther "Jack there isn't thing in 
the world I wouldn't do for you."  The 
orther said "Jim there isn't any thing 
in the world I wouldnt do for you"  
And they both said we go through 
life doing nothing for each other

March 18, 1941

Miss Helen Kwalwasser
860 Livingston Avenue
Syracuse, N.Y.

Dear Miss Kwalwasser:

For a picture of John A. Lomax I suggest you inquire
of Mrs. Ruby Terrill Lomax, whose address is San Benito
Way, Dallas, Texas.

Very truly yours,

Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk Song

William Brock
March 12, 1943

Someone said in one ship yard they
are launching ships so fast, that one day
they launched a sub, it went out and sunk
2 destroyers and an aircraft carrier then
came back to the dock and put a crew
on it.

Moe: Why does Hitler change socks
3 times a day.
Joe: I don't know! Why!
Moe: Because he smells defeat.
1st Jerk. Why can't the people in Italy
play cards anymore?
2n. "  Because the king is in the hole
and the duce is wild.

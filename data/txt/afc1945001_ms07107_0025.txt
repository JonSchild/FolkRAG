From Many Recurrent Sources Widely Spread: A story that two white ladies went to the negro section to employ maids. They saw two negro women sitting on the front porch and asked directions to find negro girls who wanted employment. The colored woman replied: "We don't know, Mam, we are looking for maids ourselves". [*Jacksonville, Florida 8-6-42 4*]

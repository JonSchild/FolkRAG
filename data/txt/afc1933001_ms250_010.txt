JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
4/16/1942

Dear Alan: Shirley has come in
and has helped nurse poor little
Susan back to a sitting up position.
Now Pat is ailing again.
As I wrote to you I have been
intermittently blind in my right
eye and have given up any
reading. Just now my vision 
has cleared up.

As I recall I exploded
in my last letter in a manner
that reasonably might have angered
you. About that and
other past derelictions I have
this to say:

1. Always I try to speak
the truth as I see it, however

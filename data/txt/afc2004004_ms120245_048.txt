When Papa Pierre comes you have to give 
him a little batou chaplet, a pipe fire or a 
cigar for his mouth, a red handkerchief.

Papa Pierre, Haitienne terrible - o 
rainbow -- drinks water everywhere -- 
the most powerful loi with Mait' Zulie 
["exciterot"] who has more money than anybody. 

In Gramave (?), a cave in the mountains 
near Gonaive, you approach & throw 
on rood 10 cents after water, then you 
can enter two rooms, here lives the first

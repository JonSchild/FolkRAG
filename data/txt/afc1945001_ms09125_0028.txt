Mme. Chiang Kai-Shek

Mme Chiang Kai-Sheck is the "First
Lady of China."
Mme Chiang Kai-Sheck was born
in China and came to America to
go to skool.  Here she graduated from
collage.  She later returned to China
and became "Mrs. Chiang Kai-Shek.
Later she became a mother.
   In the year of 1943 she returned
to America to be treated for an
illness.  She planed to tour the
country but recieved word from
her husband to call off her
tour for a few more weeks so
she will be rested up for
her trip home.

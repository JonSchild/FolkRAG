What I Hear About Russia

When people talk about the war, they always seem to talk about Russia and they ask each other is everything Russia does alright, and everything she asks for be granted her. 

When you come right down and think no nation is perfect. Russia [showed] should be judged by her acts and the results of her act. 

Russia now is trying to gain possession of the Baltic States: that territory before World War One belonged to Russia. 

In the first world war Russia fought on the side of the allies of the world, but she dropped out and made a separate peace with Germany.

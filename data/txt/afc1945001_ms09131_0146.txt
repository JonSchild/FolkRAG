V.  In an airplane post on the 
east coast a prim little old-fashioned 
lady in bonnet & shawl was doing her 
duty.  An airplane came by and 
she picked up the telephone to 
report it and said "This 
is post X, an airplane just 
went by.  A P-40, I think, and 
going like a bat out of hell."

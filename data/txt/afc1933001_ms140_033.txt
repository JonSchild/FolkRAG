OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT
December 8, 1938

Dr. Herbert Putnam
Library of Congress
Washington, D. C.

Dear Dr. Putnam:

Thank you for your letter of December
3rd and the enclosed documents suggesting a grant
 of $39,255 to enable the Library of Congress to
increase and make available the material in the
Archive of American Folk-Song. We shall bring
this to the attention of our Trustees and let you
know when a decision is reached.

Sincerely yours,
FP Keppel

October 7, 1942

State: Florida   City: Jacksonville   Reported by: Man from Daytona Beach

Place or circumstance in which rumor passed: In my office

Rumor: A man from Daytona Beach called at this office for information, stated
that the people of Daytona Beach are very much stirred up over a report
that a detachment of WAAC's are to be stationed there, or that that
city is to become a WAAC training center.

He said that the current report in that city is that negro WAAC's are
to be brought in, and that this rumor has caused great resentment.

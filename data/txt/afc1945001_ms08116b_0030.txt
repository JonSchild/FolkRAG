College -- Sophomore -- Negro -- Female 

1  The white people have stopped 
saying you people when referring 
to the the war but they now 
say we people.  

2 -- We women know what 
to do with a woman rival 
but we can't compete with 
Uncle Sam. 

3 -- Uncle Sam ain't no woman 
but he sho can take you man.

4 -- "You know that man you 
use to have?"  Yeh.  Well you 
ain't got him any more

5 -- Don't sit under the apple tree 
with any one else but me 
till I come marching home from 
war.

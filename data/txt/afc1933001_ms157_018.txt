May 22, 1940

Mrs. Annabel Morris Buchanan
16 South Boulevard
Richmond, Va.

Dear Mrs. Buchanan
I have recently found your letter of permission.
Thank you again.
I shall include references to folk-hymns of
America in the page proof of the appendix.
Sincerely yours,

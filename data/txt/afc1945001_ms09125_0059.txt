A joke 
Once upon a time there was a Japanese, a Russian & a American. 
A King has a castle & a  (???) was in the castle & the King would give 100 bags of gold to the person who could get him out. So along came a Russian. He ran out to strong! to strong. Then came a American. He also ran out too strong! too strong! Along came the Japanese! And he pull off his shoes and the (???) ran out too strong! Too strong.

Raleigh, North Carolina                           August 5, 1942

In talking with a lady of this City she stated that she heard that a ship
was sunk off the Coast of Wilmington, North Carolina on its first trial ran. This
statement was made by a lady who is an office worker and of the very highest type. [*14*]

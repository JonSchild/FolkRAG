JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
3/24/1942

Dear Alan: I am returning Dr.
Evan's letter. I don't see how I could
use it now except to lay myself
lible to prosecution - since you
say that the trip has been given the
coup detat

If you would call and see one
or all of the men you mention - 
Lanham, Johnson, Garrett, Kleberg -   tell them
our story, and ask their help, I could
then follow up with letters where you
think they might do good. Beckwith from
East Texas, who was at Bill Kittrell's
party for you, is another good
man, also a U. of T. man not much
older than you. The same may be said for

Freshman Negro & Female
1 - Why does uncle Sam want to take all of the
young men & leave the old & lades behind we
can't use them
2 - As soon as a negro goes to officers tranning
and becomes a first Lut. they are sent over
seas to dodge buttles of the Japs
3. One man was on guard duet for 48 hrs.
and his Lut. came buy and he was sleeping
and when he got up to him he said "be quite I heard
something coming like my enemies" and after
that he got a promotion from Pvt. to Cpl. for
saying something sounds like my enemies.
4 - What do I have to fight for. I dont have
any more [?ights] after war if you promise
them to us.
5 - maybe it is the time for some other
nation to rules the world maybe
they are the "Japs"

Negro Folk Song.

In any consideration of the development of Negro
folk song in this country, there are certain 
cultural facts to be taken into account.
First - Some general characteristics of African
culture.
1) Western Africa an elaborate social and 
economic structure. Many classes - highly stratified -
huge markets - artisans -
2) Religiously - elaborately developed - heirarchies
 of Gods - complex ceremonial - possessions
- divination - dancing & singing importantly
associated.
3) Cooperative work groups - work
songs.
4) A cultivation of the dance.
5) Orchestras of drums.
6) Unison harmony - departures from the
tone.
7) Ceremonial politeness
8) Leader refrain style of singing

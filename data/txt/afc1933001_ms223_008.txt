JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
1/22/1940.

Dear Alan:  This letter is
apropos of nothing - except
your birthday. I am unable
to write the run-along-and-be-
happy, altogether delightful epistles
that flowed from your mother's
pen-point as unresistingly and
delightfully as a waterfall over
the smooth stones of a sunny
glade. I am not sure just
how old you are - 23 or 24.
Nor am I sure that you like
to be reminded.

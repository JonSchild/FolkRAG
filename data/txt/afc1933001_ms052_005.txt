[Letterhead of THE MACMILLAN COMPANY, not transcribed]

March 7, 1938
Mr. Edward N. Waters
Division of Music
Library of Congress
Washington, D. C.

Dear Mr. Waters:
Could you let us know within the
next day or so when we may expect the return of
the galley proofs for Mr. Lomax's COWBOY SONGS
AND OTHER FRONTIER BALLADS. If we are to publish
in June, we should have these proofs
returned this week.
Very truly yours,
Susan S. Prink
THE MACMILLAN COMPANY
Manufacturing Department
SSP:R

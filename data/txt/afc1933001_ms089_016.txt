Bayou Bend
Houston, Texas
Dec. 17 '41

My dear Mr. Lomax :
Your letter telling 
me of your and your son's
dedication to Brother of your
recent book "Our Singing Country"
gives me the greatest pleasure.
It has been areal satisfaction to me 
to pass the volume on to many
of Brother's friends and family

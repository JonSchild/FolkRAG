September 11, 1940

Mrs. Edward A. Ward
802 Irving Ave. Saginaw, Michigan

Dear Mrs. Ward:

Your letter of the 26th. has been referred to me for answering and I'm it on to the Columbia Artist's Bureau who manage my lecture engagements. I appreciate your interest in having me and I certainly hope that I will be able to come this year.

Sincerely yours,

Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk Song
Library of Congress

JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
10/29/1941

Dear Alan: I ran on the
enclosed letter today in trying
to determine a few of the many
communications I would include
in my "Adventures". I'd
like to have it back, although
I've promised myself - and you -
not to forward you any
other paper with the request that
it be returned. Such a process
does not fit into the scheme
of things where you and I
are the [F] related and principal
agents. And, though you may indignantly
deny the statement, I
have always tried to deal fairly
by you. Anyhow - you will

JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS

6/1/1940

Dear Alan:

   You speak of the Potomac
farm as though you had written
about it some other time.  Also you
did not date your letter and
so we were unable to locate your
radio talk at Cincinnati.  I
hope you have given Bass a
happy visit.  Find One Dollar enclosed.
   Do not forget, in making your
final trade with Columbia, that I insist
on payment for the use of my
song on which I hold copyright.
   Of late I have longed to see you.
Several times I have started packing
my grip for a train trip to
Washington.  There are some

Harry on his wife "I don't know what color her
eyes were. I don't take much 'count of eyes."

On his son
"The boy died; that's the one I wanted to live.
I should ha' taught him everything I knew. I'd 
a given him all my knowledge, so he could have [gone]
kept everything, and gone on. There ain't nobody
to take my place. Out family's dying out.

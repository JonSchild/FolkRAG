                        March 22, 1941



Miss Margaret Reed
Board of Public Education
Parkway at 21st Street
Philadelphia, Pa.
Dear Miss Reed:

I am very much obliged to you for taking
the trouble to send me the songs you enclose.
That is a beautiful version of 
Perri merri dictum dominee.

                            Cordially yours,

                                        Alan Lomax
                                        Assistant in Charge
                                        Archive of American Folk Song

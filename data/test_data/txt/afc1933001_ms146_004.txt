

[*Librarian A.*]

Division of Music
June 1, 1939

I recommend that the attached bills for recording material, 
which total $80.50, be paid and charged to Increase. These were 
submitted by Alan Lomax in connection with recording done for the
Archive of American Folk-Song during his stay in New York.

Chief, Division of Music

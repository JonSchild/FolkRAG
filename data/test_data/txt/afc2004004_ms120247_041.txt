56 A3 This is sung only in the hounfort in honor of the master of
the Le Fou hounfort---Papa Dumballa. Elize balon.

Damballa Wedo-e
Dans qui sou mander salado,
Damballa Wedo-e,
Dans qui soun mander salado,
Domitans silibo,
Ayi bobo,
Damballa Wedo-e,
Dans qui sou mander salado,
Dmoitans silibo,
Ayi bobo.

Wedo--One of the names of Papa Dumballa
Salado--Language?
Domitans---'Domiso---Adamisu, Theoline's vaudoo name.
Silibo--- Langage?
Bobo--- ay bob,--an exclamation expressing satisfaction and pleasure
and enthusiasm over the virtues of a vaudoo song or how well**
it has been sung or how "hot" it is or was.

College- Senior Negro Female.

1/ This war has eliminated many of the Mr. 
Jone's & Miss Ann's.

2/ We all will met with the same
measure of justice before worlds
ward no 2 is over.

3/ The White Race has stoped saying
you people in refuring to the colored
race but we people must win
this war.

4. If I only knew Uncle Sam was going
to spair the farmers to produce food
a year ago I would have admitted
I too was a good farm hand.

5 Why do we want more babies born
when we are eating rationed 
food, by they time the arrive
we will all be starved.

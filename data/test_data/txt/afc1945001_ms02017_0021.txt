Locality in Which Rumor was Heard: – Enroute from Atlanta to
Birmingham via train

Date Rumor was Heard: – Wednesday, August 12, 1942

Locality in Which Rumored Action
Transpired: – Gulf Coast area

A yard building ships for the United States Navy was taking a
new ship on a trial test and after getting only a short distance out into
the Gulf of Mexico sighted a periscope from a submarine and turned around
and was able to reach dock in safety.

Ellen B. Wallace,
Assistant Regional Representative,
Bureau of Public Assistance

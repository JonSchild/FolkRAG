The Yorkshire Bite

In London there lived a ma son by trade,  He kept two
ser vants, a man and a maid;  A York Shire by  by the
name of  John was the one that he  kept to be his man. 
Lol - de - dum - lol -de - do,  lol - de - daddy , del -do  dum.

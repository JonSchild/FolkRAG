Selective Services

1.     The Army physical standards are going to be lowered to that practically
         every man will be taken.  (Married man with wife who works.  10/7/42)
2.     There are not enough 18 and 19 years olds to make a great deal of
        difference in deferments, because a large percentage of them have
        already enlisted.  (Worker in war industry.  10/9/42)
3.     Selective Services quota system is going to be changes as in many
         districts the quotas cannot be filled without taking skilled
         workers.  (Woman, prominent in public affairs.  10/8/42)

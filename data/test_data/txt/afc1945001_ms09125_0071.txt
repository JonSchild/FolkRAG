Astra Williams
T. J. Wilson - 130
Englewood. H. School
March 12, 1943
English
1. It has been said that Hitler 
is captured or dead, because several 
weeks he has not shown his head.

2. I wish I may I wish I 
might have the wish I wish to night 
I wish Hitler be killed this very night 
so he can't continue to take others 
lives.

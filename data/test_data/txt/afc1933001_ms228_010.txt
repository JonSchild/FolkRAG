JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
6/14/1940

Dear Alan:  It's a long time since
I have heard any news from the
Singing Country.  I still wish the
book dedicated to Will Hagg.
   Today is Johnny's birthday.
His address is George[?] West, Texas.
Affectionately,
Father

P.S.  You have never told me
what became of your date
at Pittsburg.

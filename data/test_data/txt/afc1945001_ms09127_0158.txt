Mary Agnes Majosky
March 15, 1943
English II - Per. 8
[*a*]

The Berlin radio complains of a new form
of sabotage in occupied countries.  When axis
ships are in port, saboteurs sneak onto the
piers, and paint bull's-eyes on the
ships so they will be better targets for
U.S. and British subs.

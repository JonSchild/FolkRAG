Port Aransas, Texas,
3/21/1939
Dear Alan:
Macmillans never did reply to 
my complaint about the Oxford Book
Here is the naive parry from my 
thrust at the editor direct.  And I 
handed him a mean job.
Here's what he did to us - to repeat:
1. used without anyone's permission (so 
far discovered), without any charge 
whatever, 12 of our songs -- several 
of them long composites.  These 12 
songs cover 28 pages of the book 
or more than 5% more than one twentieth of
the text.
2. In his index of titles he credits "American
Songs and Ballads" J. A. Lomax with eleven of these

A Rumor

Adolph Hitler, Der Fureher, as he is known to the world, is suffering a brain concusion.
It is said that a brain specialist, one of the best in Europe, went to Berlin to see the greatest menace that world has ever had, is losing his mind. It is said but his doctor that hitler has been taken from his command and that Doktor Goebbels and the field Marshall Herman Goering is now in command.

Benito Mussolini the IL Duce of Italy is almost out of the war.

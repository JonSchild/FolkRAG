William L. Dill, Regional Director,
Region III

Philadelphia, Pa. 
August 5, 1942

W. Oliver Kincannon,
Informational Service Representative

Report of Rumors

As requested in your letter of July 30, I have kept a
record of all rumors relating to the war which I heard during the
week of August 3 to date, and triplicate copies are being transmitted
herewith.

W. Oliver Kincannon

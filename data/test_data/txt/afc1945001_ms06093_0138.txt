5. Heard near Stuttgart, Ark. -- 8/5/42: 
"They're making all of these places bigger. They tell me that they're going to add two miles on the south end of this field, but we can't get jobs. I guess we had better go back to El Dorado because I hear that they're doubling that plant and that it's going to cost $100,000,000.00."

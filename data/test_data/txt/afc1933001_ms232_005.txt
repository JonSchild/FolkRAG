Uncle Billy Macrae, 117 years
old, his voice thin and
reedy but still true sang
some preachers steamboat
and slavery songs - and 
told stories.

"I has 36 children,
18 boys.  I can't just figure
out how many girls."
He showed me his own remedy
for restoring lost manhood.
"Two doses a week 
keeps me potent," he claimed.
A large square bottle contained
the brew:  half whiskey
and half a brew made by
boiling bull nettle roots.

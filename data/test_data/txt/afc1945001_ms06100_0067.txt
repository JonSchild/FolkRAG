Two working women report hearing there is to be rationing of
women's clothing, that silk hose are to be rationed at the rate of a 
pair a month, that girdles and lingerie will be rationed but OPA has not
decided on the extent of the rationing. [*Michigan (Aug. 3-8) 9*]

It's too hot to work in this 
section, which [is] abounds in folk 
stuff of every variety unnoticed
and unrecorded. Cooler weather 
would be far better, When I talk 
to some people I may decide 
to stay a week or so, though 
I rather think I'll go on to Atlanta, 
see Johnny again, and then 
head for Livingston, Ala. I'll put
Johnny's address (for my mail) on 
the outside of this letter.

LIBRARY OF CONGRESS
RECEIVED
DEC 16 1938 VW
SECRETARY'S OFFICE
MUSIC

Mozelle Ky
Dec. 13 1938
Library of Congress
Washington, D.C.
Dear Sirs:
Farmer Collett made a few
Photograph Records about Oct
1937, Will you please if
possible send me as many 
as Three. If they cost ship them
C.O.D and I will surley
pay them out. Please send
Three that he made By his
self if possible. Hope you
can Do me this favor.
Thanks, Ans. at Once please.
Yours Truly
(Mrs.) Ruth R. Roarck.

July 17th, 1941

Mr. Alan Lomax
Archive of American Folk Music
Library of Congress
Washington, D. C. 

Dear Mr. Lomax:

Following up my wire to you, I should like to outline 
for you in fuller detail information about 
your trip to Mexico.

A reservation has been made whereby it will be possible 
for you to fly from Nashville to Atlanta, 
Georgia, in order to join the Golden Gate Quartet 
and Mr. Leadbetter enroute to Mexico. I list below 
the schedule you may follow:

August 10th, Leave Nashville, Tenn. 10:20 PM
August 11th, Arrive Atlanta, Ga.          1:05 AM
August 11th, Leave Atlanta, Ga.          1:20 AM
August 11th, Arrive Mexico, D.F.       12:30 PM
                          (Via Eastern Airlines)

I shall be glad to forward your round-trip ticket 
as soon as I receive it, if you will tell me where 
to send it. Please call on me if there are any 
further questions.

Sincerely yours,

Ingrid Johnson
Ingrid Johnson
Secretary to Mr. Fisher

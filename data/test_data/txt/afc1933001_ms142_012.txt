LIBRARY OF CONGRESS
WASHINGTON

DIVISION OF MUSIC

Friday 
Feb. 16, 1939

Dear Harold,

My address is now & will continue
to be
124 W. 12th.
Elizabeth is still in bed.
Columbia is lot 's of fun.
Thanks for the loan. Will
write further shortly.

Yours,
Alan.

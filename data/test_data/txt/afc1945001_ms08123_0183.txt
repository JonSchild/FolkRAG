Eleanor Fredrick
7th Butler
English
I am going to tell a story about a boy in the army. He never wrote home to his parents.
Tom had come from a pretty wealthy family, and had inlisted in the army. After he had been their awhile, some of his "pals" were made corporals, but Tom never was honored.
On Sundays the men would go to church and then come back and write letters home. Tom would just sit around and never do anything. Whenever one of his friends would say anything to him, Tom would get angry and say that they were picking on him because they were a corporal and he wasn't.

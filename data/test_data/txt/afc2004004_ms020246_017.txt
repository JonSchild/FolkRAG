I GOT A SISTER
A lullaby-spiritual

I got a sister over yonder (3)
On that other shore.

By an' by we will go over an' see her (3)
On the other shore.

I has a Saviour over yonder, etc.

I has a mother over yonder, etc.

(Can be continued, including various members
of a family.)

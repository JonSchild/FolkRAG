October 1, 1937 

Mr. Alan Lomax 
General Delivery 
Hyden, Kentucky 

Dear Alan: 

The Cosmos Club of Washington, D.C., 
would appreciate an address by you upon
your return. Will you please write Mr. W. 
J. Humphreys at the Club informing him of 
the approximate date of your return and 
suggesting a suitable time for you to speak
there. 
Regards: 
Sincerely, 

Acting-Chief, 
Division of Music 

HS

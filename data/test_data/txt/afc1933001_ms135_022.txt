Dr. Spivack

Martins Point, S. C.
July 9 - '38

Dear Mr. Lomax

At last I have completed 
the task I under took almost 
a year ago.

I know you will understand 
why I have not finished it 
sooner, when I tell you the 
truth I have lost the use 
of my hands 
and had to be placed 
in the hospital.

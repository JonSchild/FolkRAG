Betty Jane Nicholson
    A                                       Anecdote

Two buck privates met a major.
After passing him, one of them
surprisingly hauled off & gave
him a wallop on the chin.
As the major staggered to his
feet the other private let him 
have it and down went the major 
again!
  After having rested in the
guardhouse for some time, the
two privates were brought before 
the major, "Why did you
sock me?" he asked the first
offender.

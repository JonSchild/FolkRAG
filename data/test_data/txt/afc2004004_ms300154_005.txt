[*Absil Morrison, Landis, Ark.*]

Landis Ark.
Jan. 14th
Mr Lomax
Dear sir
in Regard to the Records
I would like to know
soon as Possible what
you all are going to
do about my Recordings
so I'll know what to 
do.
there was a man come to
see me yeasterday wanting
me to do some Recording
for him & I told him
that I couldn't until I 
Knew What you all were
going to do.  So Please
let me know soon.
Yours truly Absil Morrison
(over)

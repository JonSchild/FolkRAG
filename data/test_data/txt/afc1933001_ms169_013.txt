Subject   The Ballads and Folk. Songs of America
Date   April 9, 1941, at 2:45 p.m.
for the sum of $100 Dollars
and [which is to cover all] expenses to and from New York
Signature   Alan Lomax
Address   Library of Congress, Washington, D.C.
Date of Contract  July 10, 1940.

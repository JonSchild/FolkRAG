Becky Deam 

This man an' this woman was gamblin'. He tried to take the 
money up. He was jivin' the woman. He thought she didn't 
know no better an' when she found de money was gone, she grabbed 
a single-tree lyin' on the levy an' draw back----

She started to hit one wid a single-tree, 
Might-a heered the rascal holl'in', "Honey, doncha murder me."

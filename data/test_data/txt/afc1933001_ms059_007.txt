[*A. A. 7. S.*]

Division of Music
June 27, 1939

Attached is an approved recommendation authorizing Mr. John Lomax
to make a recording trip for the Archive of American Folk-Song. As
will be seen from his expense account (also attached), Mr. Lomax
exceeded the amount authorized by $267.80.

I recommend that this addition sum be authorized.

Chief, Division of Music

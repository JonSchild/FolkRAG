W [*A*]
Division of Music
Feb. 29, 1940

Attached is a letter from Sam Henry offering to
make a copy for the Library of Congress of his collection
of Ulster Folk Songs for $20. I recommend that the 
Library acquire this copy and the cost, $20, be charged 
to Increase.

Chief, Division of Music

Johnson, Ira

It is said that in the Solomon
Islands battles, the Japs run through
the jungle battlefields shouting, "Hey
Joe, where are you?"  in hope that the
Americans will reveal their positions.
I have heard a rumor that after the war
all the English speaking nations will
unite to form one great nation

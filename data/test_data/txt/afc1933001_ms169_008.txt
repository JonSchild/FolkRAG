April 5, 1941

Mrs. Frank Day
c/o 4 C
Naco, Arizona

Dear Mrs. Day:

The names of articles dealing with American Folk Festivals are
listed below.*  For additional information I suggest that you
write the following:

   The White Top Mountain Festival, White Top, Va.

   The Old Fiddlers' Contest, Galax, Va.

   Bascom Lamar Lunsford, c/o National Folk Festival Association,
     Washington Post, Washington, D. C.

   Sarah G. Knott, c/o National Folk Festival Association,
      Washington, Post, Washington, D. C.

   George Korson, Bucknell University, Lewisburg, Pa.

   Arthur L. Campa, University of New Mexico, Albuquerque,
      New Mexico.  (In re the Coronado Festival).

   Paul Bunyan Celebration, Bemidji, Minn.

   In regard to the folk festivals of Latin-America, I suggest that
you write to Conchita James, Pan-American Union, Washington, D. C.

Sincerely yours,

Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk Song

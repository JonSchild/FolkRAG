August 29, 1940
Miss Beatrice Sawyer Rossell
American Library Assoc.
520 North Michigan Ave.
Chicago, Ill.
Dear Miss Rossell:
I guess I'll have to forego with you my order
for the 50 reprints. I would, however, appreciate
whatever extra copies of the bulletin you can send.
Very sincerely yours,
Alan Lomax

Archives of American Folk Songs
Library of Congress

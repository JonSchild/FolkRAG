William L. Sherer, the news
commentator and auther of Berlin
Diary, was in the grocery store at
the Connecticut village where he
lives, and heard a woman ask
the clerk for can of dog food. The
told her that there was none in
stock, because of government order
prohibiting the canning of dog food.
The woman was loud in her
complaint... "Look, lady," Sherer told
her, "Here in America we've stopped
canning dog meat because we are almost
out of tin. but in Germany they
stopped canning dog meat because they're
all out of dogs.          [*Folder118*]

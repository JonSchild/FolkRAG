   Perhaps you are wondering what all this
has to do with the subject of this
seminar -- the collection of folk-lore.
It has, I believe, much to do with
it.  Folk lore collecting costs a lot of money.
Besides it is very hard -- and sometimes
dangerous work.  We must know
in our own minds what we are doing
and why before we begin.

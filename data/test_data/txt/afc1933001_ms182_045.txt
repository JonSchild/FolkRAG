May 25 '42

Dear Mr. Lomax,

I intend to go to Washington
next Friday, May the 29th. As soon
as I arrive I will call you by
phone and may be we can start
work on Saturday morning.

Sincerely yours

S. Comhair

Madame Suzanne Comhair Sylvain

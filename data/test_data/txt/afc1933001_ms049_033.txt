"Old Fiddlers'  Convention"
October  22-23 in Galax,  Va.

         I recommend that Mr. A. Lomax be sent as the Library's 
official representative to record the folk-music at Old
Fiddlers'  Convention in Galax,   Virginia,   on October 22-23,  1937,
and any other folk-music which he find in the surrounding
territory.     For this trip, Mr. Lomax will need mileage and per
dime for a period not to exceed ten days, to be charged to

                                                  Harold Akwack 
                                                       Acting-Chief,
                                                             Division of Music

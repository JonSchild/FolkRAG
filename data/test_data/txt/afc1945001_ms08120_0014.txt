Norma Ann Jones       March 12, 1943
O'Donnell    7th Period

Rumors

We have not been sending aid
to Russia.
Hitler is dead (I hope)
You can get can foods with
out stamps
Gasoline is being stored up to waste.
Sugar is plentiful
Mussolini is dying in fear of
what might happen to Italy &
himself.
The war will be over next week.
Milk will be rationed soon.
Hitler is married & has twelve kids,
quite & old man don't you think?

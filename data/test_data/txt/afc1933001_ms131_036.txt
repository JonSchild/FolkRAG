March 23, 1938

Mr. Lee Lamar Robinson
232 Woodward Building
Washington, D. C.

Dear Mr. Robinson:

    We are returning herewith the
draft of your article on the Archive
of American Folk-Song. As you will
notice, Mr. Alan Lomax made a few
corrections and several suggestions
in pencil which I hope will prove
useful to you.

    Very truly yours,

    Acting-Chief,
    Division of
    Music

Eloise Ellison
March 12, 1943

On the radio, March 11, 1943 was announced
that meat, cheese, all oil and fat foods
would be rationed by April 1st.

One morning coming to school
on the streetcar some women
were saying, "maybe it'll be good
for us if the Japs do come over."

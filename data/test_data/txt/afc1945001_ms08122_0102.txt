Catherine Gindice          March 12, 1943
Eng. 2 - 9th per.          Div. Knutzen - 139
Joke
"Sergeant"—Private Jones bring this letter 
to General Tomps. 
[Five] Five  minutes later Private Jones 
came back with the letter.
 "Sergeant"—I told you to give [it] that letter to the 
General. 
" Private Jones"—Yes Sir, but on the Generals 
door it said, "Private, Keep Out." You 
bring it Sarge you can go in it only 
says, "Private Keep Out."

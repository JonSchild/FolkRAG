1. For punishment in the Army, a soldier is sent to 
bed with a Waac.
2. A little boy was on his way to his Grandmothers
on the train. 
The first stop a beautiful woman got on and 
sat down beside the little boy. 
The second stop a group of sailors got aboard.
The following stop the lady got off.
Then a strange thing happened, one of the sailors 
looking very cross came over to the little boy 
and said "Listen you little weasel. Why didn't 
you tell us she wasn't your mother"?
3. Did you hear the one about [y] the man that 
took a tape - measure to bed with him to see

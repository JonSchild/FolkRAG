Waitches, Leonard     1st Per. 

[*a*] 

When American Troops landed in
North Africa, rumors reached me
concerning the capture of African towns 
by these same troops.  It was rumored
that in attack, the American soldiers
died by the hundreds, although the
towns were captured and the occupants
made captives.

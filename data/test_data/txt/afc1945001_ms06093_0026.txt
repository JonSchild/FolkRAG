I was told that a woman who sells
eggs to Pine Camp could not deliver
more eggs until she could get some
egg crates. They, at Pine Camp, refused
to sell her the old egg crates and
put them in a pile and burned
them.                      Garage
38                            Newark. N.J.
Male                        Helen W Herman.
C                              8/8/42

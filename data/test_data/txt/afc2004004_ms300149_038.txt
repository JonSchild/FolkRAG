St. Simons Isld. Ga Oct 8-1961
Mr. Alen Lomax Dear Sir
How are you today I trust that you
are well & getting on fine. now 
lomax I recive the check you sent
me & How glad I was to get it as it
Just come in the needy time now
when you need us come or send for
us will be glad to go John & all the
rest of the Boys receive there check
I am still Singing at the Cloyster
Hotel I am the leader there
thank you. thank you
Resp yours
Willis Proctor

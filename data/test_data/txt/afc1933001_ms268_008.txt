OBH/tre
February 21, 1941.

Dear Mr. Lomax:
I am very pleased to learn that the program
Monday night was so successful and that our men contributed
to this success. Your letter will be shown
to them.
We were glad to cooperate with you in this
matter and appreciate your thanks.
Sincerely yours,
O B Hardison
O. B. HARDISON
Commander, U. S. Navy,
Commanding.

Mr. Alan Lomax,
Assistant in Charge,
Archive of American Folk Song,
Library of Congress,
Washington, D. C.

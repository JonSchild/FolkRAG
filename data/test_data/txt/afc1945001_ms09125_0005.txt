Shirley Gill
March 12, 1943
A Joke
Mary Jane [was] fell off the
White Cliffs of dover and was ran
over by the Chattanooga Choo Choo and
was buried Deep In the Heart of Texas
and Jim never brings her Pretty Flowers.

February 11, 1939

Mr. Edward N. Waters,
Acting-Chief
Division of Music
Library of Congress
Washington, D. C. 

Dear Mr. Waters:

    Thank you for your letter of February
10th. You can be assured that your records
will not be used for any commercial purposes
whatever. This Corporation is, under the
terms of its Royal Charter, prohibited from
broadcast commercial programs or any
programs for which we receive payment.

    Your sincerely,
    Felix Greene
    Felix Greene

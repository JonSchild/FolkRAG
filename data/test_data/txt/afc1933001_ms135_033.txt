July 12, 1938
Mr. Allen Lomax
I hope you have a very successful trip and if you get time, send me your address if you know what it is going to be. Also, if there is anything that I can do for you here in the city, such as procuring needles or blanks, please let me know. 
Sincerely yours,
CITYWIDE SOUND SERVICE
David M. Bull, Jr.

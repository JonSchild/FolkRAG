10th (May)
Dear Alan (1)
Dear Elizabeth (2)
:
Last night was a fine night.
I had given up and gone to sleep 
at 4:00 in the afternoon, and 
when I woke up it was 7:00
and raining. So I lay and thought
how fine it would be, and then 
I got up and went out.  The rain 
soaked me soon, and I had 
small lakes in my shoes.  It was 
quite dark and little lightenings 
came and shivered and went 
out. It was a glorious weather.
But this morning I was horrid 
and stupid and sleepy and 
very very cross.  Sometimes I despair

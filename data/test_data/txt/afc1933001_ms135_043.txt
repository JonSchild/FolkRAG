July 18, 1938

Dr. Chas. Edward Brown
2011 Chadburn Ave.,
Madison, Wisconsin.

Dear Dr. Brown:
Thank you for your very encouraging and hospitable
letter.
I shall communicate at once with Mrs. Brown when 
I arrive in Wisconsin.  That should be on or before the 
fifteenth of August.  I will send you a card shortly before 
I arrive.

Sincerely yours,
Alan Lomax
Assistant in charge
Archive of American 
Folksong.

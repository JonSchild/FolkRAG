"Tom Bolyn had no boods to wear.
He bought him a goatskin and made him a pair,
The wooly side out and the skinny side in,
"I'm going a courtin'," says Tom Bolyn.

- - -

"Tom Bolyn, his wife and her mother,
All went over the bridge together,
The bridge it broke, they all went in,
'First to the bottom' said Tom Bolyn."

Here's a man with trouble on his mind:
"I'm going to lay my head on Jay Gould's railroad line,
Let that 2:19 ease my troubled mind."

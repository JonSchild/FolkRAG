Alice Vinckunas     English 121

     One soldier stationed at Los Angeles,
California, was dressed in a gay print shirt
and pants which came above his ankles. He
was soon standing at attention when the
sergeant came tripping down the way.
The sergeant with bulging eyes, said to
him. "Good Lord! man, do you know what
clothes you're wearing? But the only
reply from the soldier was. "But sarge
Everyone in California dresses like this."

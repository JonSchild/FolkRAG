May 28, 1940

Dear John:
Go to it. Write the Ballad Hunter
book as you might talk it off to good
friends. Do a first draft cramming in
everything that comes to your mind.
Then cut it down. You have a dandy book
on that subject in you.
Always good luck to you,
Paul

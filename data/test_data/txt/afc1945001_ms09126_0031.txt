Marion Elaine Thomas 




One day, in (?) a general was
writing a private the the cook phack
when the private told the general
that it was about time for Annete 
to come. The general surprised asked
who is Annete so the private replied 
"Annete is my little black hen who
comes in every morning about 9:00 and 
lay me an egg. She is very shy and won't 
lay in front of (?). Annete usually
finished up about 10 though. So the general
and the private shivered outside in the
cold while little Annete did her work.

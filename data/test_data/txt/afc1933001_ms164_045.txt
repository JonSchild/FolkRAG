December 19, 1940
Mr. Alan Lomax
Division of Music
Library of Congress
Washington, D. C.
Dear Alan:
I enjoyed my little visit with you in New York and also meeting
Nick Ray.

Will you please give me your choice by return mail on GRAY GOOSE?

When you recover the $5.00 Huddie, will you please send me a 
check.

A very joyous Christmas to you and Mrs. Lomax.
Cordially,
Bob

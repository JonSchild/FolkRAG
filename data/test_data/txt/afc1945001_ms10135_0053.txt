Army Slang

1. Armored cow - canned milk
2. Battery acid - coffee
3. Blind flying - a date with a girl you have never seen.
4. China Clipper - dishwasher
5. Hashburner - cook
6. Homing device - furlough
7. Jackson - form of address for any soldier
8. Roll up your flaps - stop talking
9. Serum - intoxicating beverages
10. Skirt patrol - search for feminine companionship.
11. Sugar report - letter from girlfriend.

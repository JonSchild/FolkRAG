                                                                             8-8-42
W.A.A.C.

There are innumerable jokes about the W.A.A.C.'s traveling about the state, most
[of them] definitely on the smutty side. As put by one observer, "We haven't had
a swell topic like this for years and everyone is really going to town on it. I could
go on for hours and not run out." [*Iowa 8*]

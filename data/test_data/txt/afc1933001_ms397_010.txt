Later, as I climbed up the rocky hill, again headed for
Deming, I heard the old woman of the desert who gave me the two
parodies sing cheerfully as she turned to her work:
"Give me a girl with a chubby fat hand,
An' a dimple in her cheek like a hole in a pan."

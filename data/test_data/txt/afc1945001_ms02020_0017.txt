October 13, 1942.

Mr. William H Webber,
Office of War Information,
1400 Pennsylvania Avenue, N.W.,
Washington, D. C.

Dear Mr. Webber:
Enclosed is Rumor Report re-
quested in your letter of October 1, 1942.

California takes Columbus Day
very seriously and this necessitates waiting until
today to gather in the last rumors.
Very truly yours, 
/signature/ J. Crawford Brooks /end signature/

J. Crawford Brooks,
Administrative Assistant.

THE SOUND SPECIALTIES COMPANY
LINCOLN THOMPSON, PRES
31 BECKLEY AVE. STAMFORD, CONN., U.S.A.

Aug. 2, 1937
Mr. Alan Lomax
Lib. of Congress
Washington, D.C.

Dear Alan, 
I am going on a trip this
week & can return through Washington and 
be there first thing Sat. morning, Aug. 7th
If this date will not be satisfactory
for the demonstration, will you kindly write
me c/o Miss Constance Welch, Dept. of Public 
Speaking, West Virginia University,
MORGANTOWN, WEST VA.
Sincerely,
Lincoln Thompson


[* I want to go to New York Thursday afternoon. Its my only chance.  A.L.*]

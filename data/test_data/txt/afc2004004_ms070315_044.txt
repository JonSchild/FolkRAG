New Stagolee Blues
Joe Williams , learned 
on a strawberry harvest, Independence,
La., a year ago. [Lea] Learned part from 
a boy - who said it was on a 
record.
S was a bad man, you know everybody know
His buddy shot him wid a special & he kilt
him wid his forty-fo
-
You know we heard old Stag was dead
He rose this mornin' dressed bofe black,, 
blue & red.
-
I'm goin' to town, Stag, what you want me to bring you back
Bring me a new pair o' panties & a little
Stetson hat

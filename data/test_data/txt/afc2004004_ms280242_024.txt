9) "O daughter, now I will read thy dream,
The blood it proves for sorrow.
That your true lover, John, lies dead and gone
In the dowie dens of Yarrow."
10) Her hair bein' of three-quarters long,
And the colour of it was yellow,
She wrapped it round his middle so small,
And carried him home from Yarrow.
11) "O mother dear, come make my bed,
And make it long and narrow.
My true lover, John, died for me today,
And I'll die for him tomorrow."

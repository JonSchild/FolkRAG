From Margery C. Quigley, Montclair, New Jersey
Heard in Montclair, New Jersey, August 10, 1942
Subject: AXIS PROPAGANDA

"A Russian musician recently come here from occupied France
says that the French are being told again and again that with a victory
of the Americans and British the Communists and Jews will immediately [*6-*]
come back into the community life and into power."

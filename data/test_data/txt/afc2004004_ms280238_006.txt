year.  What he had to take - he must've had thousands, I think.
Lomax: well, didn't they get any salary?
Cox: Not until they was home. Yes, they got a little
spendin' money. They never earnt nothin'. Weather'd blow 'em about,

Sometimes blows away a fortnight. Blowin' right away there, hard, an'
then they had to get back to Yarmouth.  Now with this here steam,
off you go, you sail out of the harbour - back again.

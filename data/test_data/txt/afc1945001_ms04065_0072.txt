The wife of a contractor told her next door neighbor that in conversation at her father-in-law's farm home a few days before, all were agreed that if the President would do more about the war and less about State politics, (such as interfering in Allred's campaign for the United States Senate), more would be accomplished.
San Antonio, Texas
8/4/42

NEW ENGLAND RUMORS – 11.

MASSACHUSETTS.
August 10, 1942.

Mass. Committee on Public Safety (continued)

England will not open a second fromt at this time because it does not want
to use their own troops — waiting for American troops.

Defense plants are laying off men because of lack of material.

Jew baiting still persists to a large extent.

Japan has a large invasion force in the Aleutian Islands.

Beef being held up at packing plants to force prices up — artificial storage.

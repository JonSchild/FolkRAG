Music   Mail 
February 23, 1938
Please have your delivery wagon call at the Cosmos Club, Madison 
Place and H Street, N.W. and get the following:
2 recording machines, 2 batteries, and phonograph.
Extra care should be taken in handling these machines and batteries.
Acting-Chief,
Division of Music

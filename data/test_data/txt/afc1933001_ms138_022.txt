A October 7, 1938
Mr. Felix Greene
The British Broadcasting Corp. 
620 Fifth Avenue
New York City
Dear Mr. Greene:
Permit me to thank you for your
very kind letter in regard to the Archive 
of American Folk-Song.  We are pleased to 
have been of service to the British
Broadcasting Corporation.  Please do not 
hesitate to call on us again. 
I hope that you will visit Washington
again soon and that we will have more time
to discuss matters. 
With kindest regards, 
Sincerely yours, 
Harold Spivacke
Chief, Division of 
Music

August 5, Helena
1. While at the Great Falls Fair, a woman who should know what
she's saying, said that they have discovered seepage in the ground
where the Bomber Base is situated, and it is rumored that the project
will be moved to Helena where it was supposed to have been constructed
in the first place. (Writer and housewife.) 12

Akins Clarke

1.  It was rumored that the Japanese are so
well prepared for this war, that the minimum
amount of time that they will last will be
five years.

2.  A recent report from a Japanese Commander
aboard a sinking ship went as follows,
"So sorry to report that our ship, now
settling on the ocean bottom has been
sunk by a group of American ships which
had been reported sunk five weeks ago
by our Jap submarines."

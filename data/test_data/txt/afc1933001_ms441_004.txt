What did you bring me, dear mother,
To keep me from the gallis pole?"

"Son, I brought you some silver, 
Son, I brought you some gold, 
Son, I brought you from de gallis pole."

"Aunty did you bring me any silver?"
and so an though like all the relatives one can think of.

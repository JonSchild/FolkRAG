Rumors.
I heard the following rumors;
1. Clothes will be rationed April 1st and $40.00 will be allowed a year for clothes.

2. School will be out in May to allow students to help in the agricultural labor 'situation.'

3. In southern training camps, Negro boys are deprived of their guns and are given broom-sticks as ammo.

4. Hitler is gravely ill.

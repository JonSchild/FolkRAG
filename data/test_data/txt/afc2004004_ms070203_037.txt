The old firm of
Wright & Ketchum
called
Skinem & Catchem
banked their logs
at Averill. One [of]
crew coming & one working
Paid their men off
at $10 a month &
a boot in behind.

50 yrs. ago Bill whipped
2 fellows bigger than
him - Boston & Raymond
two tough eggs - came
into his grocery store &
raised hell after

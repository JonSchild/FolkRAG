A lady went to the meat market and ordered six pounds of steak, three pounds of lamb, five pounds of ground beef and a roast. After she had bought this meat, she told the butcher she would like to have this delivered. He said, “Lady, I’m sorry”  She said, “Why not, there is the cart outside the door.” He said, “But you just bought the horse.”

July 14, 1941

Dear Alan : Today I got by Express
the packages. In additions to 
the controls you mentioned, I [got]
found several records, for which 
I am especially grateful
I am returning tomorrow to the Archive
by Express the Master Geographical
list, and I shant breathe
freely until I hear it is 
safely in your hands.

"Well, we're still losing the war.  We haven't won a battle, and it
doesn't look good for quite a while to come.  We thought we were safe
out here in Kansas.  I read some place that we are number seven in
States that would be most apt to be bombed.  Just look at the war industry
we have here, and those long range bombers could reach us
plenty easy, too.
[*56   8-3 Topeka, Kan*]

July 30, 1941

Dear Mrs. Tart:

I think the recording trip will get started sometime 
between September 1 and September 15.  Father seems 
to want to bring the machine to Alabama to teach you 
the ropes.  I will let you know more about this 
shortly.

Sincerely yours,

Alan Lomax.

Mrs. Ruby P. Tart
Livingston
Alabama

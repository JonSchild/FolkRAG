428 Midland Bank Building,
Minneapolis, Minnesota
August 19, 1942

To: Special Service Division,
Bureau of Intelligence, Office of War Information

From: Mrs. Winifred Ferguson,
Regional Supervisor,
Division of Social Protection

Subject: War Rumors

I heard no war rumors during the week of August 10-15,
though I eavesdropped right and left. There were plenty of
comments on the war, expressions of opinion, and references
to newspaper articles and radio announcements, but no rumors.

Mrs. Winifred Ferguson
Mrs. Winifred Ferguson

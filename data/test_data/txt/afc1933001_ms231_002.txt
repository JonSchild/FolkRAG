JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS

9/1/1940.

Dear Alan:  Shirley and the children
and Bess came out to Eagle Nest
and spent three happy days with us.
As the three of us, Bess, Miss T. and I, were
driving in to Dallas from Lubbock
word came that Miss Teurise's brother
had died suddenly in Canyon, just
out of Amarillo, if you remember.
Back the three of us traveled over
that (mostly) parched and drouth-
stricken [?] to spend three days
amid the trappings of mourning,
listening to the memories that custom
still imposes on the process
of putting away a good man to his
eternal sleep.  For the crucial

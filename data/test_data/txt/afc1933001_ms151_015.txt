November 20, 1939
Mr. Sheldon Dick
67 West 46 Street
New York City
Dear Mr. Dick:
Your letter reached me just as I was going
out of town for a few days. I will be able to give
it my full attention when I return on the 22nd. In
the mean time, thank you for letter and the enclosures.
Sincerely yours,
Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk Song
Library of Congress

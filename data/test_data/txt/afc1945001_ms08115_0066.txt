College Sophomore Negro Female

1. The slang - Slap the Jap. 

2. The men in service have a better time than
Civilian men because of the comfort, food and 
other things they get. 

3. Join the Navy to Free the world instead 
of seeing it. 

4. Always remember the American Way Is the 
Democratic Way - Roosevelt.

5. Most people predict about when the War is 
suppose to end as in the next year or the
next two yrs but they don't know exactly.

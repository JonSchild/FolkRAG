About three weeks ago a lady called 
a butcher and asked him could he send
her a steak. He said, "Yes." So she fainted.

A soldier was playing golf and was
not doing very well. He turned to his
caddies and asked, "Can you tell me
what's wrong?" The caddies answered,
"Soldier, you just ain't got Rhythm.

A soldier wrote a letter home to his mother.
He was telling her about his maneuvers near 
a town that had signs, "Read nigger and run,
if you can't read run anyhow."

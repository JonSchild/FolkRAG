EXECUTIVE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT
OFFICE FOR EMERGENCY MANAGEMENT
              OFFICE MEMORANDUM


To:               Messrs. Swords and Eugene Katz
                                                                                           Date: March 22, 1942
From:          R. Keith Kane, Assistant Director

Subject:

            Why not suggest to the Bureau of Operations and Bureau of Liaison
that the press and radio can insert here and there the sentence, "Don't 
gossip. Ask 'How do you know?'"

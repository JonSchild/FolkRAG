Now I left my mama in the courthouse cryin',
Sayin', "Lawd, Lawd, have mercy on this boy of mine".

Mama, if I'm not home next summer, well, don't look for me,
I will be dead and buried under this old peckerwood tree.

Mama, I don't want to be buried in these old convict clothes,
Because I want to be ready, Lawd, Lawd, when old Gabriel
                                                                                              blows.

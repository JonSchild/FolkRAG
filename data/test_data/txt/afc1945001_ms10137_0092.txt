1. Two soldiers were laying in a fox hole in England.  
All of a sudden a loud noise suddenly 
burst the extreme silence. 
One of the soldiers remarked "was that 
a bomb or [a] more thunder."  His friend 
said "It was a bomb what did you think 
it was."  First Soldier answered, Thank 
goodness "I thought it was more rain."

2. First Farmer: Did you hear that old man 
                            Browns hired help left.
Second Farmer: Why?
First Farmer: He heard that farm labor 
                        was going to be frozen.

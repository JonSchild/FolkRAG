[*Records to come 
soon*]

Marion Va
Sept.28, 1942

Music Division
Library of Congress
Washington DC.

Dear Sir
some time ago I rec. a contract
to sign for whitch I was to rec.
(2 two) records, Pretty Polly
+ Jimmy Jinkins + $15_00
I have rec. the money
but haven't yet rec. the records.
I was to rec. two copies
of each
Would you please mail the
records as early as possible
Respectfully yours
Mr + Mrs E.C. Ball
My old address is Rugby Va

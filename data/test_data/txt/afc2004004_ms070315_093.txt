I'm in the Well
Set a chair in the ring. Girl
takes it. Director stands behind
her -
G : I'm in the well
L. How deep?
G - 50 feet (any (?))
(If she wants a big juicy kiss
she says 500 feet)
L. What takes to get you out?
G - Sweet kiss
L - Who from
G - (Names the one she
wants to kiss)
[The person has to come + kiss
the one in the chair.]

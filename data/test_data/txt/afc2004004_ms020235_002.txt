Bessie Jones Tape 21
Interview by Antoinette Lomax on October 25, 1961 in New York
City. This is a 3 3/4 IPS tape on one side only. The last section
of the tape is blank, and there are some blank and bad spots in
the last transcribed section. All dialogue. Transcribed by
Dena Hirsch.
3 JOKES: The country girl who feared thunder & lightening
               The country girl who got her maidenhead sewn on
               (True story) The man who urinated against the house

WILLARD: (apparently a sequel) in Millen
CARDELL: in Millen and then in Waynesboro

Transcribed and reviewed by volunteers participating in the By The People project at crowd.loc.gov.

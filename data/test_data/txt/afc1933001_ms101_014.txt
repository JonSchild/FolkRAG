Dear Mr. Emerich : The Folk-Song Archive
should have a machine present to get the 
best of this square dance Hullabaloo at
Dallas on the 29" of this month. I figure
you might get 100 fine records. All
the time you should have a machine
available at the University here.

Yesterday I received from 
the Library the batch of records I made
for the Oklahoma copy and also his
batch which i assume you wish me
to forward on to him. They are very
good examples of the out-of-doors singing
of an ordinary cowboy, with some
exceptions. No one told me that this
shipment was coming. What do you
wish me to do?

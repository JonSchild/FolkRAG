LABOR 
This was handled by Mr. William T. Miller, President of the U.E.A.W. Union at the Allison airplane motors plant here. This plant, the largest manufacturers of airplane motors in the world, is working 100 per cent on war contracts. Mr. Miller maintains two offices --- one in the Personnel Office at the plant and one in union Headquarters downtown. He comes in contact with scores of employees daily.

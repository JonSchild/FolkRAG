General Delivery,
Houston, Texas,
4/14/1939.

Dear Mr. Spivocke:
I'm very glad to say
the machine is working satisfactorily,
though as yet I have
not found any sapphire cutting
needles. No mention of them
in the memorandum of supplies.
The new set of tubes, cables recovered
and general overhauling
is resulting in fairly good recordings.

I told Alan that the [[Garnut?]] 
matter was in your hands

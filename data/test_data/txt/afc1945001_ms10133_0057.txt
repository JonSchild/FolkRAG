I

Joe: "Why are the Russians called "Reds"?
John: "I don't know Joe, why are they called "Reds"?
Joe: "Because they're up to their necks in blood.
John: "Now I know why they call Japs the little brown men."

II

Lets remember Pearl harbor and keep 'em dying [(ToJo only)] (For ToJo and Co.)

III

Bill seems upset as a result of [his] the loss of his wallet.
He puts the following add in the paper. Lost: a wallet
containing $100. Finder keep money but return the lump
of sugar.

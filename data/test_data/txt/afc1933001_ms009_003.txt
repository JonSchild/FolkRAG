October 3, 1933

W. M. Mooney,Postmaster
U. S. Post Office
Washington, D. C.

Dear Sir:
In reference to the enclosed card:
Mr. John A. Lomax is at present in 
New York and would it be possible to have 
this C.O.D. package forwarded to him at 
the following address:

c/o H. S. Latham
Macmillans
60 Fifth Avenue
New York City

Yours truly,

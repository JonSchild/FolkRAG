                                              October 14, 1940

Mr. Burton Paulu
General Extension Division
Minn, Minnesota

Dear Mr. Paulu:

               I'd be delighted to speak on your informal
interview program on Thursday November 7. I suggest
that you contact me immediately after the lecture in the
morning so that we can arrange the details. 

                                                         Sincerely yours, 


                                                              Alan Lomax
                                                              Assistant in Charge
                                                              Archive of American Folk Song 
                                                              Library of Congress

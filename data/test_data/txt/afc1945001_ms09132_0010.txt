Freshman N. M.

1. "Conditions won't be better for the Negro after
this war. Look at the many race problems
he faced again after the last world
war for Democracy."

2. "America is the only home for the Negro.
He should cooperate one hundred percent
with the war effort."

3. "The Negro is discriminated against even in
the armed forces of the country. If I can't
fight on the same bases as the whites,
I don't fight."

4. "The Negro race should show its loyalty to
the country by taking part in the war effort."

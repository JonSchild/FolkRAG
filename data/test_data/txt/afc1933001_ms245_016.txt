JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
11/18/1941

Dear Alan: As I wired you, I
renewed with Columbia for $250.00
a year's use of all my songs, save
Our Singing Country, on their noncommercial
programs. I felt
sure that you [know] recall the details
of this program inasmuch as
we had discussed it. My guess
is that Columbia will pay
$100 for using Our Singing Country
songs. You have entire liberty
to settle for whatever you can
get. As I see the general situation
whatever they pay is a
pick-up. Otherwise they'll go
ahead an use whatever they like
and pay nothing at all - just
as most outfits have done and

Little sleep closed my eyelids that
night - but at break of day
my mind was made up, and in
the forenoon I started for the 
place where O'Neel was to meet
me, if I consented to the
scheme.
{meets militia - ask passport}
"What! none? Then Miss, we must
just tak' ye afore our officer". And
so I was walked off in charge, and
confronted with - my stepfather! but
not until the next morning, for
the savages kept me locked up in the
guard-room all night.
--------- {gets passport and clothes or
goes to meet the Prince}.

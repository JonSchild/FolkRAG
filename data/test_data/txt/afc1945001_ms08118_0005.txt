Jokes

"Yesterday I felt like having a big,
round, juicy steak, a strong cup of
coffee, a delicious salad, and all of the
trimmings, again," said a gentleman to
his friend one day.
"You mean to tell me that you
had that for dinner, lately?"
"No, I felt like that last week."

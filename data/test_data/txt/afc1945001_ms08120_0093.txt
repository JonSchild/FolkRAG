George Wilson
English

Why is there rationing or wars? 
On account of three men rat
ratso: ( The heel they called HITLER)
fatso ( The one that hoards the 
fats and lards)
Japso: ( The four eyed flat footed 
knock needed dog)
Those are the three heels
which will make trouble 
all of the time and if we
all pick in the only [kind??] 
of they will get from us allied 
is the steel from our shoes ( if 
we have any)

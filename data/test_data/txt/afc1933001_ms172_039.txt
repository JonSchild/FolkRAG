LIBRARY OF CONGRESS

OFFICE OF THE LIBRARIAN         WASHINGTON

July 23, 1941


To Whom It May Concern:
     The bearer of this letter, Mr. Alan Lomax,
is Assistant in Charge of the Archive of American Folk
Song in the Library of Congress. He will be engaged
between July 24 and August 15 on a field trip in connection
with the preparation of a documentary radio program
for the Library of Congress and in the collection
of folk songs for the Archive of American Folk Song in 
the Library of Congress.  His work will be done in 
collaboration with Fisk University. Any help which can be given
him and any courtesies which may be extended to him will
be very much appreciated.

Faithfully yours,
Archibald MacLeish
Archibald MacLeish
The Librarian of Congress

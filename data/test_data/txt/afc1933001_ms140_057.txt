Bessemer, Mich.
Dec. 12 - 38

Alan Lomax
Library of Congress
Washington, D. C.

Dear Sir:

If possible send me a duplicate record
of the one I played for you, I would like to have it
for a souvenir. The name I gave was, "on the plains the sun
is shine-ing."

Wishing you a merry xmas, and a happy new year.

Sincerely,
John Shawbitz

add. R. F. D. Bx. 129-A
Bessemer, Mich.

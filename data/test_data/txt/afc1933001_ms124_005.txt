Music Division
August 3, 1937

h.brarian (AAFS)
Lomax moving pictures

I should like to recommend that the moving pictures (6 1/2 reels, 325
feet) made by Mr. Alan Lomax for the Library of Congress on his recent trip
to Haiti, be sent to the Eastman Kodak Co. Store in Washington for editing
and splicing. 

Acting Chief, Division of Music

ep

Jokes

The efficiency of the assembly 
line is truly amazing. The army
physical exams are being speeded
up. One doctor looks in one ear,
another doctor look in the other.
If they can see each other they
reject the man.

There was once a military homing
pigeon who was flying leisurely
to its destination with a report
from the front, it was stopped by
another pigeon who landed. "Get
a move on, I've got a denial of the
report."

Rumor:

a detail of eight enlisted men
were to move the post library at
there camp. The men finished
carried out there assignment, but
left one book. It's title was,
"You Cant Take it With You."

Fresh. N. M.

1. I am a negro democracy's step-child, what do I have to fight.

2. Once I heard a discussion on a lynching that took place sometime ago. In the particular instance[?] - nation a premature negro mother was lynched and the fetus was cut from her stomach. One in the conversation remarked "and they call it democracy."

3. To kill a jap I'd be a sap, He and I are brothers and friends. 

4.  Rickenbacker "is a skunk", its' o.k.

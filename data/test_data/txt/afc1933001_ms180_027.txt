The Library of Congress
WASHINGTON
DIVISION OF MUSIC

Date March 18, 1942
I. Mr. and Mrs. E.C. Ball
of Rugby, Virginia
do hereby in consideration of a payment of $15.00 grant to
the Recording Laboratory of the Music Division of the Library
of Congress permission to make and sell copies by pressing or
to duplicate by any other process without further payment to
me the following musical performance (s):
Jennie Jenkins
Pretty Polly
performed by myself (us) and recorded on (Date) October 1937
by John A. Lomax.
Sign here    Signed E.C. Ball
                     Date: 3/25/42

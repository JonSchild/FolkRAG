Seattle, Washington
August 5, 1942

Lumber mill owner said:

Kaiser by his build-up and ballyho is able to get steel and other
critical materials when others making tanks or ships are out of luck.

Kaiser has sometimes 150 carloads of steel plate on the sidings--most
of it rusty from standing out a long time, while other shops are either shut
down or running part-time for lack of steel.  22

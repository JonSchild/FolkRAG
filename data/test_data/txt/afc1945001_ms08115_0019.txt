College Sophomore - Negro - Female
1. Our Young Men are being trained to
do the best job of killing that the
world has ever seen.
2. Uncle Sam ain't no woman but
he sho can take your man.
3. That young man beside you might
be in Japan or Berlin next year
this time.
4. A young lady told a young man in
civilian clothes that his clothes
were out of style and also the
wrong color. Meaning that he should
have on a uniform.
5. Even Our Presidents Initials have
taken on a new meaning; F for Freedom
D for Democracy and R for Right.

Transcribed and reviewed by contributors participating in the By The People project at crowd.loc.gov.

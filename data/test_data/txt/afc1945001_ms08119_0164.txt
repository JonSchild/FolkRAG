Howard Smith
A
Joke
Two naval officers toiling in a War Production
Board never spoke to each other, just watched
each other. One quit daily at 4:00 p.m., the other
6:00 p.m. or later. One day the harder worker
asked the other "How come your always through at
four o'clock"?  "Well," replies the other, "when 
ever I come to a hard detail, I mail it, "Refer to
Commander Smith." I figure there ought to be a 
Commander Smith in a large out fit like this. 
I must be right because they never come back." 
The other one began taking off his coat. "Brother
prepare for action, I'm Commander Smith.

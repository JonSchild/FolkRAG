The ladies separate and soon Mrs. Jones meets Mrs. Murphey.
"Good morning, Mrs. Murphy. Where are you going?"
"I just heard from Mrs. Brown that clothes rationing starts tonight and I was going to Sears to buy material to make some dresses for the girls. Its the least I can do to win the war."
"Well I'll come along."

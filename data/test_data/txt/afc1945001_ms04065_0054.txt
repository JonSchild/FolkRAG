II. Class Conflict.

"We're losing this war because Labor is ruling the Government. I 
believe in organized labor all right, but I don't believe in these
fellows worth $50 to $100 a month up there telling the real backbone
of American Democracy what to do." (Business Men, Birmingham, 8/7/42.) [*1*]

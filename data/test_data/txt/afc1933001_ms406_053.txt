One of the first stanzas of
[the endless ballad of] the Old Chisholm 
Trail runs as follows:
"Oh a ten dollar hoss
and a forty dollar saddle,
And I'm goin' to punching
the long horn cattle"

of Texas it is true that a 
good saddle cost more than
a sound pony.  [In those
times] Many mustang

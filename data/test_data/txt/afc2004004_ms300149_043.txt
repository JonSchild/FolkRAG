Oct 14, 19.61
Mrs. Alberto Ramsey
Rout 4 Box 217 Simon
Island Ga
Dear Mr Lomox I want
to write you the day I
got my little check that
I got for singing with Mrs.
Bessie Smith. Witch was $15
Mr Lomox do you all think
that was right at all
I dont no but I am
ask you. you no that I 
have sing till I could
not sing iny more you
have to get some body
to take my place. you

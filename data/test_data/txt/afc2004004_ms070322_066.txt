Thursday -
Scene at breakfast.
Coffee shop across from Castle Hotel.
We approach the sallow waitress
"Could we have breakfast?"
"We don't serve breakfast
except by special arrangement, sir"
"Well, we have a couple of eggs
in the car and we -
"We have eggs, too. But we
don't ordinarially cook them unless
it's ordered in advance."
"But all we want is boiled -"
"Very well, I'll speak to
the proprietor."
"Do you suppose we could have
fruit juice."
A pained expression - "Why no we
wouldn't have fruit juice."
Then, heartily trying to cover
up the fauxpas - some breakfast

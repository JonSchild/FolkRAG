State: Montana
City: Helena
Reported by: Field Representative

(October 9, 1942)

Told to Field Representative at a Club Meeting:

It wont be long before we wont be able to get any more coffee or tea.  
One of my neighbor's relatives in Massachusetts wrote her to please send them 
some coffee and tea as it was absolutely impossible to buy it anymore.

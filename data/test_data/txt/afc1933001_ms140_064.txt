December 20, 1938

Mr. John Shawbitz
R.F.D. Box 129-A
Bessemer, Michigan

Dear Mr. Shawbitz:

The Archive does not have the
facilities at this time to supply
duplicates to the general public,
but in view of your co-operation
in our work to Michigan, I shall
try to prepare a record for you
within the next six weeks.

With very best regards, I am

Sincerely yours,


Assistant in charge of
The Archive of American
Folksong

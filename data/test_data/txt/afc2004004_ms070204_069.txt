The Broken Still
There is an old white house 
At Enids hill,
And there's nothing left
there but a broken still,
They've taken him away against
his will
To a cold dark cell in Midland
Mich.
The man that lived there
made more booze
Than any 10 men in B. I. 
But now he's holding the
rock pile down

SHARON (?) Wednesday August 5, 1942.

"A friend just told me she got a letter from her daughter
in Philadelphia that civilians can't get out of Philadelphia by train 
or bus. Anyone buying a ticket for any distant place must prove it's 
a matter of death or sickness. I don't know why!" [*70*]

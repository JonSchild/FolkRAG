18. Rumor columns ought to guard against printing rumors
designed to force the release of information which has
been withheld for military security reasons.

19. As has been previously pointed out, the most dangerous 
rumors to handle are hate-rumors. The technique recommended 
by the Office of War Information is as follows:
print hate-rumors about any group only as part of a 
long list of similar rumors about other groups. For
example:

Current Rumors

"The British are getting rich on the war."
"Big Business is getting rich on the war."
"Labor is getting rich on the war."
"Farmers are getting rich on the war."

January 10, 1941

Mr. H. F. Wetherald
RCA Victor
Camden, New Jersey 

Dear Mr. Wetherald:

Would you have the hill-billy department try and 
locate for me Wade Mainer and his Mountaineers and send me 
their present address as soon as possible. 

Sincerely yours, 

Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk Song
Library of Congress

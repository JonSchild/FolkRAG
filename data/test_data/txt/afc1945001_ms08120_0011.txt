Doris Palmer
March 12, 1943

Ration Book Needed

A man went to the store to buy
some toilet paper. The clerk asked him
if he had his ration book, he said in a
very angry way "No but I got my ass." The
clerk looked at him in a blank way and said
"I guess that will have to do."

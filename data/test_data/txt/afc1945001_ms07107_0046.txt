6. Shreveport, La., August 7, 1942.
"Many colored boys just prior to coming up before the examining board
are having doctors give them a shot of something to make them appear mentally
unbalanced. Others are resorting to the drinking of some kind of wine so as to
appear woozy when before the examining board."  [*79*]

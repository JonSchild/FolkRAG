December 13, 1938
Dear Alan,
Thanks for your very
kind letter. I've spent the
whole week considering
whether or not I really
want to return to the
Archive. But the lure of
New York is strong within
me -- and with the first
big-city offer, I'd be strongly
tempted to leave you very
unceremoniously and go dashing
off to New York. I know 
you agree with me that
it would be quite unfair
to use Washington merely  as a 
stop-gap.
So I shall immediately
begin my barrage of letters
to New York -- and if I'm
fortunate enough to come

November 15, 1940

Mrs. Billy Altizer
201 Princeton Circle
Roanoke, Virginia

Dear Mrs. Altizer:

Your letter came too late for me to reply. I have
been on the road for the last two weeks. Thank you for the
kind invitation. I am sorry that I could not come.

Sincerely yours,

Alan Lomax
Assistant in CHarge
Archive of American Folk Song
Library of Congress

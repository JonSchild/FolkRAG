Shortages
1.  In the very near future it will be impossible to buy a steak in 
a restaurant.  (Young man seeking employment.  10/5/42)
2.  Clothing is going to be rationed about the first of the year. 
(Woman clerk in business office.  10/6/42)
3.  I am going to buy some new shoes because one of the clerks in 
the store says that shoes will be rationed by December 1.  He 
said that leather for soles of our shoes is now being used for the 
tops of the soldiers shoes.  (Working wife of veteran now back 
in the service.  10/8/42)

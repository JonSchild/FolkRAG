A                     Joke
       Their were two little boys playing
all of a sudden one of the boys
whose name was Billy asked his
pal Sidney this question
Billy- Sidney do you know why
Hitler is afraid of the dentist?
Sidney- No Billy, why is  Hitler afraid
of the dentist?
Billy- Because he is afraid of the
yanks.

Jesse was shot while he
was wiping the dust off
the picture of his faithful
horse - first time he had 
taken his [picture] pistols
off in his life - Ford shot
him & he fell with his hand on his gun
Took pretty good in Detroit
- had to play 2 nights
-- Wd pay the people
$1000 when they left in
the morning - Had the
"Life" a book for year -
lent it a couple of summers
a go to a moonshiner from
the South - a hell of a
nice fellow & he had 
to fly and never returned it.

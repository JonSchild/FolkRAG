Shrovetide before Lent -
The marrying season -
of early marriages

Sure, whatever we do we couldn't
be worse off than we are

Sure, the children will be a great
support to us in out ould age.

Not very pretty.  Faith, I'll use [dse?]
her pretty with cows.

The dowery goes over the cliff but the
face [your face] remains on the
women.

- Your wife's only been dead a week -
- Sure, she'll never be any deader.
- He was tormentin me & I married
him to get rid of him

It wasn't for fun or diversion I
married the third time, but to soften
me old cough that torments me in 
the winter.

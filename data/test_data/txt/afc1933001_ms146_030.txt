Music                    Chief Clerk      /////
June 23, 1939

It is recommended that the $500 study-aid grant awarded to Mr. Alan
Lomax by the American Council of Learned Societies for the study of
anthropological and linguistic methods of musicological research at
Columbia University be paid.

Please draw up the necessary vouchers at your earliest convenience.

Chief, Division of Music

JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
3/16/1940

Dear Alan: When I was in Innsbruck,
Germany, I visited the Golden Adlon
Hotel, a quaint old inn with
many private dining rooms, a
great beer hall, old statuary and
paintings against every wall and
clustering in many an alcove - 
I don't know how old was the
building. At the front was a 
long marble slab with the names
and dates of distinguished guests -
[who] Goethe, Bismarck, Wagner,
Heine, and many another. Only
the name of Heine had recently
been chiseled out (not quite, for
I could make out his name). His

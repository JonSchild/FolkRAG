say, "Heil Hitler," they are not saluting
him.  They are showing him how high
the snow is in Russia.

he said, "I am a peaceful man.  All I
want is peace."  My cousin then remarked,
"Yes.  A piece of Poland, piece of Russia,
piece of England, etc., etc."

[Rumors.]

April 8, 1942

Alice Curran                               
                                                                       
Eugene Katz

Rumor study

Rumor hath it that there is in the possession
of the Bureau of Intelligence a rumor study by Douglas
Waples, which I have been searching now for four weeks
and three days. It hasn't turned up in anybody's file.
Do you have any idea about it?

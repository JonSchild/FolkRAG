ELIZABETH STRYKER  6039 PITT STREET  NEW ORLEANS, LOUISIANA
January 5, 1940

Mr. Alan Lomax
c/o Columbia School of the Air
Columbia Broadcasting Station
New York, N.Y.

My dear Mr. Lomax;

Tuesday I heard the program
"Folk Music of America", that
you and your sister gave,
and it was most interesting
and delightful.

You may recall that my
Mother wrote you about a
year ago regarding my
work in old and unpublished
songs of Louisiana with
historical narrations.

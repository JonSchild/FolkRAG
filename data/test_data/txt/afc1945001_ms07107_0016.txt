Norfolk, Va., August 10, 1942
Office worker: "These Negroes are being provoked by enemy aliens who hand out propaganda. It's terrible to get help nowadays. Out at the Beach (Virginia Beach) my friends' maids have all joined the Eleanor Club. They say the aim of the club is to have all white women doing their own work by October first."

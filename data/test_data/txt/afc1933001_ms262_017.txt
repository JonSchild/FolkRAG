7. As you know, valuable material [is] to be had from comparison of
different versions of the same song from different regions.  So
also, are different performances of the same songs in the same
community by different singers, or the same song by different
members of the family or the same song by the same performance
at different times.  Therefore, it is not so important to us
that material be always rehearsed without a mistake or hesitation.
The great beauty of field recordings is that performers
take their own time and do things their own way, feeling that
if they make a mistake, they can try again.  The results are
such that any commercial recording company can ever hope to achieve.

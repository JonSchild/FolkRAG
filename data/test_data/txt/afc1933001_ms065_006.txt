Austin, Tex. 12/7/1939.

Dear Mr. Spivocke:
Recently Bishop Moore 
of Dallas told me that the Creek 
tribe of Indians in Oklahoma 
sang some very interesting 
versions of old fashioned Methodist 
hymns. He would be willing 
to help get recordings of this 
music for the Library. You
might send Alan down
sometime, or, when a machine
is available, I might go up

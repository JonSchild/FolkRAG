October 7, 1942

State: Florida   City: Jacksonville   Reported by: Miss Irma Groover
Place or circumstances in which rumor passed: In my office

Rumor: Miss Groover of this office overheard a discussion in which one man
related to another that a negro, returning to his home unexpectedly,
found a white sailor cohabiting with his wife and shot and killed
him.

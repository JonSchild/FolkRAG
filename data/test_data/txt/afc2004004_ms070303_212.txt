My cows have not been milked
The rest have all milked their cows
My treasured good housekeeper
his under the sod.
Song known all over the highlands
& in the islands & there is
a pipe tune composed to it.

Onich, North Ballachulish
Scotland
Mrs. Catherine MacKellraig
nearly sixty, two children,
brought up in Moidart, went away
to Glasgow to work, met husband
in Fort William  She came from same
district ] married end of 1st world
war, settled in Ft. William, then
gamekeeper till '35, while son was
in N. Afr in war, came and bought
a teashop here in Onich.

October 8, 1942
Mr. and Mrs. E. C. Ball
Marion, Virginia
Dear Mr. and Mrs. Ball:
   The Recording Laboratory has
not yet completed the records for
you. When they are ready they
will be sent to you at once.
Sincerely yours,

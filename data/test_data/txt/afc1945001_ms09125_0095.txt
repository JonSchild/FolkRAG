Army Jokes.

1. One comedian said to another, "Are you
in the draft."  The other one said,
"No, because when the draft hit
me I just pull my coat
together."

2.  A Soldier asked a civilian, "What
class are you in."  The civilian
answered, "B-B."  B-B! asked the
soldier, why what class is that!?"
"B here whenthey leave and B here
when they get back," replied the
civilian.

Richmond, Va., Monday, August 3, 1942
I was told with evidences of fear being expressed that the 
trial of the eight saboteurs was being deliberately prolonged because 
the War Department had certain information that the moment a decision 
was reached there would be immediate reprisals on the eastern seacoast.

War Information
L.  It is being rumored about town that the Yorktown was sunk with a
     loss of 400 men, while the newspapers only said that it was put out of
     commission. (Mining engineer with son in Ireland, member of most
     elite social group, has been quite wealthy but present income quite
     reduced, general lack of confidence in war information and lack of
     agreement with policy of holding back war  news - Phoenix 8-3-42) [*2*]

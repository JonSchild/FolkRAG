Sept. 25, 1939



Mr. Paul G. Brewster
University of Missouri
Columbia, Missouri

Dear Paul:

I will try to arrange for transcription
of the folktale version of 
the Two Sisters sometime this fall.
I will also make a search in recent
library acquisitions for possible
variants of the ballad which I had 
not  before found.

With very good wishes, I am

Sincerely yours,


Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge of the 
Archive of American Folksong

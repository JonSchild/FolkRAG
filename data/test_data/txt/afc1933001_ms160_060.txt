August 30, 1940
Mr. Hal Davis
Columbia Broadcasting Corp.
1473 Barnum Ave,
Bridgeport, Conn.
Dear Mr. Davis:
Thank you for sending the Album of Chain Gang Songs
to the Archive. Joshua White did a very fine job in this
album and the acoustic characteristics of the records are
really quite remarkable.
With very best regards, I am,
Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk Songs
Library of Congress

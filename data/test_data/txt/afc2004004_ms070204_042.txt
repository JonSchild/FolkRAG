Given By Henry Potter
(77) of St. Ignace. Unable to sing because of bad cold.
9 long yrs thank G 't isn't 10
With m brogs on m' fet & m' shovel
in my hand
Says I to myself it's a pretty 
poor to see
Such a dashing yg blade
dig, turf by the day.
Mush-a-gling-gling,
gling-other,
say gling, gling-ay

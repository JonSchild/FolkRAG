August 7, 1942
Dearborn, Michigan [7a] Heard from an employee of the
Social Security Board

It is rumored that hundred of teachers and professors are now working
in war industries in and around Detroit and they do not anticipate
returning to their old jobs this fall due to the high wages that they
are now receiving. [204]

August 6, 1942
Detroit, Michigan  Heard from an employee of the 
Social Security Board

It is rumored that hundreds of Federal employees are leaving their
present positions in Detroit to take positions in the war industries.
[204]

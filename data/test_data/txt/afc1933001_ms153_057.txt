Coulterville, Ill.
January 30, 1940.

Allan Lomax,
C. B. S. Broadcasting Co.,
New York, New York.
Dear Sir:
After listening to Aunt Molly Jackson
on your program, and the announcement
afterward. I am sending you the name of
my Grandmother who sings exactly the way
Aunt Molly does. She lived in the southern
part of the United States about
twenty-five (25) years ago. In Tennessee
and Kentucky. Her name is:

Mr. Joseph Roy,
732 Piggott Ave.,
East St. Louis, Illinois.

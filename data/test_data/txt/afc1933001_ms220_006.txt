Dear Alan : You may forget any
resolve, inclination, plan, device,
determination, etc. you may have
formed for answering my letters,
if you will, instead, write
a friendly letter to your friend
J. M. Winterbotham, Galveston, Tex.
(no street address necessary). Tell
him of your works, plans for
the future or anything else
whatever that comes to your
mind.

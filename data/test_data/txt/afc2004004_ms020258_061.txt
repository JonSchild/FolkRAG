Senoia Ga
R2. Box 48
9-30
Dear Mable
this Leave All Well and hope 
when these fine Lines reach you
they Will fine you OK Mable I
hope you Ant Made With Me
for not Answer your Letter it Bee
Night When I Leave home and it
Bee Night When I Come Back
And I Dont have time to Do 
Nothing I work in Day and Wash
at Night I have 5 Kids goining
to school And I get Up at 
3.30 Every Morning and
Cook And Get them ready for
school And I Leave home at
5 oclock in the Morning
they have Albert Lock up he
Got 12 Month I have Been

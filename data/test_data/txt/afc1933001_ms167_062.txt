THE WHITE HOUSE
WASHINGTON

February 25, 1941.

My dear Mr. Lomax:
Thank you very much for your kind letter of the twenty-fourth. Your performance was a grand success and I was most happy to be able to assist you in arranging for it.
A copy of the list of persons who participated in the program on the evening of February seventeenth is enclosed.
Sincerely yours,
H.G. Crim
Chief Usher.

Mr. Alan Lomax
The Library of Congress

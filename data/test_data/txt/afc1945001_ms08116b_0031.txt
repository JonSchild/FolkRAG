College-senior Negro Female.

1. This war has eliminated many of the Mr. Jone's & Miss Ann's. 
2. We all will met with the same measure of justice before Worlds War no 2 is over. 
3. The White race has stoped saying you people in refuring to the colored race but we people must win this war. 
4. If I only knew Uncle Sam was going to Spain the Farmers to produce food a year ago I would have admitted I too was a good farm hand. 
5. Why do we want more babis hare when we are eating rationed food, by they time the arrive we will all be starved.

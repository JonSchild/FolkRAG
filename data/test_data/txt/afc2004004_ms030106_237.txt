The captain stalled around behind the bar and then dashed to the
place where the liquor was hidden.  When he didn't find it, he raised
hell.  Asked everybody where they lived and why they came to a place that
was liable to get raided any time.
Then Anita rolled up her sleeve and went down in the pail and pulled
up the whiskey and told JR what Frenchy had done.  Instead of JR quitting,
he got mad.  He was threatened.  John Taylor, the toughest guy in the
city came down to protect JR.  He had beat up the Chief of Police.
Business was getting bad and Anita wanted to quit.  Anita left town
without saying anything about it to get JR out of it.  Went to Seattle and
begged JR to come ther too.  Threatened to go to Alaska and so JR wired her
that her mother was sick and to wait.  Then JR caught the next train to
Seattle, left the place just as it was and found Anita.  Anita said," I
didn't want to go to Alaska, just wanted to get you here."
Ed. Montgomery of New Orleans had become a big gambler.  JR had
started losing money.  Anita didn't care just so JR was around.  Before 
he got down to the last penny, Will Bowman asked JR to come to Vancover
and work in a cabaret.
Padio, a trombone player, living in Oakland-xxxxxx - got him.  If he
heard a tune, he'd make all kinds of snakes in it nobody ever heard before. 
So he could catch an idea of how to surround those chords.  Dead.  Never
went East. Played in all the good bands.  Wasn't dark, he was black.  No
mixed bands, mainly because no white boys than that would play.  A few
white bands from the east.

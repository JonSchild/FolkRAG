San Francisco
August 8, 1942
[*74*]

Subject: Stoppage of Construction Work

Henry J. Kaiser Company has stopped all construction jobs
in the Bay Area because of shortage of steel and has moved
construction workers to his shipyards.
Such a transfer doesn't make sense if there is a shortage of
steel. [*E2222*]

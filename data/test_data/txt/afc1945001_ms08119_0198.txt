SWANSON, CLARICE
MARCH 12, 1943                 (a)

   Two weeks ago my girl friend heard a
rumor about clothes being rationed
so she went down town and
bought four dresses and 4 slips.
Last week a lady was saying that
meat would be rationed within
48 hrs.

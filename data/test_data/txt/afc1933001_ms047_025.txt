Dear Mr. Spivacke: 
The recording machine 
and accessories, and blank records, 
the moving picture machine, 
etc. go out today by express
1. Alan will need more 
steel cutting needles as only 
half dozen are left. 
2. The machine is locked 
To unlock it take out two 
screws from a plate at left 
front of the machine. 
Unscrew the screw underneath 
as far as it will go. Open
the throttle and turn on the

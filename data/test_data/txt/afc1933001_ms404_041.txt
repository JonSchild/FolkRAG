1940
Billy Goat Latin
or
Billy Goat Language

At Livingston, Alabama, we recorded 
from a couple of Negro
boys a queer, explosive, consternation
of sounds which came 
mainly through the noses of 
the performers. These boys called
it billy goat Latin. Each later
told me he also could make 
these sounds.  "[I] You use it in de 
field when you be off to yosef"
He was too timid to give
me a sample before the 
microphone. Many years ago an
English instructor at the University

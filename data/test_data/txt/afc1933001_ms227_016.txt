John A. Lomax

5/18/1940

Dear Alan:

Johnny has sent me a chapter 
for my Adventures which he based on 
his and your Log, a most charming 
and interesting piece of 
writing.  He has made extensive 
quotations from your contribution.
It'll be an outstanding chapter of 
the book.  I keep forgetting how
beautifully and masterfully you 
could set out your impressions,
not only for a boy of seventeen
but for any writer at any time.

K. Rumor No. 11 was heard in Tulsa, Oklahoma, August 6, 1942.
1. "MacArthur can't whip the Japs without men and materials, and
them damn politicians and desk generals in Washington are so
interested in their own political careers they'll never send
him enough equipment where he is to do any good or appoint him
in the top Allied command, for if they did he would overshadow
them." [*(19)*]

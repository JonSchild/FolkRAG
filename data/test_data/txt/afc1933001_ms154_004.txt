[*AL:JK
2/1/40*]

Mr Allen Lomack
Dear Sir
We used to play a game
called Here Come a Doodly
Riding.
You choose sides evenly
one set comes walking up to the
others singing the set walking
up we will call No 1 the one who
answer me will call No 2
So here goes
No 1 Here Comes a Doodly Riding
Ransom transom cincinamon
tea No 2 what are you
riding here Far R. T. C. tu.
No 1 we are all riding here
to get married R. T. C. tu No 2
Oh you are all too dirty & gooey
over

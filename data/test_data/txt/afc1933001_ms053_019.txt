Port Aransas, Texas,
12/13/1938.

Dear Mr. Spinacke:  The enclosed
letter came to me when I was
in the hospital from a skinned-up
infected leg, caused in a fight between
me and a big fish. You'd
better answer it, anyhow. What I
once wrote to Miss Dinsmore
I cannot now recall. You may,
however, be sure that I made no
promises, nor did I involve the
Library in any policies.

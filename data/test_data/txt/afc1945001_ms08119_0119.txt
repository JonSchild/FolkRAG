A
M.C. Linner-Brown

Joke

Ben Theurer of Harrisburg, Pa. told
his son, Dick, a private in the Marine
Corps, to write frequently even if he
had no news to tell.
Mr. Theurer shouldn't have been
surprised when he recived a letter with 
the following:
Dear Dad:
Affectionately yours,
Dick.
P.S. Boy, am I tired!

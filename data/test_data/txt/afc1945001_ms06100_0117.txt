"I don't know why all these folks are traveling on 
buses -- if I had a car I'd sure use it -- before the 
government took it away from me. I'd use those tires 
myself -- rather than spend all my time trying to get 
some place on a bus."     [*30*] 
(Bus Station, Clovis, N. M., August 6, 1942)

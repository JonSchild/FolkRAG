Buffalo

Indians on Sharps rifle - [shootfor] shooting for - shoots today and kills tomorrow.

"The herd was six or seven days crossing the Arkansas."

For seven miles from the Arkansas river back to the foothills was a living mass of buffalo, pressing in countless thousands upon each other.  Migration of millions of prime animals - no young or old - from Indian Territory to the Hudson Bay basin where winter killed many.

"For 5 days we had ridden through [camp] and camped in a mobile sea of living buffalo."

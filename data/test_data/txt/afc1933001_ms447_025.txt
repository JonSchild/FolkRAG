WHISTLE, DAUGHTER, WHISTLE

"Whistle, daughter, whistle,
And I'll give you a sheep."
"Mother, I'm asleep."

"Whistle, daughter, whistle,
And I'll give you a cow."
"Mother, I don't know how."

"Whistle, daughter, whistle,
And I'll give you a man."
"Mother, now I can!

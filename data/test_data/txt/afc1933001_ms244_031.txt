JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
10/28/1941

Dear Alan:

1. Mr. Spivacke's authorization,
dated Oct. 21, came today. As you
will see from the enclosed envelope
[same] he still thinks I live in
Austin. Kindly get my address
changed with his office.

2. I am writing to Mrs.
Tartt so as to fit in with her plans
and to find out when she can go
to work. I feel sure that she
has nothing to advance on her
expenses; but I'll do that for
her. You've done a humane
and most worthy service
in giving her this chance. I'll
do my best to see that neither

13.
So he barred the door so neat an' snug,
An', "Let us go to bed,
For a pain that do lay in my breast
I can no longer rest, boys, rest,
I can no longer rest."
So he took a stick an' beat her so,
Till she was wonderful sore;
"Oh, forbear, forbear," she cried, "husband dear,
For I'll never do so no more, boys, more,
I'll never do so no more."
"For if you do I will make you rue,
An' curse the hour were born,
For deceiving of your husband dear,
I will make you wear the horn, boys, horn,
I will make you wear the horn."
So come all (tape runs out)

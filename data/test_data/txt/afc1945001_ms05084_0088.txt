3. Officials of the Anaconda Copper Mining Company and Butte
and Anaconda Selective Service Boards held a meeting. The officials
informed the draft boards that more deferments will have to be made for
miners and smeltermen, because so many have been inducted, others left
for the coast and defense industries, thus crippling production.

Born in Cavendish, Vt.--been in
state most of the time, 22 yrs in
this town--works carpenter,
cutting wood, peeling logs, pulp,
used to make maple sugar + 
send it to England but the
Spanish war broke that up,
farmed it a good deal--

Went on the air when the local
station opened--as the Skitchawang
Hill Billies--were never
paid, although they were used as advertizing
 filler-3 fiddlers, 2
guitars, a banjo, piano, 3
harmonicas--played mostly 
old time music--1933 or 4

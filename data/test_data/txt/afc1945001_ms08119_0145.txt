A
Rumor

On a submarine there was a couple of 
sailors bombing our enemies. They were 
all brothers, while they were fighting 
under sea one of the brothers collapsed. Just 
then all the brothers did not know what was 
the matter with him. Fortunately one of them 
knew how to take the place of the doctor

Cat Island
Old Bight
Dec-9th-42

Mr. Allan Lomax

Dear Sir your letter was
received by me and I was very glad and thankful
for wat you send I give you many and many
thanks I am well for the present time and I do
hope that you are well also give my love to Miss
Elizabeth
truly yours Elizabeth Austin

Everybody Goes
A man with no arms was drafted into the Army. He said to the Lieutenant, "There's nothing I can do. I have no arms."
The Lieutenant looked around and saw a blind man pumping water for the fighting soldiers and asked, "Do you see that man pumping water?"
"Yes", replied the man with no arms.
"Well, he is blind. You can go over there and tell him when the pail is full.

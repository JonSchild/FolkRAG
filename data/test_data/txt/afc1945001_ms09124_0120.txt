Rumors
I've heard that, with the coming of warmer
 weather, Chicago and other big industry
 cities are liable to be gassed by the 
enemy.
While coming home from work the other
 day, I heard a lady tell her companion that
 almost the entire strawberry crop of America 
this year was to be sent to England.

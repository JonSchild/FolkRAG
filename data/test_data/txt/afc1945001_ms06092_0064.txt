Employee of Telephone Company:  A lady employee of the Bell
Telephone Company was quite positive that the Federal Government is about
to take over operation of this organization.  She is very much alarmed,
fearing that she will lose seniority privileges gathered through some years
of employment.  [*Jacksonville, Florida  4   8-3-42*]

All supplies are here.
Thanks for your fine promptness

Some special items

1. A lot of cowboy songs with
genuine cowboy singers.

2. A group of Spanish narrative
ballads with local
setting; e.g. The Purple Bull

3. Some fine Mexican play
tunes and lullabies.

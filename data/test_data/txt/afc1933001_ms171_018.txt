June 27, 1941

Mr. Carleton Sprague Smith
The New York Public Library
Fifth Avenue & 42nd Street
New York City

Dear Carleton:

I still would like to know what you think about a project
to record foreign folk songs in New York? How is the School of
the Air?

With best regards,

Alan Lomax

AL-eh

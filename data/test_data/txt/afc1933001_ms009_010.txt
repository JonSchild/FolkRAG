October 14, 1933

John A. Lomax, Esq.
160 Pleasant Street
Rumford, R.I.

My dear Mr. Lomax:

Many thanks for your note
of the 12th. I expect to be in
New York on the 14th and 15th.

Faithfully yours,

Chief, Division of Music

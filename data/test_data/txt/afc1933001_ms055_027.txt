WIER LONG LEAF LUMBER COMPANY
WIERGATE, TEXAS

February 27th. 1939.
Mr. John A. Lomax,
Port Aranses, Texas.
Dear Sir:-

Replying to yours of February 23rd. making inquiry
concerning the present location of Henry Trivillion, colored,
wish to advise, that Henry is employed here and a letter addressed
to him at the above named Post Office will reach him.
Yours very truly,
P Ashley
CASHIER.

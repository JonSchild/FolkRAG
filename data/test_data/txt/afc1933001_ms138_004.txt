Music 

Mail
October 3, 1938

Please arrange for these 75 records (all blank) to 
be sent to Mr. Alan Lomax, General Delivery, Ironwood, Michigan. 
They are to go via Railway Express, charges prepaid. Their value 
is $75.00.  Mr. Lomax will pick them up at the Ironwood express 
office.

Chief, Division of Music

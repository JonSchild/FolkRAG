Josephine Adams                              March 17, 1943
Economics

I Mary: Have you paid your income tax? 
Sue: I'd like to open a charge account.

II. The Draft Board says Junior isn't really
4F, But it'll have to do until they fig-
ure out a lower rating!

III. Where do I apply for a job as elevator
starter? I know there's going to be a 
vacancy soon, because my, dad's on 
your draft board.

IV. "Save all your scrap;
To help slap the Japs."

V. "A slip of the lip will sink a ship"

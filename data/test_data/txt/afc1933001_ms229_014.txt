JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
7/29/1940

Dear Alan: After an annoying delay
and following some prodding
by me, Columbia sent me
the $250.00 which I am reserving
for Bess. She is doing a second
year in German in Lubbock. when
very occasionally I hear, that
group reports busy and happy 
days.

I'd like to hear in your
next just where Our Singing 
Country is at.

1. I return for your information
and letters the cards that
were nearest where our road
will probably lead.

over.

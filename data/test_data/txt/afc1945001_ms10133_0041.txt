Junior Negro - Female
1. They are rationing everything it seems. The
soles of my shoes are so thin I can place
my feet on a record and tell you whether
Tommy Dorsey or Jimmy Dorsey is playing.
2. Have you heard of the Man who was examined
and upon examination confessed that he was 
blind. He was rejected by nine physicians. He left
the examining board in a jovial manner, and
attended a show. He looked up and saw one of
the Doctors sitting next to him, so he asked him
is that was the bus heading for New York.
3. With the rationing of tires, gas, food, and shoes
one finds that the best form of entertainment
is to remain at home and play this little
piggy went to market.

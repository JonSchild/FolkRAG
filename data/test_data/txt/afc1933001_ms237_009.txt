JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS  3/30/1941

Dear Alan : I call your attention 
to two features of the enclosed cancelled
check :
1. The "M.H." in the lower left
hand corner.

2. The modest amount written 
into the blank spaces, when the 
writer inner had the signer completely 
in his power up to the amount of 
his bank balance.

Yesterday, five circulars having 
come in by slow mail I 
put in a full day for you - 
visiting the Junior College at 
Arlington, the Masonic Orphans'
Home near Ft Worth, and 

over

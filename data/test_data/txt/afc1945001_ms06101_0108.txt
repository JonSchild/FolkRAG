Social Security Board
Old Age and Survivors Insurance
Austin, Texas
8-5-42
While seated at a coffee counter two shop women were discussing the tire situation and one of them remarked that she had quit driving her to work and was using the bus service. The other one said, "Well, I am still using mine because I have always been accustomed to the use of a car and I'm going to use mine until Uncle Sam says to turn it in for government use."

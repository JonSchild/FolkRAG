2nd R. of 10/12/61, Song 6, p. 2
If You Started for the Kingdom
TL:  Where do you remember that one from?
BJ:  Oh, that's way back home.

record go out. . .Yeah, so much in it.

got anything. . .Jus' sing ov' and ov' again, you know. . .If you ain't got nothin', you jus' go right on ahead with

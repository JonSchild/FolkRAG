MARY NANCY GRAHAM 
544 CHESTNUT STREET, WINNETKA, ILLINOIS

January 16, 1942

Mr. John A. Lomax
7456 San Benito Way
Dallas, Texas

Dear Mr. Lomax:
Thank you for permission to reprint 
"Cowboy's Gettin'-up Holler." I 
shall give full acknowledgment and 
send you a copy of the book when it 
is published. Enclosed you will find 
a check for ten dollars.
Sincerely yours, 
Mary Nancy Graham

[*When I was 15 years old I 
took a load of wheat to a flour 
mill[ed] and slept by the side of 
my mules on a pallet. The 
next morning at daylight as I drank  my
coffee another camper across
the creek from sang this *]

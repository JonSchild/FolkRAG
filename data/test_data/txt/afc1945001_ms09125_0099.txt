Rumors: (comical)
A rumor going around at Fort Dix
That some army men were moving books
To the post library but they lefted one
book. "The title was  You Can't Take it With You."

With America's plane factories
working at top speed, the story is told
that at one of them they built a plane in
a dozen hours. Five minutes later a
pilot took off in it, and six hours
later he called: "Am in Australia.
Please send motor.

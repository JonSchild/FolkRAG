Swanson, Earl

War Stories

A slip of the lip, might sink
a ship.
Loose lips, Sink ships.

I heard a rumor clothes are to
be rationed.

I'd rather be a private with 
a chicken on my lap, than
a major with an eagle on my 
shoulder.

Hitler was riding in his 
car down a street in Berlin, 
when suddenly they ran
over a dog.  Stopping the car
Adolph told his orderly to
go into a nearby tavern and
inform the citizens of the 
dogs death.  Several minutes
passed and the orderly
appeared laden with money
and gifts.  Hitler curiously
asked how he obtained these gifts.

JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS

3/28/1940

Dear Alan:

   Yesterday Miss Terrill
and I took Mrs.  Smith
to a recording shop.  She sang
the song you wanted and I
shipped you the record at a
total expense as per receipt enclosed
of $2.30, not including 20
miles of driving, my time, Miss
Terrill's time, and a good deal
of mental acumen in bolstering
the confidence of a garrulous 
and timid fat lady of uncertain
age.

Thompson, William
March 12, 1943

English VI

A policeman stopped a civilian one nite
and questioned him.
Policeman: "Let me see your card."
Civilian: "What card?"
Policeman: "Registration card, dope."
Civilian: "Which one?"
Pol.: "For the Draft."
Civ.: "Pardon me sir, but I have a coat for the draft."

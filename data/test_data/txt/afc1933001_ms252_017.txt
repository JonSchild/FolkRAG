JOHN A. LOMAX
7456 SAN BENITO WAY
DALLAS, TEXAS
6/18/1942

Dear Alan: I enclose you the
original of martin, the "neither
long and slender bartender" which
I happened on this morning. Unless
you wish personally to own
these records of my ballad
collecting days I [am] plan to
give them to Harvard in memory
of George Lyman Kittredge who
was primarily responsible for
whatever I have done in the
fields of folk song.

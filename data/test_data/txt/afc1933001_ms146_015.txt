June 12, 1939

Mr. E. C. Forman
Recording & Recording Sales
RCA Manufacturing Co. Inc.
Camden, New Jersey

Dr. Mr. Forman:
Mr. Alan Lomax would like to visit
you next Monday and spend two or three
days in learning about your folk song
material.  Will you please tell me if
this will be convenient?
Very sincerely yours,
Harold Spivacke
Chief, Division of Music

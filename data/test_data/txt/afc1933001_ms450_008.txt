Track Caller:
[*Italics*] [Oh, get your back breakin' holts, young men.

Singing Leader and Gang:
Oh, the Valley is a buggy,
And the I.C. is a hoss,
North-Eastern is the captain,
But the L&N is the boss,
But the L&N is the boss.

Track Caller:
[*Italics*] [Now, boys, put yo' guns on yo' shoulders an' get back in the shade.

*For a further description of this operation, see "Tie Shuffling Chant", American Ballads and Folk Songs, Volume I, page 14. The men heave on their lining bars 
on the beats indicated by the accent marks.

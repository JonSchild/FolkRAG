August 18, 1959
TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN
This is to introduce Mr. A.Lomax of New York City.
Mr. Lomax, formerly head of the Folklore Division,
Library of Congress, is making a study of folk music
and is interested in recording authentic folk songs.
He is a friend of the Association and a member.
Any help you can give him in locating authentic sources
and making contacts with persons who may assist him,
will be appreciated.
Sincerely yours,
Gloster B. Current
GLOSTER B. CURRENT
Director of Branches

Mr. R. J. Coar, Manager,
Radioscriptions, Inc.
726 - Iith Street, N.W.
Washington, D. C.

Dear Mr. Coar:

    I want to thank you for writing
me. I shall try to visit your studio
at the earliest opportunity.

    Very truly yours,

    Acting-Chief,
    Division of Music

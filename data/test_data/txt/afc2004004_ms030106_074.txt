73.1

1907--Met Walter Butler from out of Birmingham.  Also met Phillip
Moore and Oscar Curry, a good single-handed entertainer.  When I
returned to New Orleans, I started hanging around the uptown district
near Hattie Rogers' place.  Also met Richard Jones who wrote
tunes.  Not a good piano player.  They thought I was a stranger
and they thought they was bringing me down town.  Met all the big
pool players and gamblers--Buster Brown, Bama, Pensacola Kid,
Morris Morre--all very good cheaters.

Shortly after war was declared a 
very wealthy woman had to part with 
her Japanese servant who had faithfully 
served her for a number of years.

“Well, Togo,” she said, “After 
this war is over, you may come back and 
work for me again.”

“No, no, Missy,” said Togo, “When 
I come back you work for me."

Leo Wind

A

1 Someone has said that we will be getting out of school early so that the boys may be inducted earlier than was originally planned
2 March 15th there will be a show with the largest cast in the world. It will be staged at the Office of the Collector of Internal Revenue, with all proceeds going to Uncle Sam the name of this play is the Great Divide.
3 A sergeant of the guard approached a private who was on guard duty & wished to ask some questions. Sergeant, "If you saw a battleship coming across the field what would you do."
Private, "I'd get a torpedo & sink it." 
Sergeant, "Where would you get the torpedo!"
Private, "Same damn place, you got the battleship.

10-24-61
Mr. Alan Lomax.
Dear Sir.
this is to inform that with apprecations
I received the check few weeks ago.
Mr. Lomax please Sir accept the 
Song poem which you shall find 
enclosed and if you have ample 
time take the poem have it set 
to music. Vocalised, and recorded. 
Im confident, that with correct music, 
a good vocalist, and the proper 
promotions It will be O.K.
May I re-State again thanks to you.
With my best wishes
Please forward my mail.
Bridges, Lee Cole
Route 2 Box 77
c/o Lue Alice Harris
Isola Miss.

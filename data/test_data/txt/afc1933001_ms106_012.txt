Inside of the box [are] gumdrops and
other kinds of candy! Iisn't that
sweet! I got up early Easter morning
and hunted eggs all day. I got fifteen
candy eggs and twelve hen eggs.
I went to Sunday school and afterwards
church with Jimmy and Sueu 
(Short for Dorothy Sue) and liked
it even so much. In the afternoon
the Standerfers took us riding and
we went out to Buffalo Springs. It
is about the lovliest place I ever
saw. It is way down in Canon and
the canon [drus] hill and stone face
kind of things and everything.
There are just a few houses around
and the rest of it is wild. They is
lake full of fish and an island

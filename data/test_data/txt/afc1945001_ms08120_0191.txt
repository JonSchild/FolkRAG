Brown, Charles
Byrne

March 12, 1943
Seventh Period

Rumors

There was a rumor out that Hitler was dead not very long ago. My mother said that there was a paper, that was out it was an extra the paper said that Hitler was very ill. The paper says Hitler doesn’t make any more speeches.
And he is never seen by anyone. Before this was started not many Americans thought we would have a war so soon. But there was a fellow called Uncle Bill who never did work he used to stand on the corner. He said by christmas we’ll be in war. Not of us paid any attention to him because he was a veteran from the last war and he was suppose to be shellshocked. Uncle Bill said that in

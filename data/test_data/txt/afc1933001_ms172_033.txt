July 22, 1941

Mr. Sam Henry
Sandelford, Coleraine
Co. Londonderry
Ireland

Dear Mr. Henry:
     The last series of songs has
been received, Nos. 101-246. The
songs will be strongly bound and of
constant use in our folk-song work.
     Again, many thanks for this
invaluable acquisition.

Cordially yours,

Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge of the 
Archive of American Folksong

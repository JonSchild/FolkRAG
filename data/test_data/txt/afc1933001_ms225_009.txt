Also your point of the $300 advance
I made to Mrs. Seeger. I wouldn't
wish you ever to think that I had
drawn a hard expeditious bargain
with you, - or Elizabeth or anyone
else. For the moment I feel in
the humour to allow the proposition
to [swal] "jell" a bit longer.
Once you agreed with me that,
since you were connected with the
Library, it would best for me to
make all the trades, for the use
of any of our material in
any way. You could tell
inquirers that all the copyrights
and ownership are carried by
me. People don't mind to use wires,
telephones or air mail letters; so

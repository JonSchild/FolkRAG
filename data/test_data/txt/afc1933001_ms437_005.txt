THIS AGREEMENT made on the 20th day of OCTOBER,
1937, by and between HUDDIE LEDBETTER, JOHN A. LOMAX,
ALAN LOMAX, and the MACMILLAN COMPANY, a New York corporation
having its office at New York, N.Y.,

W I T N E S S E T H :

WHEREAS John A. Lomax, Alan Lomax and the Macmillan 
Company are the authors and publisher respectively of 
a book entitled "Negro Folk Songs as Sung by Lead Belly",
and the Macmillan Company is the exclusive owner of the
copyright of all the songs and other material contained in
the said book, and has exclusive ownership and right of
publication of all such songs and material; and

WHEREAS Ledbetter now wishes to sing certain of
such songs and use other material in the said book in
radio braodcasts, motion pictures, or in personal public
performances, which would be in violation of the said
copyright but for the consent thereto of The Macmillan
Company; and

WHEREAS Ledbetter has asserted sundry unfounded
and frivolous claims against John A. Lomax, Alan Lomax and
The Macmillan Company;

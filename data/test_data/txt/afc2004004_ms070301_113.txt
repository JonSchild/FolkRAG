Pat, the Limerick Rake, comes to 
visit the island of Irish bally. 
He flirts with the Airy Girl and
she falls in love with him even tho 
she is married already to old Tigue, 
who is rich + powerful because 
he has the stone.

Tigue, lives with his old sister Lizzie
and she looks after the house +
the baby because the Airy Girl
is so often away gallivanting.

First scene - Airy Girl + Tigue - Pat
Came Over the Hill.  She runs out
into the night.

2nd scene - She meets Pat.  Love scene with
songs.  He says he will leave her
+ she tells him about the stone +
agrees to steal it.

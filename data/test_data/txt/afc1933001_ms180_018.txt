March 16, 1942

Mr. Frank Mlakar, Asst. Editor
Common Ground
222 Fourth Avenue
New York, N. Y.

Dear Mr. Mlakar:
           My father sent me your letter
this morning and I wish you would send me a 
copy of the issue in which Woody Guthrie's
article "Ear Players" appeared with a note
about the rate you are able to pay for articles
in your publication.

Sincerely yours,


Alan Lomax
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk song

Roosevelt in Cheyenne, Wyoming when I had about finished collecting
material for Cowboy Songs resulted
in a fine boost for the book published
later in the year. He wrote an Introduction
that has been many times quoted. His hearty
words of commendation proved an offset
to the sneers of some critics who [had]

product of our country.

1.  Keep 'im flying, 
Keep 'im rolling, 
Keep 'im eating. 

2.  Striking matches, aid the axis. 

3.  Get in your scrap and lick the Jap. 

4.  We got plenty of nothing. 

5.  If you pay a dollar, don't be a tale-taler. 

6.  Bootlegging shoes, will make us lose. 

7.  We are going to starve now. 

8.  A Loose lip will sink a ship. 

9.  Their places are more important to go, so 
economize on rubber and gas. 

10.  The war is over -- all over the world.

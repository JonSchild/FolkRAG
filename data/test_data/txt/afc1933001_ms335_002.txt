from Rubie Lee Woods, May 1933, Austin, Texas

By'em by, goin' see de king,
     (repeat twice)
I wouldn' mine dyin' ef dyin was all.
Afteh death we's goin' stand de tes'
     (repeat twice)
I wouldn' min' dyin' ef dyini was all.
Jordan river so chilly an col,
     (repeat twice)
I wouldn' min' dyin ef dyin' was all.

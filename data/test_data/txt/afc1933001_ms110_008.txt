Seguin, Box 135, Texas
10/4/40
Dear Father:
     Uncle Allie wrote out the enclosed memo of [?] for you to meet at the division headquarters of the Mo. Poc. at the depot in Little Rock. They have no knowledge of ballads but would get you cooperation, etc. if you'd need that in contacting the section gang at Wynne. My Atlanta girl friend writes that she is working on some negro contacts to substitute for the Alabama

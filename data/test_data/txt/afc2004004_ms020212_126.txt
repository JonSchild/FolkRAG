New York City
October 24, 1962.

To whom it may concern:
     It is hereby agreed between Alan Lomax and Antoinette Lomax
that she receive in payment for her work in interviewing 
Bessie Jones, one third of all monies due to Alan Lomax from book
use of the materials collected about the life of Bessie Jones, and
that these royalties amount to not less than one sixth of the
total royaltiew on the book.  It is furthermore agred that
her name, if she wished, appear on the cover and the title page
of the book.
Alan Lomax

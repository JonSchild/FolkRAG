"Whan that my swete son was xxxti wynter old,
Than the traytor Judas waxed very bold;
For xxxti platis of money, his master he had sold;
But when I wist, lod my hart was cold."X
O, my hart is wo! "Mary, she sayd so,
"For to se my dere son dye; and sonanes haue I no mo."

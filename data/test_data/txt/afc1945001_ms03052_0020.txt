Austin, Texas
8-15-42

It is being circulated in Liberty Hill, Texas, that, while the
President and his wife are good people, and all that, they associate
with a very "fast" set of people, who have interests in making fortunes
out of the war, and who distract Mr. and Mrs. Roosevelt from doing
their best for the American people.

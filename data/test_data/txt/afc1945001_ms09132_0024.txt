F.N.F

I   I have heard the remark made that
the negro will be [just as well] better 
off if the Japanese would win 
this war as if the United Nations
would. The reason is that the 
Japanese would be kinder to 
them than the U.N. would because
we are of the darker races.  And 
the Japanese feel that the darker
races should rule the earth.

II   The English are not worth 
having as allies [aff] after the way 
the turned on the French. This refers
particularly to the bombing of the 
French Navy.

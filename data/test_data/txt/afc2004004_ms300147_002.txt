2/25/59

W.D. Stewart
Washington County Jail
Greenville Miss

Mr. Alan Lomax

Dear Mr. Lomax I recived
your letter of A few day ago
And I was to glad to here
from you.  And was sorry that 
I was unable for to call you
Back.  As you Asked me to do
on the letter.  listen Mr. Lomax
get a good understanding of what
I am telling you.  I Come down
in Mississippi. And I had Some
trouble, But it is not to Bad 
I only have a fine now and
I am doing it in jail in fact
I am a trustie, and my fine 
would Will Be up the 9th of June

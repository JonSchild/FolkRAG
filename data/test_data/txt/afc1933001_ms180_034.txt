The Fort Valley State College
Fort Valley, Georgia

March 21, 1942

Mr. Alan Lomax
Library of Congress
Washington, D. C.

Dear Mr. Lomax:
     I am very sorry you were unable to come and 
witness our Annual Folk Festival, but I am sure that
we will be able to work out a very valuable sound
trip for you in the State of Georgia.

With best wishes, I am

Sincerely yours,
{{singed}} H. M. Bond.
H. M. Bond,
President.

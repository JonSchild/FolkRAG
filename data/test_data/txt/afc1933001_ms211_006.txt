Port Aransas, Texas
1/6/1939.

Dear Alan:

When I do come to Washington
to help finish the book
I ought to come by Clemson and
record the songs mentioned by
Ben Robertson of the enclosed letter.
Tell Mr. Spinacke so. Also I should
visit again the Livingston, Ala., Negroes
and the remarkable white Florida
cracker woman. Talk it over and
let me know.

I now guess that your postal
card offer of help referred to
the subject of Mrs. Latham's letter I

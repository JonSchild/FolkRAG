Jane Thomas
March 12, 1943                                                                                 

A man from Gualdecanal was telling a friend of mine that while the soldiers were waiting for the Japs to attack they drank hair tonic. Concerning Rationing: many people say that clothes rationing is very near and that the government is telling the people that there will be no clothes rationing because they are going to start rationing on Sunday like they did

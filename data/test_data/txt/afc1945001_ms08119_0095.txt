Joke

One cold December morning on the Russian Front two German
infantry-men, while repeatadly dodging Russian shells struck up a 
conversation about pease. Says the first German, "Well soon dis nightmare 
vill be offer, der Fuhrer hass asked for peace." "Ya"! exclaims 
the excited second German, "I would neffer belive it." "Ya
says the first German, a piece of England, a piece of Russia und a
peice of der United States.

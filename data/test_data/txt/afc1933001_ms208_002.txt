Mr. John A. Lomax,

Washington, D. C. 
Dear Mr. Lomax:
The Department has under consideration an application 
for registration of Alan Lomax who has
given your name as a reference.
In this connection it will be appreciated if 
you will call at Room 151, Department of State
Building, 17th and Pennsylvania Avenue, Washington,
D. C., at your earliest convenience bringing with 
you some person who can testify that you are the
person you represent yourself to be or in lieu 
thereof documentary means of identification.
Sincerely yours, 
RB Shipley
R.B. Shipley, 
Chief, Passport Division

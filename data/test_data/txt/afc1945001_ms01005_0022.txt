Office Memorandum
Office of Government Reports
Date: 9/1/42
To: Eugene Katz
from William H. Webber [?] WHW
Subject: Eleanor Clubs

Enclosed are copies of special reports
on the existence of Eleanor clubs in
Alabama, Florida and Virginia. Field
Representatives in the three States have
discovered no evidence of the existence
of such organizations.

(?] Horowitz

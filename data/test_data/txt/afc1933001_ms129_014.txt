Division of Music
January 21, 1938

For use in connection with the work of the Archive of American
Folk-Song, it is respectfully recommended that one gross of B.C.N.
playback needles (cost $8.40) be purchased from Droop's Music Store,
Washington, D.C., to be paid from the balance ($23.80) remaining
in the grant of the American Council of Learned Societies, "for expenses
incurred in the collection of materials for research in American
Folk-Songs".

Acting Chief, Division of Music

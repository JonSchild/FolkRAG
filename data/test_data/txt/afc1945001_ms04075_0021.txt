WARREN, OHIO

August 6, 1942
"At one of our local defense corporation plants, it is necessary 
for the A. F. of L. to permit a C.I.O member to sit on the job doing 
nothing but getting paid more per day than the A. F. of L. member who is 
operating the crane."    [*Warren, Ohio         (18)*]

On December 9th 1941 Japan bombed
Pearl Harbor, ever since then America
has been willing and ready to give
up anything to beat those Japs. 
There have scrap drives in which
the collection of iron tin and copper
has taken place. all of these materials
are necessary in wining the war.
There have been bond and stamp
drives to buy shells, guns, bombs
and other vital war materials
that are essential in wining the
war.
Come on America keep em rolling,
keep em flying, keep democracy from
dying.

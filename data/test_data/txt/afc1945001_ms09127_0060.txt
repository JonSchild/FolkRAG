Dorothy Sepper 
Eng - 1A - 9th per. 
337 - Zlatnik 

March 12, 1943 
Knutger - 139 

[*A*] 
A rumor: 
The boys in the service are
receiving only one bar of candy
a week and very little gum. 
Rumor: 
The merchant seaman would
not unload supplies at Guadalcanal 
throwing the burden on the tired and
sick Marines.

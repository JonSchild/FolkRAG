Basseterre, St. Kitts—
Irishtown—W.  of the
Civaes. Fort Thomas Road.
A narrow back yard—back of
a tailor shop. Machine on
a cutting table. A bass
drum from Anguilla—a
homemade [x] drop (called
bittle) a fife man with 
an iron fife. When
I said one-two-three
he began with a tune of
that title. This is
the old Xmas music foolishly
being replaced by steel
band. Mar??? come out
on [Xmas day] Boxing Day

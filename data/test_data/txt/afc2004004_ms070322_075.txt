Friday - Too fine. I had a hard
time getting out. Robin said she
would be cowardly to let me. I
said I couldn't stand it. Suicide
on going to bed. Murder getting
up. Three more days & then goodbye.
And if I don't stick to
my intention I really may 
die. My heart aches - I feel
now she has lost all respect
for me.
Somehow we made peace &
got out to lunch by 11:30.
St. Ives where the lady with 
the cats. Grey stone streets uncoiling
from a crystal shaped harbor,
floored with clean yellow sand.
Blue & black fishing boats leaning
upright at low tide - a crisc
cross of great red anchor
chains. Out beyond the

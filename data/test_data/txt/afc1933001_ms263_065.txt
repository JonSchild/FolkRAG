Number 1

One Morning in May, sung by Mrs. Texas Gladden of Salem, Virginia. A variant of an Irish ballad known as The Unfortunate Rake, which has produced many versions in America, among them The St.James Infirmary Blues, The Dying Cowboy, and this present song, The Unfortunate Girl. Mrs. Gladden is considered one of the finest Virginia mountain ballad singers.

Special Service Division - 8/15/42
Minneapolis, Minnesota, August 14.--Mr. E stated that the present
steel shortage is not actual but is due to hoarding and unwillingness of
the steel manufacturers to fully cooperate with the ceiling prices.
Minneapolis, Minnesota, August 14. --Miss F laments the fact that
our war effort is being side-tracked for political manipulations before
elections. She said our all-out war effort will not become truly effective
until after the November elections.
S.T. Lillehaugen
Sr. Specialist on School Facilities,
U.S. Office of Education

12B - MALE
White

I - At a recently built camp in Louisiana there have been many fires. Those fires have destroyed several barracks and a recreation hall.

II - I have heard many rumors that Hitler has been dead since 1940 and that Goebbels and Goering are really running Nazi Germany.

III - I heard a rumor that a man who is said to have predicted the World War II and several other events has predicted the war to be over by Sept. 1943

IV - That the United States will never redeem the Savings Bonds.

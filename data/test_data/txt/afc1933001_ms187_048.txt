Oh be unto the wars
the hour they first
begun.

They have taken
from our Country
a many a clever
man.

They have robbed us
of our sweethearts
the joy of our
lives.

And their bodies
fed the wild fouls
on the banks of
the nile.

We crossed the
rugged waters
we floted across

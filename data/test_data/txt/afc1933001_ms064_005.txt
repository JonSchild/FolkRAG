Rugby Va
Nov. 20 1939

Music Division
Lybrary of Congress
Washington D C.
Mr John A Lomax
Dear Sir.
On Oct 22 & 23 - 1927
we recorded several songs
for you at Galax Va.
which you Promised to 
send us a copy of them.
We have requested this
Several times as we and 
our Friends would like 
to have them. Sincerely
yours Mr & Mrs E. C. Ball

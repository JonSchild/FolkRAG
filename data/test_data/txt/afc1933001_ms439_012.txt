                          Match Box Blues--P.G. Blues

The blues is comin' down like a shower of rain---

He went in the other room where he could hear his wife 
hummin' this little tune--(Guitar)

What I mean he had his mind concentrated---

She thought she had all his money, but she was mistaken. And yet
unstill she had him pretty bent.

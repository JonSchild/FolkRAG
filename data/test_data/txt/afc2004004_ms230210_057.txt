8 MARIE GALANTE (ibid)

Cric-crac (a phrase or expletive used at the end of
songs or stories at wqkes)
Rinto Galante, marie galante gallant
It dit rinto galant, marie galanto gallant e volez
Le dix canoe kai nancio volez
Le dix canoe kai island nancio volez.

It dit canard petit canard canard (zo zay ce une oiseau)
Canard petit canard zo zay canard zo zay

Rinto gallante, marie galante gallant
He said [sid] rinto galant, marie galanto gallant fly
The ten canoes will nancio fly
He sai duck,little duck, duck bone egg[s]
Duck, little duck, bone, egg, duck, bone egg.

(This song is sung at wakes . . an informant Mr.
Paul said he knows this song from his boyhood
and the word "gallant" is applied to a young flirtatious
girl.)

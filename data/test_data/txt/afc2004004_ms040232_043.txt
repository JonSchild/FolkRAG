The Castle
Edinburgh
Oct 23rd 1951

Dear Mr. Lomax
Than you for your most
interesting letter.  and I am 
so glad you had a happy
time doing your recordings
The recordings will I am
sure be of great interest to
everybody.  Wishing you very
best of luck.
Yours Sincerely
W Ross

-14- MICHIGAN
Many signs were held aloft by individuals, however, bearing
the words "Open It NOW."
Mother Bloor (Ella Reeve Bloor) here to talk second front, told
newspapers Britain and the United States were letting Russia down, said [*4*]
civilization would lose 1,000 years if we're defeated.
Sentiment among most people seems to leave second-front
decision up to Government as having best knowledge on feasibility, [*4*]
although a half dozen sources have said to us: "It couldn't be, could
it, that vested interests would prefer to see Russia die than to see her
conquer and spread her Communism?"

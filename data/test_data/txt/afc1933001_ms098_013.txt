July 14,1943

Mr. John Lomax
7456 San Benito Way,
Dallas, Texas

Dear Mr. Lomax:

    We take pleasure in sending you a copy
of 75 Years of Freedom, Commemoration of the
75th Anniversary of the Proclamation of the
13th Amendment to the Constitution of the
United States. The comments accompanying the
programs of folk songs should be of special
interest.

    Sincerely yours,

    B.A. Botkin
    Assistant in Charge
    Archive of American Folk Song

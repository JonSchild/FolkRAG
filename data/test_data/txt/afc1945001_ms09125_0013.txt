Gwendolyn Greene                             March 12, 1943
English 1 - 7th period                          Marquart - 232


Be careful of what you say
A sunken ship a'int hay
So keep your mouth clamp shut
For we will win - we must

The cause of many cities' fate
Was that of rumors small and great
The cities soon in panic fled
But many now are lying dead.

the Spring of '65.
I thought myself quiet lucky to
find my self alive.
I girted up my horses my
business to pursue +
Instead of hauling 4 loads
I only hauled but 2.
I got so drunk at Harpers
station I couldn't haul no
more.

ever was known before

money [?] spending free[e] when

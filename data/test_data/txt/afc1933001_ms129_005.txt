January 7, 1938

Mr. Lincoln Thompson  AAFS
Sound Specialties Company
31 Beckley Avenue
Stamford, Connecticut

Dear Mr. Thompson:

Dr. Spivacke will not be in Washington on
the 17th, but I should be delighted to see you
again and see a demonstration of your new recorder
Sincerely,

Assistant-1ndcharge
Archive of American Folk Song.

November 16, 1943

Mr. Fletcher Collins
Fairchild Aircraft Corp.
Bulington, N. C.

Dear Fletcher:
I hear that you were in town overnight and am
very sorry that you did not get in touch with me.
Don't forget to let me know when you come through here
again.
With best wishes,

Sincerely yours,

B. A. Botkin
Assistant in Charge
Archive of American Folk Song

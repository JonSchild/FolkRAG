August 15, 1942.

Inflation is inevitable. It can't be stopped with as many cracks in it
as the present wage policy permits. (Overheard)

Negroes are more defiant. You can see it on the buses and everywhere,
and there certainly is a difference in the attitude of servants, They
are so impudent and don't care whether they hold a job or not. (Housewife)

Knoxville, August 13.
Ku Klus Klan is being rejuvenated to prevent negro workers from getting
same wages as white workers on the same job. (CIO Labor leader)

people will not tolerate negroes on skilled jobs and getting same wages

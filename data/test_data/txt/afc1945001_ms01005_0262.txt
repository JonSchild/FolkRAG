Dr. Leo Rosten, Deputy of the Director                 October 30, 1942 

Eugene Katz, Special Services Division

Miscellaneous Mail reporting Rumors

Arrangements have been made with Miss Harriet Root to
answer all miscellaneous mail reporting rumors and to relay the mail
to Special Services Division for analysis.

Also, Miss Root has agreed to send to us for reply all communications 
concerning rumor clinics and rumor analysis.


cc to Mr. Horowitz

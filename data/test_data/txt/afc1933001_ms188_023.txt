SPELMAN COLLEGE 
ATLANTA, GEORGIA 
Nov, 16 1942 

Dear Mr. Lomax- 
It has occurred to me that 
I did not say in my letter, that 
I own a car. Yes, I do. It is
a 1937 Plymouth in fair shape. 
I would need to have it worked 
over somewhat and equipped with 
new tires if possible in order to 
carry out my program 
Sincerely, 
W. L. James

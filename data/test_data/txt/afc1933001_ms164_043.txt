LIBRARY OF CONGRESS
WASHINGTON

Division of Music

December 16, 1940
Memorandum to Dr. Spivacke

Dear Dr. Spivacke:

Do you expect me to play records in Cleveland? If 
so, how?  I understand that Miss Bush will need a machine too.

What about transportation?

Lovingly,
Alan Lomax

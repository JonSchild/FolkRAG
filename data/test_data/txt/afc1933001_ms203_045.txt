HOTEL WALTON
BROAD STREET AT LOCUST
PHILADELPHIA

JOSEPH F. McARDLE
MANAGER

May 27th

Dear Alan:
Enclosed you will
find Kay Anderson's card - the
one I was given to deliver
to you way last March.
Be sure to read the note 
on the back.
I had such fun talking
to you last Saturday. Your
work is so vitaly interesting
to me & so worthwhile. Your
sister is a most interesting
and charming person!
Thank you again for
giving me of your time.
Sincerely,
Betty Burnett

DEPARTMENT OF STATE
WASHINGTON

September 10, 1940

My dear Mr. Lomax:

In accordance with a request received from Dr. William Berrien, I am returning to you herewith your manuscript entitled "List of American Folk Songs on commercial Records" which has been embodied in the report of the committee on the Conference of Inter-American Relations in the Field of Music, a complete copy of which will reach you shortly under separate cover.

Sincerely yours,

Charles A. Thomson

Charles A. Thomson
Chief
Division of Cultural Relations 

Enclosure:

Manuscript, returned.

Mr. Alan Lomax,
Archive of American Folk Songs,
Library of Congress
Washington, D. C.

22a Clifton Hill
St. John's Wood,
N.W.8.

November 21st 1953

P.S. Moore, Esq.,
41 Parkhill Road,
Sidcup,
Kent.

Dear Mr. Moore,

Thank you for your kind letter. I would like to
go to Yorkshire and wonder if you know any person in the
town of Dent who would be a good local contact?
Meantime aren't there any singers in your own town? Isn't
it the home of the black-faced dancers?

Sincerely yours,

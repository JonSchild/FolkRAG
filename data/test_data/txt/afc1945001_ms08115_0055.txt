College - Sophomore - Negro - Female

1. When I die I want you to bury my body
near a sugar plant or by a rubber tree.

2. Man, did you hear about shoes being
rationing now? No, talking about
these everlasting ones - (feet) I
been doing that all the time.

3. Boy, how long are you going to 
be here? Ans. Long as Uncle
let me.

Fisk 25. 1. [Holiness]
Holiness Church. Testimony by two women.
Precious ones, I'm glad tonight, to be back at the house for prayer.
Say, now I'm glad tonight for everyone. But my soul is glad for Jesus.
I thank the Lord for our precious holy ministers of God and for our
mother's missionaries. Thankful for the most high invisitude. Truly
I'm for everyone. Amen. But I'm glad for Jesus most. For He keeps
me free fomr sin and saved my life. Amen. And have no mind.
Amen. I did things that's wrong. I thank God for picking me up
out of sin and shame. Placing me on this highway to holiness and
put the will and mind in me to work right things, down here in this
sinful world. For men and women, girls and boys here, you can't help
but sin. Amen. Truly now I can report victory over sin and shame.
Amen. Cause He's my keeper each day. Amen. I thank the Lord
tonight. Amen. So He guards my soul with the Holy Ghost and the Son.
I'm praying that He will continue for me. With the Lord take up and
raise and shine and wait. Amen. I want to say I thank the Lord for
my Cathy--he isn't present , but I thank the Lord for him. Amen.
Every day for my pastor. Amen. In Memphis. But I'm glad for him.
Amen. I'm glad tonight. I want to thank God to pray for me, for the
Lord will ever carry me off. Cause truly I means the same thing. For
I know there ain't no going back. Amen. I lift up holy hands to
God and be the humblest of men. Amen. I lift up holy hands
to God and be humble servant of the Lord. Cause these are evil days.
Put my strength in the Lord. Amen.

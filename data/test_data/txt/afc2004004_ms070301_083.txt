A man that is not afraid of
the sea will soon be drowned, for he
will be going out on a day he shouldn't.
[A man] But we do be afeared of the
sea and we do only be drowned
now + again.

A man that is not married
is like an old jackass.  He goes
from one house to another, eating
a bit [x] in this place + in that 
but has no home for himself
like an old jackass straying on
the rocks.

Greatest virtue in woman is that
she be fruitful

Have you got?  It is an agreeable air.

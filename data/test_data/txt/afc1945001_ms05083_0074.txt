RUMORS HEARD IN ATLANTIC CITY, NEW JERSEY, DURING WEEK ENDING
AUGUST 7, 1942

8-1-42  I was informed a soldier raped a girl, 12 years of age. The
soldier was assigned to the Ambassador Hotel, and took the
girl into his room and placed a gas mask over her face.  The
informant did not reveal the name of the girl, but said he
was personally acquainted with her family.  The identity of
the soldier had not been solved. [*Atlantic City, N. J.  70*]

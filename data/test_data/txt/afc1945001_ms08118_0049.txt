Anecdot

William Shirer, the news commentater and author of Berlin 
Diary was in the grocery store at the Connecticut 
village where he lives, and heard a woman ask the 
clerk for a can of dog meat. The clerk told her 
that there was none in stock, because of the government 
order prohibiting the canning of dog food. 
The women [replied] was loud in her complaint. 
"Look Lady, Shirer told her, "Here in 
America we've stopped canning dog-meat because 
we're almost out of tin. But in Germany they 
stopped because they're all out of dogs.

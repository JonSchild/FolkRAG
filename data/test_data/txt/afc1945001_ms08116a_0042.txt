College - Sophomore - Negro - Female

I.  Hilter shall eat breakfast
with the President on Xmas.
II.  This store will close for
Hilter's funeral.
III.  Hilter and another man had
a conflict.  So he decide to make
the Man eat some manure.
The Man wouldn't eat, so he call
his wife & told her Hilter & I 
are having breakfast on manure.  Each
time he would tell Hilter to eat
some more.

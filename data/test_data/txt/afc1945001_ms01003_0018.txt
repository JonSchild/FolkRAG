Andie Knutson

A Bibliography on National Socialist Germany
Compiled from the Public Opinion Quarterly, Volume 4, 1940

Birchall, Frederick T. The Storm Breaks: A Panorama of Europe and the
Forces that have Wrecked Its Peace. New York: Viking, 1940. 366, pp.

Blanco White, Mrs. Amber (Reeves). The New Propaganda. London:
Golanez, 1939. 383 pp.
(Examines from a Freudian point of view the authoritarian propagandas
of recent years.)

Borkensu, Franz. The New German Empire. New York: Viking, 1939. 167 pp.

Dewilde, John Charles; Popper, David H.; and Clark, Eunice. Handbook
of the War. Boston: Houghton, Mifflin, 1939. 248 pp.
(Chapture 13 is on Propaganda.)

Franke, Hermann (Major-General), Editor. Handbuch der neuzeitlichen
Wehrwissenschaft. Berlin: De Gruyter, 1936-1939. 5 Volumes.
(Standard reference manual for the German Army. General Franke and
Dr. Friedrich Bertkan have articles on "Psychological Warefare",
Vol. 1, pp 105-9; "Propaganda", pp 555-6; "Military Propaganda",
pp 710-12. See also "Military Psychology", p. 712, by Dr. Max
Simoneit.

Gumbel, E. J., Editor. Freie Wissenschaft: Ein Sammelbuch aus der
deutschen Emigration. Strassbourg: Sebastian Brandt, 1938. 283 pp.
(Symposium of eminent German scientists now outside Germany.)

Knop, W. G. J., Compiler. Beware the English: German Propaganda
Exposes England, foreword by Stephen King-Hall. London: Hamilton,
1939. 304 pp.

Lengyel, Emil. The Danube. New York: Random House, 1939. 482 pp.
(History, legend, folklore, politics.)

Loewenstein, Karl. Hitler's Germany: Nazi Background to War.
New York: Mcmillan, 1940. 176 pp.

Lajos, Ivan. Germany's War Chances as Pictured in German Official
Literature. London: Golanez, 1939. 160 pp.

Maddox, William Percy. European Plans for World Order. (James-Rowe-Patten 
Pamphlet series no.8) Philadelphia: American Academy of
Political and Social Science, 194). 44 pp.

Millis, Walter. Why Europe Fights. New York: William Morrow, 1940
222 pp.
(History of the years since the last World War by the author of
"Road to War", 1935.)

McKenzie, Vernon. Here Lies Goebbels! London: Michel Joseph, 1940.
319 pp.

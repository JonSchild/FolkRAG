December 22, 1939

Herbert Robbins 138 Haven Ave
New York City

Dear Herbert:

I will sind your five bucks after New Years
day. I hope you won't feel any differences over this
matter because I've just plain forgotten it. Please
give my best regards to Mary and remember me your friend,

Alan Lomax

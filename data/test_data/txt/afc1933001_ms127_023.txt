Division of Music
November 29, 1937

Pass for Alan Lomax

I recommend the issuance of a pass to Mr. Alan
Lomax, admitting him to the Music Division and the
Southwest Attic during the following hours:
Saturday 1:00 to 10:00 P. M.
Wedsdays 4:30 to 10:00 P. M.
Sundays and Holidays 2:00 to10:00 P. M.

Acting Chief, Division of Music

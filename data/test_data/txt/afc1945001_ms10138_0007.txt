Sophomore  White  Female

Did you hear about the Jap who thought 
the Chamber of Commerce had a handle
on it?

Japs are sneaks
But we will beat,
Because we got America for keeps.

First soldier: can you describe Italy?
Second soldier: no, except it is shaped
like a boot and run by a heel.

First lady: my hens won't give me
any eggs.
Second lady: Is that so?
First lady: Yes, I have to bring back a
empty shell before they will
give me another egg.

June 11, 1943 Dr. willis James The Fort Valley State College Fort Valley, Georgia
Dear Dr. James:
Under separate cover I am sending you two needles: Please return the others immediately
for resharpening. I trust you will have no trouble with them. Let me know what your
recording plans are.
With best wishes,
Faithfully B. A. Botkin Assistant in Charge Archive of American Folk Song
P.S. What type of recording machine are you using? Does it have an adjustable head?

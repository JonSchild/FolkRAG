BUILDING JACKSONVILLE? FLORIDA
615 W. Ashley Street June 21 1937
Dr. Carita Doggett Corse State Director Federal Writers' Project Roberts Building Local
Dear Dr. Corse:
I am submitting herewith a partial list of songs which may be heard and recorded at the
Clara White Mission:
“Another Day's Journey”
“When he call me, I Will Answer”
“A Charge”
“When He Calls Me”
“If You Miss Me”
“Plumb de line”
“Some Sweet Day”
“Alleluia”

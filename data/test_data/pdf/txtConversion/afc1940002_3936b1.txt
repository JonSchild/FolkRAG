La última cena (The Last Supper) [Audio]
Textual Transcription
Con mansedumbre y ternuray señas de un fino amor,les previene a sus discípulosla
última cena el Señor.Y con mucha caridad,que en los mortales no ves,después ya de
haber cenado,les lava humilde los pies.Luego consagró su cuerpo y con cariñoso afán,y
les dio escondidoentre accidentes de pan.
English Translation
With docility and tendernessand signs of a fine love,he prepares for his disciplesthe last
supper of the Lord.And with much charity,that in mortals you do not see,after having
eaten,he humbly washes their feet.Later he consecrated his bodyand with loving zeal,he
gave it to them hiddenin the morsels of bread.

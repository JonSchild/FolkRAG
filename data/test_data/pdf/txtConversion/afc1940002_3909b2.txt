Textual Transcription
Ayudemos, almas,en tanto penar,a la Virgen purade la Soledad.Al pie de la cruzla vemos
que está la Madre sin Hijo porque ha muerto ya.Se aumenta su penade ver a Jesús,que
no hay quien lo bajede la Santa Cruz.Crece su dolor,pues no halló un sepulcropara
sepultara su Hijo difunto.Tanta es su pobreza,que no hay un sudariopara recibirsu cuerpo
sagrado.Tres necesidadestiene esta Señora,pero Dios le envíaquien se la socorra.
English Translation
Let us help, souls,in such sufferingthe pure Virgin of Solitude. At the foot of the crosswe
see the Motheris without her Son because he has died.Her pain grows seeing Jesus, for
there is no one to lower himfrom the Holy Cross.Her pain grows,she can find no tomb to
inter her departed Son. Such is her poverty,that here is no shroudto receive his holy body.
Three needs has this Lady, but God has sent someone to help her.

El columpio [Textual Transcription]
Record 8. A2 “El Columpio” (“The Swing”) sung by Bienldes Remond Isern, Key West,
Florida, San Carlos Institute, January 22, 1940.
El COLUMPIO
Spanish:
Come me gusta montar en columpio Que suavecito es: Subir tan alto, sentir el fresso,
Para bajar despries. Desde el columpio ye veo les campos Los pajares volar Veo las
vacas, las avejitas, Que vienen a pastar.
THE SWING
English:
How I love to get in a swing How easy it is! Clumb (climb) so high, feel the breeze, To
come down afterwards, From the swing I see the fields The birds flying, I see the cows, the
little lambs That come to graze.

Queda en paz (Be at Peace) [Audio]
Textual Transcription
Queda en paz, aurora,ponga su clavel,adiós que Tubal te viene a ofrecer,adiós que
Tubalte viene a ofrecer.
English Translation
Be at peace, dawn, put out your carnation,goodbye because Tubalcomes to make an
offering,goodbye because Tubalcomes to make an offering.

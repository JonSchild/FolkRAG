Dear Bess:
 
May I thank you for the excellent course in Beginning Guitar I
 
have had the pleasure of taking under your instruction.  Every
 
session has been both educational and pleasureable.
 
For the past two Mondays I have been unable to attend because of
 
the necessity of attending Board of Education meetings in my
 
school district.  The entire curriculum is being reviewed in an
 
attempt to cut expenses and in order to protect the music program
 
it is essential I attend these meetings.
 
I have no way of knowing how long it will be necessary for me to
 
continue this work and for that reason I have decided to post-
pone my second semester of guitar for at least one semester.  I
 
hope to resume in the fall of 1962 and perhaps by then I shall
 
have had the time to consolidate the gains you have given me in
 
guitar.
 
I am very sorry to have to interrupt a study which has been ex-
tremely enjoyable to me.  However, I have also just taken on a
 
church choir directing job and to change to another time would
 
at this time be more than I can handle.
 
Again, thank you for your kindness, your wonderful teaching
 
ability and the hours of pleasure I have had while learning to
 
play the guitar.

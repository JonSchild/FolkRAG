Frank P. Ingram ADMINISTRATOR
W. A. McMullen, Jr. ASST. ADMINISTRATOR
Florida Works Progress Administration (STATE OFFICE) EXCHANGE BUILDING
Jacksonville, Florida
June 22, 1937
5 3
PLEASE ADDRESS REPLY: ATTENTION
Mr. John A. Lomax National Consultant of Folklore Federal Writers' Project 1500 Eye
Street, N. W. Washington, D. C.
My dear Mr. Lomax:
Please let us know the date of your arrival in Jacksonville so that we may have singers
ready here and elsewhere.
We are inclosing copy of letter from our Negro Unit, submitting partial list of Negro songs.
Sincerely yours, Carita Doggett Corse Dr. Carita Doggett Corse State Director Federal
Writers' Project

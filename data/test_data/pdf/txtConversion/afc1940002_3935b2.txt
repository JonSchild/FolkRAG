Textual Transcription
Dulce esposo de mi alma, mi Redentor, Jesucristo,Hijo del divino Padre,Dios eterno
e infinito.Esposo de mis entrañas,dulcísimo amigo mío,a mis ojos más hermosoque
el fresco cárdeno lirio.Dime, esposo de mi alma,tengo de verme contigo,ha de llegar
aquel díade tan grande regocijo.Tengo de ver con mis ojosaquí estos ojos de amigo,veré
risueño el semblantede tu rostro cristalino.
English Translation
Sweet husband of my soul,my Redeemer, Jesus Christ,Son of the divine Father,God
eternal and infinite.Husband of my vitals, sweetest friend of mine,in my eyes more
beautifulthan the fresh red lily.Tell me, husband of my soul,I have to see myself with
you,that day will comeof such great joy.I have to see with my eyeshere these eyes of a
friend,I will see the smiling imageof your crystalline face.

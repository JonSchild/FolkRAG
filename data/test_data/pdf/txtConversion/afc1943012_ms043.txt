July 28, 1943 Dr. Willis Laurence James The Fort Valley State College Fort Valley,
Georgia
Dear Dr. James:
Thank you for your letter of July 15th. As I am going on my vacation the first two weeks
of August, I want to wish you the best of luck at the Festival. I am looking forward to the
results. I am passing on your message to Lewis Jones.
With best wishes,
Sincerely yours, B. A. Botkin Assistant in Charge Archive of American Folk Song

During the play the followling points may be made and scored.
 
FIFTEEN -
 If a player plays a card which makes  he numerisal value
 
exactly fifteen, he scores two points. Eg. a player starts with a
 
jack announcing "ten". The other player lays down a five making
 
fifteen. The latter player announces "fifteen-two", scoring his two
 
points. Any combination of more than two cards totaling fifteen
 
scores two points for the player of the last card.
 
PAIRS -
 When a player plays a card of the same denomination as the last
 
card played, he scores two points.
 
PAIRS ROYAL -

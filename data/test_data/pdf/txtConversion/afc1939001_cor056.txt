A.A.F.S.
Division of Music June 27, 1939
Attached is an approved recommendation authorizing Mr. John Lomax to make a
recording trip for the Archive of American Folk-Song. As will be seen from his expense
account (also attached), Mr. Lomax exceeded the amount authorized by $267.80.
I recommend that this additional sum be authorized.
Chief, Division of Music

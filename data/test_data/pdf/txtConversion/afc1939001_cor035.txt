[Letter from Harold Spivacke to John A. Lomax;
University Station, Austin, TX]
A
May 3, 1939
Mr. John A. Lomax University Station Austin, Texas
Dear Mr. Lomax:
Enclosed is the letter of introduction which you hope will get you into prisons. I hope that it
will also get you out of them.
The small size records have been ordered and should reach you very soon.
Sincerely, Harold Spivacke Chief, Division of Music
HS mr

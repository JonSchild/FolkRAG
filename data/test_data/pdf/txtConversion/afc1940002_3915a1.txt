Textual Transcription
Atención pido a toditos,hoy a este público honrado,para festejar el autode los dichos
esposados.Quiso Dios con su podery Adán con sabiduría,y le sacó una costillay de
ahí formó a la mujer.Piensen bien, pongan gran cuidadoantes de pedir mujer,qué
les vendrá a sucedersi se ponen en estado.Si dejas otros por otra,ella a pegar un
suspiro,Dios te querrá responsableante un tribunal divino.El Señor los ha apuntadocon
su santo matrimonio,procuren de vivir bien,no le den gusto al demonio.El Señor los ha
apuntadocon su mano poderosa,ya sí lo deben de entrarel esposado y su esposa.Al
darles los buenos díasy al saber cómo les va,quedarán estos esposadosy haga Dios su
voluntad.
English Translation
Atention I ask from all,today from this honored public,to celebrate the deedsof those who
were married.God deemed with his powerand Adam with his wisdom,and he removed
a rib and from it formed woman. Think well, take great care before asking the hand of a
woman,what will come to happenif you are in the state of matrimony.If you desert them
for another, she will breathe a great sigh,God will hold you responsiblebefore a divine
tribunal.The Lord has registered youwith his holy matrimony,try to live well, and give
no pleasure to the devil.The Lord has taken note of youwith his powerful hand,now you
will surely enteras husband and wife. By giving you good day and knowing how you are
doing,these two are so joined and God's will be done.

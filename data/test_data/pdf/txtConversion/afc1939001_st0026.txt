ROLY BOLY
I rolly bolly, I rolly bolly, An' dere I trump de horn. I drang him down to de water's edge An'
dere I tuck him in. An' dere I tuck him in, An' dere I tuck him in.
Sung by Mrs. Grace Lungino, Huntville, Texas, April, 1939.

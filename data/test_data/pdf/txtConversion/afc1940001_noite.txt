CHRISTMAS NIGHT On Christmas nightNight of so much joyTraveling along went JosephTraveling
along went Mary.Both together to BethlehemMore by night than by dayAnd when they arrived in
BethlehemAll the people were asleep.St. Joseph went to get fireFor the night was coldAnd in the
night a bright starIllumined the whole world.When St. Joseph returnedHe saw the Virgin MaryWith
the Child Jesus in her armsWhich she enveloped in her mantle.

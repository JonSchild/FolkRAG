English Translation
I believe in God the omnipotent Father,creator of heaven and earth, universal
protectorwhere all goodness lies.I will always believe in Jesus Christ,his only and true
Son, Father of mercy,who the whole world praises. I believe in the Holy Spirit,which is
our only Lord, who was also conceived bythe will of the Eternal Father.We all believe that
fromSaint Mary was born the Verband died crucified to save the universe. I also believe in
the nailsand in his divine crown,and also in the passionwhen he died in the final hour.

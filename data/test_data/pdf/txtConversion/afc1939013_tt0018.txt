Puntos Guajiro [Textual transcription]
RECORD 6
A-2 “Puntos Guajiro” sung by various members of the Sesteto Encanto, Key West, Florida.
En el puente Boca Chica, Sacaron un timaron, Y del buche le sacaron, A Bebito en
camison. (In the point of Boca Chica, They pulled out a shark, And out of its stomach they
pulled, Bobito in a gown.)
Te voy a ser un contrato, Que no te van a dar trabajo, Subir a una mata de mango, Con
la cabeza pa bajo. (I am going to give you a contract, That will not give you work, Climb a
mango tree, With your head hanging down.)

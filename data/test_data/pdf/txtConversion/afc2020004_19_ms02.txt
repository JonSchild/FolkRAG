START OF RECORDING 
 
[Imaikalani Kalahele reads “Maoli”] 
 
Imaikalani Kalahele:  Basically this poem talks about the first people. We as Hawaiians refer to 
ourselves as Maoli. The first people. So it talks about how we as an original people came to one 
place and became the roots of this place.

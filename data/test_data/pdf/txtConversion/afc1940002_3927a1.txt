no se supieraque alguien te amaba, prendita,y esa fue causa, negrita,de que no me

English Translation
Upon taking my leave of youI could not do so sincerely,heaven willed that I returnto give
you satisfaction.I left with my soul rent and my heart pierced, I left with that care I did not
warn you of, my love.My departure was so suddenthat if I could make amends,I would
keep it from herthat someone loved her, darling,and that was cause, sweetheart,for me
not to say goodbye.But it is not like a penitentand I have lost the opportunity,there is the
reason,I am absent from my homelandand now that I see you here,God did not want me
to die,fortune would bring meand he that sees you now said,-Do not deny me your sweet
eyesheaven wanted me to return.

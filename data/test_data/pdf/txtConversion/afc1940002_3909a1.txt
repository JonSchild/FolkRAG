Textual Transcription
Lloren, pecadorescon mucho dolor,la pasión y muertede Nuestro Señor.Cuando lo
prendieron,con beso de paz,lo llevan presoen casa de Anás.Luego lo remiten,bien
asegurado,al rey cruel Herodesque fuera azotado.Por loco lo tienen,falso y cruel ingrato,lo
visten de blancose le envía a Pilatos.Con los tribunaleslo traen con prisionescon caja
y trompetasy falsos pregones."¡Ecce Homo!" le dicenpuesto en el balcón;le leen su
sentencia a voz de pregón.Los judíos pidieronque sea coronado,de manos y pies,que sea
en cruz clavado.
English Translation
Cry, sinnerswith much pain,the passion and deathof Our Lord.When they arrested
him,with the kiss of peace,they take him prisonerto the house of Annas.Then they
turn him over,well secured,to the cruel king Herodto be scourged.They took him for a
crazy manfalse and cruel ingrate,they dress him in whiteand sent him to Pilate.With the
tribunalsthe bring him imprisonedwith drum and trumpetsand false cries."¡Ecce Homo!" the
tell him,displayed on a balcony;they read him his sentencein public pronouncement.The
jews askedthat he be crowned,nailed to a crossby hands and feet.

Textual Transcription
¡Ave María purísima,Ave María poderosa,Ave María prodigiosa,Dulce Ave María
santísima!Siempre de Dios benditísimaeres refulgente aurora,seno con Dios
protectora,dulce Ave María santísima.De aquella sierpe cruelísima,que no quiere
devorar,de ella nos has de librar,dulce Ave María santísima.Pues eres excelentísimay
de gracia tan cabal,líbranos de todo mal,dulce Ave María santísima.Pues eres
excelentísima,Virgen Reina y sin igual,siempre diré sin cesar,dulce Ave María santísima.
English Translation
Hail purest Mary, Hail powerful Mary,Hail prodigious Mary,Hail holiest Mary!Always
most blessed of God you are the resplendent dawn,bosom with God protectress,sweet
holiest Mary.Of that most cruel serpent, who is want to devour,from it you will liberate
us,sweet holiest Mary.Well you are most excellentand of grace so complete,free us from
all evil,sweet holiest Mary.Well you are most excellent,Virgin Queen without equal,I will
always say without ceasing,sweet holiest Mary.

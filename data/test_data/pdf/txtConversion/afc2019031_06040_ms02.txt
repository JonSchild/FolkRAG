Diane Cho Bio from Cho Benn Holback, a Quinn Evans 
Company Website
An architect and long-time advocate for the arts and education, Diane is skilled in overseeing 
multidisciplinary teams to deliver projects that reflect each client’s mission and unique program 
requirements. She combines a strong management discipline with an approach that inspires 
creativity and resourcefulness in the design process. 
Diane’s acclaimed portfolio includes many theater, museum, and education projects, including 
complex adaptive use and renovation initiatives. Her projects have been credited with helping to 
transform neighborhoods, enliven urban districts, and enhance campus environments. 
Diane enjoys the intensity and collaborative nature of the architectural process, much like the 
interaction and lively group dynamics that take place in the artistic and educational settings she 
designs.

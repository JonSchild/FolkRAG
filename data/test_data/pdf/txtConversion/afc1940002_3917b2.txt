Textual Transcription
(Niño. Parte de la relación del niño que va cantada diferente de los otros distritos en que
canta él.)Ay, María, mi querida madre,yo siento el dolor entre ambos,el que a mi padre
Joséal corazón yo he llegadoOh, envidias, serpiente y fea,astuta, atroz y reparo,no tendrá
fin hasta queel mundo sea finado.
English Translation
(Child. Part of the speech of the child which is sung differently from the other districts in
which it is sung.)Oh, Mary, my beloved mother,I feel the pain between you both,that which
to the heart of my father Joseph have I brought.Oh, envy, ugly serpent, astute, atrocious
and faulted,will not be eradicated untilthe world comes to an end.

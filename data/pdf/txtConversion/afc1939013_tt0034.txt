RECORD 15.
(Made in Ybor City, Tampa, Florida, August 26, 1939. Stetson Kennedy, intermediary,
interviewer. Robert Cook, operator, photographer)
A
Item:
1) “Antonio” the woodcutter concluded by Martin Noriega.
2) An old Cuban love song, heard in Key West, sung by Evelio Andux.
3) “Se Encuentra dos Carreteros” (The meeting of Two Travelers) a Cuban song sung by
Martin Noriega.
4) “Trenaban los Risenores” an old Cuban song sung by Martin Noriega.
5) Un Punto Cubano, sung by Martin Noriega.
REVERSE
6) An Italian rhymed prayer. by Augustine Vicari, Italian, age 24, born in Tampa; taught to
him by his father who was born in Italy.
7) A pornographic verse in Italian, by Augustine Vicari. He learned the verse from his
father, who lives in Ybor City, and “Who knows very many dirty songs and jokes.”

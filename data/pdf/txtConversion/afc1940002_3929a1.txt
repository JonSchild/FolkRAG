Textual Transcription
Ayudar, almas queridasa sentir a vuestro Padre,que es Jesús Nazareno,Hijo de la Virgen
Madre.El Jueves Santo en la tarde,cuando a mi Jesús prendieron,a María, su dulce
Madre,el corazón le partieron.-Adiós, Madre de mi vida,dice Jesús con amor,-ya me llevan
al supliciopor culpas del pecador.El Viernes Santo en la tardePilatos lo sentenció,en
presencia de la Virgen,que leer la sentencia vio.-Adiós, Jesús de mi vida,prenda de mi
corazón,en público te sentenciancomo un hombre malhechor.
English Translation
Let us help, dear soulsto grieve for your Father,who is Jesus the Nazarene,Son of the
Virgin Mother.On Holy Thursday in the afternoon,when they arrested my Jesus, they
broke the heart of Mary, his sweet Mother.-Goodbye, Mother of my life,says Jesus
with love,-they are taking me to torturefor the blame of the sinner.On Good Friday in
the afternoonPilate sentenced him,in the presence of the Virgin,who saw him read the
sentence.-Goodbye, Jesus of my life,treasure of my heart,in public they sentenced youlike
an evil doing man.

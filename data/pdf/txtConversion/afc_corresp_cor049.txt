Federal Works Agency—Work Projects Administration OF FLORIDA 49 WEST DUVAL
STREET JACKSONVILLE
April 26, 1940
Roy Schroder State Administrator
Dr. Harold Spivacke Chief, Division of Music Library of Congress Washington, D. C.
Dear Dr. Spivacke:
Thank you for granting an extension of time for use of the recording machine. Recordings
are now being made in Tampa and Tarpon Springs.
Enclosed is a description of photographic supplies needed to furnish you with pictures of
our work in the field. This is in accordance with your letter of April 19, 1940.
Enclosed also is copy of a letter from Professor Alton C. Morris regarding duplicates of the
Florida records and my reply. I trust this is a correct report of my conversation with you.
Sincerely yours, Carita Doggett Corse Dr. Carita Doggett Corse State Supervisor WPA
Writers' Project of Florida

August 7, 1940]
w Music
Mr. Mearns August 7, 1940
The express charges on the packages now in the Mail Division received from the Florida
Writers Project, should be charged to Increase, Music Division, Archive of American Folk
Song Allotment. These packages contain the equipment lent to the Florida Writers Project
for the purpose of collecting for the Library folkloristic material in the state of Florida.
Chief, Division of Music

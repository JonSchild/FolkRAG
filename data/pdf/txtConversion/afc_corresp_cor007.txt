Division of Music August 4, 1939
Attached is a telegram from the Director of the Federal Writers Project in Florida asking
that the Library of Congress support a recording trip by the theeminent authoress Zora
Neale Hurston. For this trip the Project requests the loan of a recording machine and
duplicates of the recordings deposited in the Archive of American Folk-Song. The total
cost to the Library will be approximately as follows:
200 discs $150 8 saphire needles 32 Miscellaneous 18 $200 - Total
I recommend that these be authorized and charged to the appropriation of Increase of the
Library.
Chief, Division of Music
Original memo approved. Sent to Accessions Div with memo ordering cutting needles. Aug
7/39 R

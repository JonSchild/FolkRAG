Textual Transcription
Delante el pie del altarhincarían los dos,diciendo los despaguesde testigo hacían a
Dios.Escritura les leyerona toditos en el altar,recibiendo aquella hostiaque reciben a
cantar.Ya salieron de la iglesiaen ese dichoso día,ya salieron esposadoscomo San José y
María.El padrino y la madrinaya saben su obligación,entregar a sus ahijadosy echarles la
bendición.
English Translation
At the foot of the altarthe two would kneel down,saying their vowsmaking God a
witness.Scripture they read toeveryone at the altar,receiving that host like receiving a
song.They left the church on that blessed day,and emerged married like Saint Joseph and
Mary.The godfather and godmotherknow their obligation, to deliver their godchildrenand
give them their blessing.

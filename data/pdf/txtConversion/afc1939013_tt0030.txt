RECORD 11.
(Made in Ybor City, Tampa, Florida, August 24, 1939. Stetson Kennedy, intermediary,
interviewer. Robert Cook, operator.)
(This is a continuation of the recording of the birthday party singing, joking, and
conversation begun with record 10.)
Item:
1) Martin Noriega sings another canto juajiro (Cuban farm song.) The singing is
interspersed with laughter, conversation, and cries of “Mas vino.” (More wine.) Esther
Andux is the feminine voice heard throughout; she is the wife of Evelio Andux.
About the center of the record, Evelio begins to sing.
REVERSE
Martin Noriega tells a story in Spanish.
Esther Andux tells a joke about “Quevedo” in Spanish.
The recording of the birthday party ends with Martin Noriega promising to tell another
“good long story tomorrow.”
2) (Recorded the following afternoon.)

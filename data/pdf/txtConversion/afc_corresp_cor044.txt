MEMORANDUM
November 22, 1939
TO: Alan Lomax
FROM: B.A.Botkin and Charles Seeger
SUBJECT:Florida RecordingTrip
The Joint Committee on Folk Arts, WPA, recommends that you send Mrs. Corse the
batteries and transformer for the recording machine as requested in her letter of November
1st, which is attached herewith.
A. L.

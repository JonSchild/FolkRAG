Music Superintendent of Buildings November 27, 1939
Please have box made for shipping batteries and transformer in electrician's office. (Some
sort of special box should be made for handling the little motor.) These batteries and
transformer are to be shipped to Dr. Carita Doggett Corse, State Supervisor, Statewide
Writers' Project, Federal Works Agency, Works Projects Administration of Florida, 49 West
Duval Street, Jacksonville, Florida.
Chief, Division of Music

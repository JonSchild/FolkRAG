Textual Transcription
Sigan con gran devociónen quien Dios ha de agradar,a María del Socorro,segunda
estrella del mar.En Barcelona nació,y bañada en clara luz,para su esposo Jesúsque
la merced le asentó,bendito Dios quien la dio,vamos todos a alabara María del
Socorro,segunda estrella del mar.Sobre las aguas llovidas,envidia causa la tierra,Santo
Espíritu se encierraen mujer tan elevada.¡Oh, qué bien aventuradala que imita sin amar
(cesar)a María del Socorro,segunda estrella del mar.
English Translation
Follow with great devotionin whom God will find elation,Mary of Succor,second star of
the sea. In Barcelona was she born,and bathed in clear light,for her husband Jesuswhich
mercy found for her,blessed God who granted it,let us all praise Mary of Succor, second
star of the sea. Above the raining waters,the world causes her envy,the Holy Spirit is found
in such an elevated woman.Oh, what good fortune she who imitates without ceasing,Mary
of Succor, second star of the sea.

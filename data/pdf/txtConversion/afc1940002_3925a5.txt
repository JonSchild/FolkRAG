Queda en paz (Be at Peace) [Audio]
Textual Transcription
Queda en paz, Niñito,duérmete, mi bien,adiós que ya Gilte vendrá a ofrecer,adiós que ya
Gilte vendrá a ofrecer.
English Translation
Be at peace, little Child,go to sleep, my beloved,goodbye because Gilwill come to make an
offering,goodbye because Gilwill come to make an offering.

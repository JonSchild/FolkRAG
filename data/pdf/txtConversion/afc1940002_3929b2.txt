Textual Transcription
Padre de todo lo creado,¡oh, Señor de Mapimí!en la cruz murió por mí,por librarme del
pecado.Todos, con buen corazón,debemos decir así,que el Señor de Mapimínos eche
su bendición.Por tu sagrada pasión,óyenos, Jesús amado,en la cruz murió por mí,por
librarme del pecadoCon aquel beso de pazcon que Judas lo entregó, luego al momento
se vioen la presencia de Anás.Lloremos lágrimas máspor Jesús sacramentadoen la cruz
murió por mípor librarme del pecado.Salió con Dimas y Gestaspor toda la calle amargay
del peso de la cargacayó con la cruz a cuestas.Y con burlas y respuestasa Jesús han
injuriado,en la cruz murió por mípor librarme del pecado.Con mano de hierro armada,su
rostro bello han herido,dejándolo sin el sentidocon tan recia bofetada.Vio su sangre
delicada,la que por mí ha derramado,en la cruz murió por mípor librarme del pecado.
English Translation
Father of all creation,oh, Lord of Mapimí!On the cross he died for me,to free me from
sin. All, with good heart, should say this way,that the Lord of Mapimíshould grant us his
blessing.Through your sacred passion,hear us, beloved Jesus,on the cross he died for
me,to free me from sin. With that kiss of peacewith which Judas betrayed him,a moment
later he saw himselfin the presence of Annas.Let us cry more tearsfor sacramented
Jesus on the cross he died for meto free me from sin. He went out with Dimas and
Gestasall along the bitter streetand from the weight of the loadfell with the cross on his
shoulders.And with taunts and mockery,they have injured Jesus,on the cross he died
for meto free me from sin. Armed with a hand of steel,they have injured his beautiful
face,leaving him unconscious,with such a mighty slaphis delicate blood was seen,that
which he shed for me, on the cross he died for me,to free me from sin.

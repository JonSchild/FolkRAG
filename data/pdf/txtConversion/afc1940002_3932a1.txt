Textual Transcription
Pues será nuestro consueloy mediadora con Dios,ruega, Señora, por nos,Virgen del
Monte Carmelo.Por la pena y agoníallorando estuvo en el huerto,y de su pésame
cubiertoque del rostro le vertía.Y un ángel que le traíael suave licor del cielo,ruega,
Señora, por nos,Virgen del Monte Carmelo.Con los ñudos, los cordelesque con sus
manos ataronal pilar donde le dieroncinco mil azotes crueles.Para que las almas
fielestengan divino consuelo,ruega, Señora, por nos,Virgen del Monte Carmelo.Con
la púrpura y la cañacon que le mostró Pilatos,al tropel del pueblo ingratopara mitigar
cizaña.Con la sangre que le dañadesde la cabeza al suelo,ruega, Señora, por nos,Virgen
del Monte Carmelo.
English Translation
Well, she will be our consolationand mediatrix with God,pray, Lady, for us,Virgin of
Mount Carmel.Through the pain and agonyhe was crying in the garden,covered in his
condolencewhich flowed from his face.And an angel who brought himthe soft liquor from
heaven,pray, Lady, for us,Virgin of Mount Carmel.With the knots of the ropeswith which
they tied his handsto the pillar where they gave himfive thousand cruel lashes.So that the
faithful soulsmay have divine consolation,pray, Lady, for us, Virgin of Mount Carmel.With
purple robe and cane scepterwith which Pilate showed himto the troop of ingrate people,to
mitigate the discord.With the blood that hurt himfrom his head to the ground,pray, Lady, for
us,Virgin of Mount Carmel.

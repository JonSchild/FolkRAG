Textual Transcription
Aquel hombre tan decenteque parece un clavel,esta estrella relucientey el nombre de
Juan B. Rael.El nombre de don Juan B. Raelsiempre lo traigo presente,y lo tengo en
mi memoriay aunque este les puse ausente.Señor don Juan B. Raelponga la mano en
la mesa,a usted le dicen el rey,y a su esposa la princesa.Yo no he sido cantador,ahora
me quiero enseñar,aquí está su servidorsoy suyo y puede mandar.A todo este público
honradolas gracias les voy a dar,si en algo me he equivocadome deben de dispensar.
English Translation
That man, so decent who looks like a carnation,this brilliant star with the name of Juan
B. Rael.The name of don Juan B. RaelI will always have in mind,and will have it in my
memoryeven though I may not remind you.Mister don Juan B. Raelput your hand on the
table,you they call the king, and your wife the princess.I have never been a singer,now I
want to learn, here is your servant I am yours and you may order me.To all this honored
public I would like to give thanks,if in anything I have erredyou should forgive me.

Jacksonville September 26, 1940
Dr. Harold Spivacke Chief, Division of Music Library of Congress Washington, D. C.
Dear Dr. Spivacke:
Thank you for your letter of September 23. We shall be glad to receive duplicates of the
Florida recordings whenever you have to them made. It has been a pleasure to work with
the Library of Congress music division in this program, and we hope that our recordings
will prove to be of some value.
Sincerely yours, Carita Doggett Corse Dr. Carita Doggett Corse State Supervisor WPA

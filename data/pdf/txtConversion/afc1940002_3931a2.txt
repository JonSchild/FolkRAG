Textual Transcription
Cantemos con dulce acentoy cantemos sin cesar,adorar el estandarte,que es la guía
fraternal.Hoy cantemos todos juntosy cantemos sin cesar,adorar el estandarte,que es
la guía fraternal.Pues este santo estandarte,porque es imagen del cielo,hoy lo vemos
retratadoa Jesús de Nazareno.Pues venir, venir, criaturas,pues venir, venir a verloal
estandarte dicho,que es Jesús de Nazareno.
English Translation
Let us sing with sweet accentand let us sing without ceasing,to adore the standard,which
is the fraternal guide.Today let us sing all togetherand let us sing without ceasing,to adore
the standard,which is the fraternal guide.Well this holy standard, because it is the image of
heaven,today we see it pictureJesus of Nazareth. Well come, come, creatures, well come,
come, and see itthe mentioned standard,which is Jesus of Nazareth.

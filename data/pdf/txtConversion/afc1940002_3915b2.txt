English Translation
Lee he has as his nameand Rael for more greatness,may they know him as a manand
also for his thoughtfulness.The name of Lee Raelthis fresh and beautiful rose,who is like
a carnation,alongside his beloved wife.This shining star will I now mention,Mister don
Leandro Rael,when will I ever forget? Mister don Leandro Rael,now that he is present
here,along with his beloved wife,that shining star.

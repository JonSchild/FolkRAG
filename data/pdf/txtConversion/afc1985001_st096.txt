Runnin' Stewball
RUNNIN' STEWBALL 4098 11B2
Vester Whitworth Arvin, 1940
Oh Stewball, Oh Stewball, he holds a high head His mane and his tail are as fine as silk
thread.
He's born in New England and he run in St. Paul And the name that I give aim is Runnin'
Stewball.

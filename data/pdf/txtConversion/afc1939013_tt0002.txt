A-2 “Old Maw-Maw” A Conch song learned in the Bahamas, sung by Wilbur Roberts,
Riviera, Florida, January 16, 1940. Recorded by Stetson Kennedy and Robert Cook.
OLD MAH-MAH
Old Mah-Mah, The old tom cat, 'e got so fat, 'E couldn't run at the mousey's mom.
The children lost their fine teeth comb, And their 'eads crawled away with the licey.

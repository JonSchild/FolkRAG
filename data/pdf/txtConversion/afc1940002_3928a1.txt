Textual Transcription
Por el rastro de la sangreque Jesucristo redamacaminó la Virgen puraen una fresca
mañana.De tan de mañana que eraa la hora que caminaba,las campanas de Belénsolas
tocaban al alba.Encuentra a Juan Bautistay de esta manera le habla:-¿No me has visto
por aquíal Hijo de mis entrañas?-Por aquí pasó, Señora,antes que el gallo cantara,cinco
mil azotes llevaen sus sagradas espaldas.Con una cruz en los hombrosde madera muy
pesada,como el madero era verde,cada paso arrodillaba.(ya me cansé)
English Translation
Along the trail of blood that Jesus Christ spilledwalked the Virgin pureon a cool morning.
So early was it that at the hour she walked,the bells of Bethlehemplayed alone to the
dawn.She encounters Saint John the Baptistand speaks with him in this manner:-Haven't
you seen come by herethe Son of my vitals? -He passed by here, Lady,before the rooster
sang, five thousand lashes he carried on his holy back. With a cross on his shouldersof
very heavy wood, since the beam was green,he knelt with each step.(I'm already tired)

Dawn) [Audio]
Textual Transcription
¡Dios te salve, bella aurora,Dios te salve, luz del día,Dios te salve, sol y estrella,y
Dios te salve, María!Eres llena de hermosura, llena de amor y eficacia,llena de mil
rendiciones,llena toda de gracia.Eres Reina poderosa,tú venciste al enemigo,eres
todo nuestro amparo,pues el Señor es contigo.Bendita la hizo Dios Padre,bendita del
Hijo eres,bendita del Santo Espíritu,bendita entre las mujeres.Bendito el Dios que te
creó,bendito el día en que naciste,bendito el nombre que tienes,bendito el fruto que diste.
English Translation
God save thee, beautiful dawn,God save thee, light of day,God save thee, sun and star,
and God save thee, Mary!You are full of beauty,full of love and mastery,full of a thousand
surrenders,all full of grace. You are a powerful Queen,you defeated the enemy,you are all
of our help, well the Lord is with you.Blessed made her God the Father,blessed of the Son
are you,blessed of the Holy Spirit,blessed among women.Blessed the God that created
you,blessed the day you were born,blessed the name you have, blessed the fruit you
gave.

June 11, 1943 Dr. Willis James The Fort Valley State College Fort Valley, Georgia
Dear Dr. James:
Enclosed are two needles. Please return the others immediately for resharpening. I trust
you will have no trouble with them. Let me know what your recording plans are.
With best wishes,
Sincerely yours, John Langenegger Chief Engineer Recording Laboratory
L/k ENC.

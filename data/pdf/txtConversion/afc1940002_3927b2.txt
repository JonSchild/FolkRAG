Textual Transcription
El santo ángel de la guardanos eche su bendicióny nos libre del demonioy de
mala tentación.He de poner ésta, mi alma,en las manos del Señor,que murió
afrentosamentepor salvar al pecador.En el huerto estaba Cristopostrado su rostro al
suelo,sudando gotas de sangrerodeado de desconsuelo.Sale la Virgen, su madre,dando
clamores al cielo,por ver si baja San Juany le trae algún consuelo.Bajaron las tres
Maríaspor ser enviadas del cielo,a traer mortaja y sepulcropara sepultar su cuerpo.Lo
llevaron al Calvariocon cinco y dos mil tormentos,allí cae, allí levanta,porque le faltó el
aliento.Lo clavaron en la cruz,sus miembros atormentados,con duros clavos de hierrosus
pies y manos clavaron.
English Translation
May the holy guardian angel give us his blessingand free us from the deviland from evil
temptation.I will put this, my soul, in the hands of the Lord,who died affrontedto save
the sinner. In the garden was Christ with his face towards the ground,sweating drops
of bloodsurrounded with disconsolation.The Virgin, his mother, comes outraising cries
to heaven,to see if Saint John comes downto bring her some consolation.The three
Marys went downbeing sent by heaven, to bring shroud and sepulchreo put his body to
rest. They took him to Calvarywith five and two thousand torments,there he falls, there
he rises,because he breath was failing.They nailed him to the cross,his members all
tormented,with hard nails of iron his feet and hands they nailed.

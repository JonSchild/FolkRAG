FADO HILARIO Our Lady knits stockingsWith thread made of lightThe clew is the full moonThe
stockings are for JesusHail Maries - are kisses Our Fathers - are embracesThe Rosary- - my
desiresThe cross---- your open arms My old cloakHas the color of the dark nightIn it, I want to
enshroud myselfWhen I go to the graveI want my coffinTo have a bizarre formThe form of a
heartThe form of a guitar.

April 14, 1943, letter from H.M. Bond to B.A. Botkin
The Fort Valley State College Fort Valley, Georgia
OFFICE OF PRESIDENT
April 14, 1943
Mr B A Botkin Music Division Library of Congress Washington, D C
Dear Mr Botkin:
Thank you for your letter of April 1st. I am sending to you at once the recordings already
made. I am sure that Willis James would be highly competent to use the balance of the
records in a manner that would meet your entire approval.
With best wishes, I am
Sincerely yours, H M Bond H M Bond
